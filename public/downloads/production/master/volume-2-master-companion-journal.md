# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## January Week 1: The Invitation to Follow

Use these pages with Days 001-007.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, call me close enough to follow You with my actual life. Save me from self-rule, approval-seeking, hidden need, false expectations, relational heaviness, pride, and comparison. Teach me to walk Your road with peace, mercy, humility, and surrendered trust. Amen.

### Day 001 Reflection

Where is Jesus asking me to move from admiration into actual surrender?

Practice: Name one place where self-rule has been louder than Jesus, then take one concrete step of surrender before noon.

### Day 002 Reflection

Where has approval been shaping my road more than the way of Jesus?

Practice: Choose one conversation or decision where you will practice truthful peace instead of approval-seeking.

### Day 003 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need to cry for mercy instead of hiding my need?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one place where you need mercy, and bring it honestly to Jesus before you attempt to manage it.

### Day 004 Reflection

Where am I asking Jesus to bless my way instead of letting Him lead me in His?

Practice: Ask Jesus to expose one expectation that needs surrender, then choose His way of peace in one practical action.

### Day 005 Reflection

Where can I carry the peace of Christ into a relationship today?

Practice: Offer one concrete gesture of peace today: a kind greeting, a repair, a blessing, or a calmer response.

### Day 006 Reflection

Where has defensiveness been blocking the healing Jesus wants to bring?

Practice: Identify one place where Jesus is making you teachable, then respond with humility instead of defensiveness.

### Day 007 Reflection

Where do I need to receive becoming like Jesus as enough?

Practice: Choose one specific Christlike response today and practice it where comparison or self-protection would normally lead.

### Week Review

- Where did I hear Jesus calling me from admiration into surrender?
- Which approval pattern began to lose power?
- What did Sabbath mercy help me bring honestly to Christ?
- What expectation did Jesus correct with peace?
- Where did I carry peace into relationship?
- Where did teachability feel like healing?
- What Christlike response do I want to keep practicing next week?
