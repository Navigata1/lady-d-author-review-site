# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## September Week 2: Power to Witness

Use these pages with Days 251-257.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form power to witness through settled obedience, courage in weakness, presence over self-reliance, purified assurance, Sabbath preparation as worship, ordered faithfulness, and reverent awe. Keep every assignment submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 251 Reflection

Where is God moving me from question into obedience?

Practice: Identify one assignment God has already made clear, then take one concrete step toward it.

### Day 252 Reflection

Where has inadequacy become my whole explanation?

Practice: Tell God one place you feel inadequate, then ask for dependence instead of self-inflation.

### Day 253 Reflection

Where do I need presence more than self-confidence?

Practice: Before one responsibility, pause and confess, "God, I need Your presence more than my confidence."

### Day 254 Reflection

Where do I need clarity, and what part is already clear?

Practice: Ask God for clarity about one next step, then do the part that is already clear.

### Day 255 Reflection

What preparation could become worship today?

Practice: On this Sabbath, prepare one simple offering of time, service, attention, or generosity and give it to God with prayer.

### Day 256 Reflection

What clear instruction needs calm attention today?

Practice: Follow one clear instruction or commitment with calm attention.

### Day 257 Reflection

Where do I need reverence to guard my witness?

Practice: Pause before one sacred responsibility and ask God to restore reverence.

### Weekly Review

- Which question became obedience?
- Where did weakness become dependence?
- How did God's presence challenge self-reliance?
- What assurance did God clarify?
- What preparation became Sabbath worship?
- What instruction needed calm attention?
- How did reverence protect witness from pride?
