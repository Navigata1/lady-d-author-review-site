# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## October Week 2: The Cross and Daily Grace

Use these pages beside Days 277-283.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, merciful Teacher and risen Lord, keep me close enough to bring You my questions, tender enough to serve with clarity, grounded enough to hold fast to Your word, awake enough to meet You in ordinary life, discerning enough to resist deception, peaceful enough to stay ready, and rested enough on Sabbath to keep the Master's heart. Amen.

### Day 277 Reflection

- What question do I need to bring close to Jesus instead of carrying alone?
- Practice: Bring one honest question to Jesus in prayer before you seek a dozen other answers.

### Day 278 Reflection

- Where do I need Christ to hold tenderness and clarity together in me?
- Practice: Practice one act of compassion today with both tenderness and clear obedience.

### Day 279 Reflection

- Which word of Jesus needs to become more permanent in my life?
- Practice: Choose one teaching of Jesus and let it shape one concrete decision today.

### Day 280 Reflection

- Where have I been going through the motions instead of walking awake with Jesus?
- Practice: Choose one routine moment and practice it as conscious fellowship with Jesus.

### Day 281 Reflection

- What voice has been asking for trust that belongs only to Jesus?
- Practice: Test one message, pressure, or invitation by whether it sounds like the character of Jesus.

### Day 282 Reflection

- Where is Jesus calling me to readiness rather than anxiety?
- Practice: Prepare one neglected responsibility calmly as an act of faithful watchfulness.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 283

- Where has waiting made my compassion thinner, and how can Sabbath grace restore it?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, let worship renew your compassion toward one person you have grown tired of serving.

### Week Review

- What question became safer because I brought it close to Jesus?
- Where did tenderness and clarity serve together?
- Which word of Jesus shaped a real decision?
- What ordinary routine became more awake?
- How did Sabbath rest restore the Master's heart in me?
