# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## April Week 3: Conviction Without Condemnation

Use these pages with Days 105-111.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, bring grace into specific places, clarify my direction, refresh responsibility, root obedience in God's generous care, teach me protective truth, free me from isolated strength, and seal belonging through humble participation. Amen.

### Day 105 Reflection

What specific place needs the Comforter's nearness today?

Practice: Name one specific place where you need correction, and invite the Spirit to meet you there with grace.

### Day 106 Reflection

What direction is God making clear that I need to follow?

Practice: Ask God for one clear direction, then take a simple act that agrees with it.

### Day 107 Reflection

What responsibility needs to be received as stewardship instead of condemnation?

Practice: Choose one thing God has placed near you to tend, and give it one faithful act of care.

### Day 108 Reflection

Where have I heard only God's no and forgotten His generous care?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one generous provision from God, then let that provision steady one act of obedience.

### Day 109 Reflection

What warning from God have I mistaken for rejection?

Practice: Identify one boundary God is using to guard life, and honor it in one concrete way.

### Day 110 Reflection

Where has isolation disguised itself as strength in my life?

Practice: Ask one trustworthy person for prayer, counsel, or practical help in a place you have carried alone.

### Day 111 Reflection

What responsibility has God brought near because my presence matters?

Practice: Name one responsibility God has brought near, and respond to it as a beloved servant rather than a condemned worker.

### Weekly Review

- Where did grace become specific instead of vague?
- What direction became clearer as I prayed?
- What responsibility felt less condemning when I received it as stewardship?
- How did Sabbath rest remind me of God's generous care?
- What boundary protected life this week?
- Where did I receive help instead of performing isolated strength?
