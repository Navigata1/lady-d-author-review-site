# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## June Closeout: Restored by the Father's Heart

Use these pages beside Days 179-181.

### Closeout Prayer

Father, let mercy tell the truth before fear spends someone else's safety. Give me patient eyes for vulnerability, teach me to grieve what love must grieve, and close this month with restoration that is honest, courageous, and tender. Amen.

---

### Day 179 Reflection

**Focus:** Truthful speech before fear creates harm.

**Write:** Where is fear tempting me to protect myself in a way that places pressure or risk on someone else?

**Practice:** Name one place where fear may be shaping your words, then choose one truthful and protective response.

### Day 180 Reflection

**Focus:** Patient sight that protects vulnerability.

**Write:** Where do I need the Father's patient sight so I can notice vulnerability instead of rushing past it?

**Practice:** Pay attention to one person who may be overlooked, exposed, or treated as useful instead of beloved, and respond with honor.

### Day 181 Reflection

**Focus:** Love strong enough to grieve and tell the truth.

**Write:** What painful truth do I need to hold honestly before the Father instead of rushing past it?

**Practice:** Hold one painful truth before the Father without rushing to explain it away, then ask for one faithful response rooted in love rather than fear.

---

### June Review

1. Where did the Father's heart restore my identity, responsibility, or courage this month?
2. What kind of fear did I learn to name more honestly?
3. Where did Sabbath rest protect obedience from performance?
4. How did compassion become wiser and more protective?
5. What truth do I need to carry into July with courage, tenderness, and clarity?
