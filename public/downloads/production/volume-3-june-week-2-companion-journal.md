# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## June Week 2: Gifts for the Body

Use these pages with Days 159-165.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, strengthen patient imagination, renew detailed service, free my gifts from possessiveness, breathe trust where craving wants control, anchor memory in truth, restore gratitude in dry places, and renew my sight to recognize provision. Amen.

### Day 159 Reflection

Where does my gift need patient imagination instead of panic?

Practice: Give one unfinished work patient attention instead of anxious rushing.

### Day 160 Reflection

Where has detailed service begun to dry out my joy?

Practice: Take one detailed task and offer it to God slowly, without resentment.

### Day 161 Reflection

Where am I guarding knowledge, skill, or opportunity too tightly?

Practice: Teach, explain, invite, encourage, or make room for someone else to grow in one concrete way.

### Day 162 Reflection

Where is craving speaking louder than calling?

Practice: Name one craving that is trying to steer your attitude, and surrender it before it steers your gift.

### Day 163 Reflection

Where am I romanticizing an old place or pattern God has called me out of?

Practice: Write one honest sentence about what God delivered you from, and one sentence about what He is forming now.

### Day 164 Reflection

Where has dissatisfaction made me contemptuous toward God's provision?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one provision you have been treating as nothing, and thank God for it honestly.

### Day 165 Reflection

What have I stopped noticing because it has become familiar?

Practice: Notice one familiar mercy and let it become prayer before you move on.

### Weekly Review

- What unfinished work received patient attention?
- Where did detailed service become less resentful?
- Who did I help grow?
- What craving lost authority over my gift?
- What memory became more honest?
- How did Sabbath gratitude challenge contempt?
- What familiar mercy did I see again?
