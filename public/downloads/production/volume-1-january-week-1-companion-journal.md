# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## January Week 1: The Depth of God's Love

Use these pages beside Days 001-007.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, let this week begin in Your love. Quiet the voice of striving, fear, and shame. Teach me to receive before I perform, surrender before I control, and obey because I am loved. Amen.

### Day 001 Reflection

**Focus:** Receiving the Father's welcome.

**Write:** Where have I been trying to prove that I deserve God's nearness?

**Practice:** Begin the day with one minute of stillness before any screen.

### Day 002 Reflection

**Focus:** Beloved identity.

**Write:** What false identity has been shaping my reactions lately?

**Practice:** Write one sentence that begins, "Because I belong to the Father..."

### Day 003 Reflection

**Focus:** Faithfulness in ordinary obedience.

**Write:** What ordinary task can become worship today?

**Practice:** Complete one ordinary action slowly and prayerfully.

### Day 004 Reflection

**Focus:** Grace before performance.

**Write:** Which performance habit am I ready to surrender?

**Practice:** Tell the truth in one place where you usually hide behind polish.

### Day 005 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy that becomes visible.

**Write:** Who is God asking me to notice with practical compassion?

**Practice:** Do one concrete act of mercy before sunset.

### Day 006 Reflection

**Focus:** Clinging instead of controlling.

**Write:** Where am I impatient with God's timing?

**Practice:** When impatience rises, pause and pray before responding.

### Day 007 Reflection

**Focus:** Inward renewal.

**Write:** What guarded place in my heart needs the Father's healing love?

**Practice:** Invite God into that place without dressing it up.

### Week 1 Review

1. What did the Father reveal about His love this week?
2. What did I surrender?
3. Where did I resist?
4. What act of obedience grew from love rather than fear?
5. Where do I need to carry this into the coming Sabbath?
