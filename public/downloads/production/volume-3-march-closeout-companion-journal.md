# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## March Closeout: The Comforter Near You

Use these pages with Days 088-090.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, close this month by refining belonging, bringing wordless prayer home, and giving breath while God forms identity and purpose. Keep my heart near to Jesus and ready for the conviction without condemnation You will teach next. Amen.

### Day 088 Reflection

Where has fear made me question my place in God's care?

Practice: Name one fear that has weakened your sense of belonging, and answer it with one truth about God's care.

### Day 089 Reflection

What part of my life have I judged harshly that needs to be brought home to God?

Practice: Bring one self-critical thought to God and ask Him to show you what He is forming there.

### Day 090 Reflection

Where am I trying to hurry what God is still forming?

Practice: Name one area where you are waiting for clarity, and choose one faithful act that does not force the outcome.

### March Review

- Where did the Comforter feel nearer than I expected?
- What truth helped me breathe in a dry place?
- What light did God place near me?
- What rhythm helped my witness become less driven?
- What identity or calling do I need to let God keep forming?
- What theme from March should travel with me into April?
