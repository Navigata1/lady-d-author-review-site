# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## November Closeout: Freedom, Fire, and Holiness

Use these pages with Days 328-334.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form freedom, fire, and holiness in named calling, sealed belonging, gifts under mercy, boundaries in witness, Sabbath compassion, prayed-through speech, and generous service. Keep ordinary life anointed, comparison quiet, motives surrendered, direction received, service tender, speech humble, and hands open. Amen.

### Day 328 Reflection

Where do I feel too ordinary to be used by Jesus?

Practice: Speak your name before God and surrender the ordinary parts of your life to Jesus' use.

### Day 329 Reflection

Where has comparison made my calling feel smaller?

Practice: Bless one person's different gifting without measuring yourself against it.

### Day 330 Reflection

Where has gift become image management in me?

Practice: Ask God to examine one gift or role for hidden self-display.

### Day 331 Reflection

Where do I want calling without boundaries?

Practice: Honor one clear boundary instead of treating urgency as authority.

### Day 332 Reflection

Who near me needs compassion without performance?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pray for one nearby person who needs compassion, not performance.

### Day 333 Reflection

Where does my speech need to be entrusted back to Jesus?

Practice: Pray before you speak about something holy or important.

### Day 334 Reflection

Where has service become a place of grasping?

Practice: Give one act of service without grasping for recognition.

### Weekly Review

- How did ordinary life feel more available to Jesus?
- Where did comparison quiet down?
- What gift was brought under mercy?
- Which boundary became obedience?
- How did Sabbath rest seal compassion?
- What speech was prayed through first?
- Where did service become more open-handed?
