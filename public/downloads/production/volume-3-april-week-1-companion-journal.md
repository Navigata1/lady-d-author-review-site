# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## April Week 1: Conviction Without Condemnation

Use these pages with Days 091-097.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach me the difference between conviction and condemnation. Correct me without letting shame define me. Settle me in the Father's care, keep me near to Jesus, and form obedience that grows from love instead of fear. Amen.

### Day 091 Reflection

Where do I need to receive conviction as a call back to identity rather than as condemnation?

Practice: Ask the Spirit to show one place where shame has replaced identity, then answer it with the truth that you were made for God.

### Day 092 Reflection

What provision from God can help me breathe before I respond today?

Practice: Before reacting to a pressure point, name one provision God has already placed near your life.

### Day 093 Reflection

What condemning thought needs to be answered with God's true purpose for my life?

Practice: When a condemning thought rises, pause and ask what faithful choice the Spirit is actually inviting.

### Day 094 Reflection

Where has anxious striving made correction feel like rejection?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause one form of anxious self-improvement and receive God's care before taking the next obedient step.

### Day 095 Reflection

Where has exhaustion begun to sound like faithfulness in my life?

Practice: Identify one place where fear has made you frantic, then choose one slower act of obedient trust.

### Day 096 Reflection

What ordinary part of my life needs to be set apart by God's love?

Practice: Choose one ordinary part of today and intentionally set it apart for God before you move through it.

### Day 097 Reflection

What small fruit is God beginning to form that I need to notice and protect?

Practice: Name one small evidence of growth and protect it with one concrete act of obedience.

### Weekly Review

- Where did conviction feel like mercy instead of accusation?
- What condemning thought lost power when I brought it to God?
- Where did God's provision help me breathe before I responded?
- How did Sabbath rest correct anxious striving?
- What ordinary place did I set apart for God?
- What fruit is beginning to grow quietly?
