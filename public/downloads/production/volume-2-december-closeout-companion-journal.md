# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## December Close: Walking as His Witness

Use these pages beside Days 363-365.

### Closeout Prayer

Jesus, Prince of Peace and Light of grace, come near to the source of my words, fill my inner storehouse with good treasure, cleanse careless speech, and form my witness for the year ahead. Amen.

### Day 363 Reflection

- What has been filling my heart and shaping my words?
- Practice: Before one important conversation, pause and ask Jesus what has been filling your heart.

### Day 364 Reflection

- What am I storing that shapes what comes out of me?
- Practice: Store one good treasure today: a truth, prayer, act of mercy, or surrendered obedience you can draw from later.

### Day 365 Reflection

- What word-pattern from this year needs confession, cleansing, or new formation?
- Practice: Review one pattern of speech from this year, confess what needs cleansing, and ask Jesus to form your words for the year ahead.

### Volume 2 Year Review

- How did Jesus teach me to walk with Him this year?
- Where did following Him become more visible in ordinary life?
- What place of fear became a place of witness?
- What word, practice, or relationship needs to be carried into the next year with more grace?
- What do I want to thank Jesus for before closing this devotional year?
