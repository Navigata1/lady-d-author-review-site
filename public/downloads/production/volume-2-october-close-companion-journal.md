# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## October Close: The Cross and Daily Grace

Use these pages beside Days 298-304.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, compassionate Lord and returning Teacher, make my mercy hidden and brave, form eyes that see overlooked need, put shoes on compassion, wake my hope to meet You, keep me available to Your voice in limitation, remind me that You stand among Your people, and let Sabbath hope refine my witness until You are seen. Amen.

### Day 298 Reflection

- Where can I serve without needing the act to be noticed?
- Practice: Offer one hidden mercy today and let Jesus receive the credit.

### Day 299 Reflection

- Who have I been passing without really seeing?
- Practice: Notice one overlooked person or need and respond with dignity.

### Day 300 Reflection

- Where has avoidance been disguising itself as busyness or caution?
- Practice: Move toward one person you have been tempted to avoid with one concrete act of honor.

### Day 301 Reflection

- Where has my faith grown sleepy, and what would readiness look like today?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to wake one dull place in your faith, then respond with one act of readiness.

### Day 302 Reflection

- Where do I need to remain available to Jesus instead of waiting for better conditions?
- Practice: Set apart one quiet moment in a limited or inconvenient place and listen for Christ in prayer.

### Day 303 Reflection

- How would my service change if I remembered Jesus stands in the midst?
- Practice: Before serving, picture your work as done in the presence of Christ among His people.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 304

- What needs to be refined in me as I remember that Jesus will be seen?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, surrender one hurry or prideful urgency in light of Christ's return.

### Week Review

- Where did mercy become hidden and brave?
- What overlooked need did Jesus help me see?
- Where did compassion move toward someone I had avoided?
- What dull place in faith began to wake?
- How did Sabbath hope refine my witness?
