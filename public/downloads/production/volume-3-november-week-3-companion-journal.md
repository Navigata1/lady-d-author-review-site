# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## November Week 3: Freedom, Fire, and Holiness

Use these pages with Days 321-327.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form freedom, fire, and holiness in hidden responsibility, honest obedience, unavoidable visibility, public approval, ordered responsibility, faithful companionship, and restrained response. Give me courage for calling, honesty in hiding, steadiness under attention, clean identity under praise, Sabbath order before activity, gratitude for companions, and holy fire under criticism. Amen.

### Day 321 Reflection

Where do I feel named by responsibility but still hidden inside?

Practice: Bring one hidden responsibility to God before you try to explain it to anyone else.

### Day 322 Reflection

Where am I hiding near the very thing God is asking me to face?

Practice: Name one place you are hiding near your assignment, and ask God for the next honest step.

### Day 323 Reflection

Where does visibility make me feel either smaller or larger than I am?

Practice: When attention comes, breathe before shrinking or performing.

### Day 324 Reflection

Where has praise begun to direct my inner life?

Practice: Receive encouragement with gratitude, then return your identity to God.

### Day 325 Reflection

What responsibility needs clearer order before I move forward?

Practice: On this Sabbath, write one responsibility in simple, ordered terms before God.

### Day 326 Reflection

Who has God placed near me for faithful strength?

Practice: Thank God for one faithful companion, and release them from carrying what only God can carry.

### Day 327 Reflection

Where does criticism make me want to prove myself immediately?

Practice: When criticism provokes you, pause before defending yourself.

### Weekly Review

- What hidden responsibility was brought to God?
- Where did honest obedience begin to replace hiding?
- How did visibility become steadier?
- What praise was received without becoming identity?
- How did Sabbath rest bring order to responsibility?
- Which companion was received with gratitude?
- What criticism met holy restraint?
