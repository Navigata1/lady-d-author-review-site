# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## July Week 2: The Way, the Truth, and the Life

Use these pages beside Days 186-192.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to begin from beloved belonging, handle practical responsibility with peace, listen to the Father's witness, name fear honestly, rise under Your tender Lordship, center my eyes on You, and keep this Sabbath with reverence for the vulnerable and overlooked. Amen.

### Day 186 Reflection

- Where am I treating responsibility as proof of worth instead of response to belonging?
- Practice: Before answering a demand, name one truth about who you are in Christ.

### Day 187 Reflection

- What practical responsibility needs humble obedience instead of defensiveness?
- Practice: Choose one responsibility and ask Jesus how to handle it without pride.

### Day 188 Reflection

- What competing voice needs to become quiet so I can listen to Jesus?
- Practice: Silence one competing voice for a few minutes and turn attention back to Christ.

### Day 189 Reflection

- What fear needs to be named before Jesus instead of hidden behind strength?
- Practice: Tell Jesus plainly what has made you afraid, then ask for one peaceful next step.

### Day 190 Reflection

- Where is Jesus calling me to rise from fear into obedient trust?
- Practice: Take one obedient step under Christ's tender authority.

### Day 191 Reflection

- What has been competing with Jesus for the center of my attention?
- Practice: Before the next decision, ask what keeps Jesus central.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 192

- Who might I be overlooking that Jesus calls precious before the Father?
- Practice: Honor one overlooked person through prayer, attention, or a quiet act of care.

### Week Review

- Where did I begin from belonging instead of proving?
- What practical responsibility became more peaceful?
- How did Jesus meet fear with nearness?
- How did Sabbath rest slow my eyes toward the vulnerable?
