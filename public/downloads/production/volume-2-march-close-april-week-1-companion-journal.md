# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## March Close and April Opening: Watchful Service in His Healing Presence

Use these pages beside Days 088-094.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, keep me awake without fear. Heal my hurry, steady my service, and make my ordinary responsibilities places of faithful love. Let Sabbath rest form watchful readiness in peace. Amen.

### Day 088 Reflection

- Where has distraction dulled my attention to Jesus?
- Practice: Give one distracted space back to Him today.

### Day 089 Reflection

- What act of readiness have I been delaying?
- Practice: Complete one quiet act of obedience without making it dramatic.

### Day 090 Reflection

- What responsibility has God placed in my care right now?
- Practice: Serve that responsibility today without resentment.

### Day 091 Reflection

- Where do my belief and daily doing need to come back together?
- Practice: Serve one hidden task as an act of love for Jesus.

### Day 092 Reflection

- What voice has been shaping my fear, pride, or hope?
- Practice: Test that voice by the character of Jesus.

### Day 093 Reflection

- How has waiting revealed my motives?
- Practice: Choose one faithful response where impatience has been growing.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 094

- What does watchful love look like on this Sabbath?
- Practice: Pause one planned activity and give that space to worship, listening, and readiness.

### Week Review

- Where did watchfulness become practical?
- Where did Jesus heal fear-driven readiness?
- What hidden service became holy this week?
- What practice of Sabbath-shaped attention should continue?
