# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## March Week 4: The Comforter Near You

Use these pages with Days 081-087.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, steady me under God's ordered light, help me stand in placed provision, form holy discernment, renew dry places, stir obedient movement, grow real fruit, and return my witness to Sabbath rhythm. Amen.

### Day 081 Reflection

What large concern do I need to place under God's ordered care?

Practice: Step outside or look toward the sky, then turn one large worry into a prayer of trust.

### Day 082 Reflection

What provision has God placed near me that I have overlooked?

Practice: Name one placed provision God has given today, and use it for one faithful step.

### Day 083 Reflection

Where do I need grace to help me distinguish light from darkness?

Practice: Identify one blurred area, name what belongs to God's light, and take one action toward it.

### Day 084 Reflection

What repeated mercy have I been overlooking?

Practice: Name one repeated mercy God has given you, and receive it instead of rushing past it.

### Day 085 Reflection

What living step is God calling forward today?

Practice: Take one living step you have delayed, keeping it simple, prayerful, and submitted to God.

### Day 086 Reflection

Where am I trying to force fruit instead of receiving life with God?

Practice: Choose one fruit you want the Spirit to grow, and practice one small expression of it today.

### Day 087 Sabbath Reflection

Where does my witness need God's rhythm more than my urgency?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause one form of striving and receive strength from God before speaking or serving.

### Week Review

- What worry became smaller under God's ordered care?
- What placed provision helped me stand?
- What blurred area became clearer?
- What repeated mercy refreshed a dry place?
- What living step moved from delay into obedience?
- What fruit felt less forced and more received?
- How did Sabbath rhythm strengthen witness?
