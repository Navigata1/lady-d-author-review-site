# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## January Week 2: The Invitation to Follow with Courage and Trust

Use these pages with Days 008-014.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to follow You with courage, humility, rest, and restraint. Lead me beyond public pressure, private confusion, control, discouragement, minimized weakness, borrowed answers, and hurried speech. Form my heart to trust You on the road. Amen.

### Day 008 Reflection

Where do I need cross-shaped courage instead of reactive strength?

Practice: Ask Jesus whether one situation needs brave speech, quiet withdrawal, or continued service, then obey without needing to perform strength.

### Day 009 Reflection

What question do I need to bring closer to Jesus instead of hiding it?

Practice: Bring one question to Jesus in prayer today and wait for light before forcing an answer.

### Day 010 Sabbath Reflection

Where is Jesus asking me to trust His leadership without seeing the whole crossing?

Practice: On this Sabbath, release one place where you have been trying to control the whole crossing, and trust Jesus with the next step He has given.

### Day 011 Reflection

Where do I need humble persistence instead of silent discouragement?

Practice: Bring one persistent need to Jesus again today, with humility and confidence in His mercy.

### Day 012 Reflection

What ordinary need is Jesus inviting me to stop minimizing?

Practice: Name one ordinary need you have been minimizing, and respond to it with wise compassion instead of shame.

### Day 013 Reflection

Where do I need to answer Jesus personally instead of leaning on borrowed words?

Practice: Write one sentence of personal confession about who Jesus is to you, then let it shape one decision today.

### Day 014 Reflection

Where do I need Jesus to refine not only what I say, but when and why I say it?

Practice: Ask Jesus where obedience requires restraint today, then practice faithful silence or careful speech.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus teach me courage without performance?
- What question did I bring closer to Him?
- What did Sabbath trust help me release?
- Where did persistent faith keep asking for mercy?
- What ordinary need did Jesus meet with compassion?
- How did personal confession shape a decision?
- Where did restraint become obedience?
