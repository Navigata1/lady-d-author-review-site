# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## November Week 4: Eternal Hope from Seen Suffering to I AM

Use these pages with Days 322-328.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me eternal hope that begins with Your seeing, moves through Your delivering heart, rests in being known, obeys from Sabbath-rooted trust, brings inadequacy into Your welcome, comes home to beloved identity, and finds peace in who You are. Hold this week in Your faithful presence. Amen.

### Day 322 Reflection

Where do I need to believe that God has truly seen what I have been carrying?

Practice: Name one burden honestly before God without editing it into something smaller.

### Day 323 Reflection

Where have I expected survival when God may be inviting restoration?

Practice: Identify one place where you have settled for survival, and ask God to teach you what restoration could look like there.

### Day 324 Reflection

What changes when I remember that God knows the pressure before He asks for obedience?

Practice: Before making your next decision, pause and say plainly what God already knows about the pressure you are under.

### Day 325 Sabbath Reflection

What true assignment needs Sabbath-rooted obedience, and what false burden do I need to release?

Practice: On this Sabbath, ask God to separate one true assignment from one false burden.

### Day 326 Reflection

Where have I allowed inadequacy to become the final authority?

Practice: Write one honest sentence about where you feel inadequate, then answer it with one truth about God's presence.

### Day 327 Reflection

What task feels too large when I measure myself, and how does God's nearness change the way I face it?

Practice: Before measuring yourself against the task, pause and receive the promise that God is with you.

### Day 328 Reflection

Where do I need God's unchanging presence to steady what feels unstable?

Practice: Name one unstable circumstance, then pray from the truth that God's presence remains steady.

### Week Review

- Where did I let God name my burden honestly?
- What restoration did I begin to imagine beyond survival?
- How did being known change one decision?
- What did Sabbath rest separate from false pressure?
- Where did God meet my inadequacy with courage?
- How did His nearness reshape my beloved identity?
- What unstable place needs peace grounded in who God is?
