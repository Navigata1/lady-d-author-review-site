# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## May Week 2: Learning His Voice

Use these pages beside Days 123-129.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to hear Your voice in wonder, division, pressure, hope, empty places, and belonging. Let my questions become surrendered attention, my divided places become whole, and my Sabbath rest become family-hearted obedience. Amen.

### Day 123 Reflection

- What holy question may Jesus be using to draw me nearer?
- Practice: Stay with Jesus in prayer when a question rises instead of brushing it aside.

### Day 124 Reflection

- Where am I divided, and what whole-hearted step is Jesus inviting?
- Practice: Name one divided place and ask for one whole-hearted next step.

### Day 125 Reflection

- Where do I need to recognize God's kingdom breaking into an ordinary moment?
- Practice: Before reacting, ask, "What is Jesus making near here?"

### Day 126 Reflection

- Where have I been asking for certainty instead of taking the next faithful step?
- Practice: Take one clear act of obedience without demanding another sign.

### Day 127 Reflection

- What decision would change if I listened from resurrection hope?
- Practice: Pray over one delayed or buried place from hope instead of resignation.

### Day 128 Reflection

- What space has grace cleared that now needs to be filled with Christ?
- Practice: Fill one cleared space with prayer, Scripture meditation, or obedience.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 129

- Where do I need to receive Christ's compassion as belonging this Sabbath?
- Practice: Receive your belonging in Christ and practice one act of family-hearted obedience.

### Week Review

- Where did wonder become prayer this week?
- What divided place did Jesus begin to make whole?
- Where did resurrection hope change my next decision?
- How did Sabbath belonging strengthen obedience rather than pressure it?
