# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## April Week 2: Conviction Without Condemnation

Use these pages with Days 098-104.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, give me honest witness, quiet belonging, prayer beyond words, peaceful desire, oil for the journey, guided freedom, and a renewed heart around good gifts. Let Your conviction keep leading me to Jesus without shame. Amen.

### Day 098 Reflection

Where do I need honest dependence instead of performance?

Practice: Name one place that is not yet fruitful, and ask God for one faithful act instead of a performance.

### Day 099 Reflection

What quiet mercy has God used to keep my heart from hardening?

Practice: Notice one hidden mercy God has used to keep your heart soft, and thank Him for it before asking for more.

### Day 100 Reflection

What need do I keep trying to explain that I simply need to bring to God?

Practice: Bring one unnamed need to God without trying to explain it perfectly, then sit quietly for a few minutes.

### Day 101 Reflection

What desire needs God's peace and order instead of my anxious grasping?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause one restless desire and ask God how to receive or release it under His wisdom.

### Day 102 Reflection

Where am I trying to move outward without first receiving from God?

Practice: Before one responsibility, pause and ask God for received grace instead of running on pressure.

### Day 103 Reflection

Where does my freedom need submitted direction?

Practice: Name one area where you have freedom, then ask God what submitted direction should govern it.

### Day 104 Reflection

What good gift needs to be received with gratitude and held with surrender?

Practice: Thank God for one good gift, then surrender how you use it, protect it, or release it.

### Weekly Review

- Where did honesty become a witness instead of an embarrassment?
- What hidden mercy kept my heart soft?
- What prayer did I bring to God beyond words?
- How did Sabbath rest bring peace to desire?
- Where did I return to the source before moving outward?
- What gift did I receive with gratitude and surrender?
