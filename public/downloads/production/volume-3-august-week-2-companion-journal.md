# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## August Week 2: Oil for the Waiting Season

Use these pages with Days 220-226.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, give oil for the waiting season: Sabbath steadiness under gathered pressure, mercy for long endurance, hope through unexpected help, hidden preparation, quiet courage, wise timing, and settled witness. Keep every difficult assignment submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 220 Reflection

What pressure has been gathering around my heart?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one gathered pressure, then breathe a prayer of trust before responding.

### Day 221 Reflection

Where has long waiting made my hope tired?

Practice: Tell God honestly where waiting has worn you down, then ask for one grace for today.

### Day 222 Reflection

Where have I limited God to one expected answer?

Practice: Release one narrow expectation about how God must answer, and ask Him to make you attentive.

### Day 223 Reflection

What quiet preparation step is God asking of me?

Practice: Take one quiet preparation step for a responsibility God has placed before you.

### Day 224 Reflection

What difficult responsibility requires quiet courage today?

Practice: Identify one difficult responsibility and take the next faithful step without dramatizing it.

### Day 225 Reflection

Where does timing matter more than urgency?

Practice: Pause before forcing a next step, and ask whether this is the right time and manner.

### Day 226 Reflection

Where do I need to return with prayerful courage?

Practice: Return to one unfinished faithful action with prayerful courage.

### Weekly Review

- What pressure needed Sabbath breath?
- Where did long waiting need mercy?
- How did God challenge my expected answer?
- What hidden preparation became faithful?
- What responsibility needed quiet courage?
- Where did timing need patience?
- What unfinished action did I return to with prayer?
