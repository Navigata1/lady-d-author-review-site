# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## October Week 3: The Spirit and Prayer

Use these pages with Days 286-292.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach prayer through clear witness, truthful tenderness, integrity under pressure, cleansed reactions, Sabbath grief, honest disappointment, and holy interruption before revenge. Keep my words, grief, and responses submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 286 Reflection

Where has my speech become indirect because honesty feels vulnerable?

Practice: Choose one honest sentence instead of a clever or indirect one.

### Day 287 Reflection

Where do I feel pulled between compassion and compromise?

Practice: In one tense relationship, ask God for tenderness that does not surrender truth.

### Day 288 Reflection

Where am I tempted to trade integrity for relief?

Practice: Before giving in for relief, pause and ask what integrity requires.

### Day 289 Reflection

Where has exposure made me feel cornered?

Practice: When you feel exposed or embarrassed, take one prayerful pause before answering.

### Day 290 Reflection

What is grief saying inside me, and what does God say?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one grief to God before you act out of it.

### Day 291 Reflection

Where did I assume a door would still be open?

Practice: Name one blocked expectation to God without demanding control of the outcome.

### Day 292 Reflection

Where am I using hurt to justify what love cannot bless?

Practice: Before answering hurt with harm, ask God to set your response apart.

### Weekly Review

- What honest sentence replaced indirect speech?
- Where did tenderness stay truthful?
- How did integrity outlast pressure?
- What reaction was cleansed before it became anger?
- How did Sabbath rest give grief room to breathe?
- What blocked expectation became prayer?
- Where did holy fire interrupt revenge?
