## Day 213 - August 1

### Lean Into the Spirit's Fruit

**Scripture Reference:** Judges 3:5

**Production lens correction:** The architecture assigned `leb chadash`; the production lens uses Israel dwelling among surrounding nations. The Spirit's fruit is framed as inward renewal for faithfulness while living in mixed surroundings.

Judges 3:5 names where Israel lived. They were among surrounding peoples, and the environment around them mattered.

This day falls on Saturday, the seventh-day Sabbath in the 2026 production calendar. Sabbath rest gives the heart space to ask what is shaping it. The Spirit's fruit grows from a renewed heart, not from pretending the surroundings have no influence.

Lean into the Spirit's fruit by asking where your environment has been discipling you quietly. Oil for the waiting season means receiving grace to stay rooted while life around you is mixed, noisy, tempting, or spiritually dull.

On this Sabbath, let the Spirit press this from information into formation. Fruit grows when the heart returns to God before it absorbs the atmosphere around it.

The Spirit gives oil for the waiting season by renewing the heart in mixed surroundings.

**Today step:** Notice one influence shaping your heart, and bring it under God's care before the day continues.

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, renew my heart where my surroundings have been shaping me. Let Sabbath rest root me again in God. Amen.

**Journal prompt:** What influence has been shaping my heart quietly?

**Morning impact:** Let the Spirit press this from information into formation.

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, renew my heart in rest. Help me receive mercy with gratitude and steward relief faithfully. Amen.

**Morning impact:** Let the Spirit press this from information into formation.
