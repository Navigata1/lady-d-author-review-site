# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## May Close: Learning His Voice

Use these pages beside Days 144-150.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, make me receptive to familiar grace, teach me to see holy presence in humble places, steady me when honor is withheld, grow roots under my hope, keep me close when voices are loud, hold grief in compassion, and let Sabbath rest form simple mercy. Amen.

### Day 144 Reflection

- Where has familiarity made me less receptive to Jesus?
- Practice: Revisit one familiar truth or passage and ask Jesus to teach you again.

### Day 145 Reflection

- What ordinary place might I be dismissing too quickly?
- Practice: Look again at one simple place where God may be present.

### Day 146 Reflection

- Where am I waiting to be understood before I obey?
- Practice: Release one demand to be understood and take the next faithful step.

### Day 147 Reflection

- What hidden practice is Jesus using to grow roots in me?
- Practice: Choose one root-growing practice and keep it quietly today.

### Day 148 Reflection

- What voice or report needs to be brought under the truth of Jesus?
- Practice: Bring one loud voice under Jesus before reacting.

### Day 149 Reflection

- What sorrow needs to be held in Christ's compassion today?
- Practice: Bring one grief or injustice to Jesus without explaining it away.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 150

- What simple mercy is Jesus inviting me to offer from a rested heart?
- Practice: Offer one simple mercy without performance pressure.

### Week Review

- Where did familiar grace become fresh again?
- What hidden root is Jesus growing?
- How did Christ meet grief with compassion?
- How did Sabbath rest awaken mercy without striving?
