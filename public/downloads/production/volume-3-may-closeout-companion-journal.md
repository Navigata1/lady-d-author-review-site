# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## May Closeout: Fruit That Grows in Surrender

Use these pages with Days 149-151.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, clothe my service in reverent strength, awaken true freedom as willing response, and breathe God's nearness into the generosity of my heart. Let the fruit of surrender continue beyond this month. Amen.

### Day 149 Reflection

Where has my service become too exposed, too hidden, or too dependent on being seen?

Practice: Bring one act of service under God's covering before you offer it to anyone else.

### Day 150 Reflection

Where have I mistaken freedom for delay?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one instruction from God that needs a willing next step instead of another delay.

### Day 151 Reflection

What fruit has surrender made possible in me this month?

Practice: Offer one willing gift from the heart: encouragement, time, attention, money, forgiveness, service, or prayer.

### May Review

- What fruit grew through surrender rather than force?
- Where did the Spirit make obedience more willing?
- What hidden place returned to God?
- How did Sabbath rest protect reverence and readiness?
- What generosity, witness, or service became more alive?
- What lesson from May should follow me into June?
