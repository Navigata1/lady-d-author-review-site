# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## August Closeout: Oil for the Waiting Season

Use these pages with Days 241-243.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, give oil for the waiting season: Sabbath trust when old pressures return, hope when depletion touches provision, and inner freedom when pressure feels multiplied. Close August with courage that stays submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 241 Reflection

Where has repeated loss made me afraid to sow again?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one place where disappointment has made you afraid to sow again, then ask God for courage to plant one faithful seed.

### Day 242 Reflection

Where do I need hope to rise in a depleted place?

Practice: Bring one depleted area to God honestly, then choose one small act that honors hope.

### Day 243 Reflection

Where does pressure feel multiplied, and what truth can I carry there?

Practice: Write one sentence of truth over a place that feels overwhelmed, then pray it slowly.

### August Review

- Where did God give oil for waiting instead of escape from waiting?
- Which Sabbath rests steadied breath, courage, or trust?
- What hidden fear became honest prayer?
- What boundary or return step protected obedience?
- Where did hope rise after depletion?
- What truth will I carry from August into September's Power to Witness arc?
