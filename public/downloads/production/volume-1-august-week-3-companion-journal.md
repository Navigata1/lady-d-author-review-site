# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## August Week 3: Love in Relationships

Use these pages with Days 224-230.

### Week Prayer

Father, let Your covenant love form grace, same-day obedience, patient change, Sabbath release, restoring compassion, refined belonging, and tested trust in me. Keep my relationships surrendered, wise, and gentle under Your care. Amen.

### Day 224 Reflection

Where am I rushing to prove myself before letting God's grace form my response?

Practice: Pause before acting from pressure, name what God has spoken, and choose one response shaped by grace.

### Day 225 Reflection

What act of obedience has been waiting for a quieter day, and what faithful step can I take before noon?

Practice: Take one concrete same-day step of obedience in a relationship, home rhythm, or private commitment.

### Day 226 Reflection

Where have I been using delay or history as a reason to avoid present obedience?

Practice: Name one place where change has felt too late, then take one faithful step that agrees with God's present call.

### Day 227 Sabbath Reflection

Who do I need to love with prayer and release instead of fear and control?

Practice: On this seventh-day Sabbath, pray for one beloved person by name, then release one controlling habit into the Father's care.

### Day 228 Reflection

Who is affected by my response to God, and how can I obey with compassion toward them?

Practice: Identify one person affected by your obedience, then include tenderness in your next faithful step.

### Day 229 Reflection

What shared pattern in my home, church, work, or ministry needs the refining mercy of God's promise?

Practice: Take one step that makes belonging more merciful and dignifying in that shared pattern.

### Day 230 Reflection

Where does trust feel tested, and what is the next faithful step that remains wise, clear, and surrendered to God?

Practice: Pray for clarity and take only the next faithful, wise step.

### Week Review

- Where did grace form my response before performance?
- What same-day obedience moved from insight into action?
- Where did I stop calling change too late?
- How did Sabbath rest help me release a beloved person into God's care?
- Who received more compassion because I noticed how my obedience affected them?
- What shared pattern became more merciful or dignifying?
- Where did tested trust become clearer, wiser, and more surrendered?
