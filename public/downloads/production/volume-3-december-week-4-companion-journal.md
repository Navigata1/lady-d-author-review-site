# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## December Week 4: Living Sealed and Filled

Use these pages with Days 356-362.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach me to live sealed and filled as Jesus quiets fear, calls for witness, forms honest allegiance, frees me from counterfeit peace, gives Sabbath breath in family pressure, brings hope near in close relational pain, and purifies my loves under the first love of Jesus. Make the Father's care more real than worry, make confession humble, make loyalty clear, make truth peaceable, and make love rightly ordered. Amen.

### Day 356 Reflection

Where has fear made me forget my value to the Father?

Practice: Pause before one anxious response and breathe a prayer of being seen and valued by God.

### Day 357 Reflection

Where has silence become self-protection instead of wisdom?

Practice: Name Jesus faithfully in one place where quiet courage is needed.

### Day 358 Reflection

Where has convenience tempted me to hide my allegiance to Jesus?

Practice: Refuse one convenient compromise that would hide your loyalty to Jesus.

### Day 359 Reflection

Where have I called avoidance peace?

Practice: Choose one truthful, peaceable response instead of an avoidance pattern.

### Day 360 Reflection

Where have family expectations made obedience feel suffocating?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one family loyalty pressure to Jesus without surrendering obedience.

### Day 361 Reflection

Where has close relational pressure made me feel alone?

Practice: Name one close relational pressure to God and choose one non-bitter, truthful response.

### Day 362 Reflection

Where has a good love started demanding first place over Jesus?

Practice: Put one good love back under Jesus through a concrete obedient choice.

### Weekly Review

- Where did the Father's care quiet fear?
- Where did quiet courage become confession?
- What convenient compromise was refused?
- Where did truth become peaceable instead of avoidant?
- How did Sabbath rest give breath in family pressure?
- What close relational pain was met without bitterness?
- What love was reordered under Jesus?
