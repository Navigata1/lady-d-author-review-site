# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## January Week 2: The Depth of God's Love

Use these pages beside Days 008-014.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, give me courage to stand, grace to remember, a whole heart to love You, and humility to keep Your ways as a response to Your mercy. Amen.

### Day 008 Reflection

**Focus:** Courage in transition.

**Write:** Where am I being asked to stand instead of shrink?

**Practice:** Take one faithful step before asking for perfect confidence.

### Day 009 Reflection

**Focus:** Remembering the promise that holds.

**Write:** What past mercy proves that God was already working before I understood?

**Practice:** Carry one written evidence of God's faithfulness today.

### Day 010 Reflection

**Focus:** Whole-heart love.

**Write:** Which part of my life is still divided from surrender?

**Practice:** Invite God into one hidden compartment of your daily life.

### Day 011 Reflection

**Focus:** Obedience as love made visible.

**Write:** What act of obedience would make love visible today?

**Practice:** Do it quietly, without needing applause.

### Day 012 Reflection

**Focus:** Resting in covenant mercy.

**Write:** What non-material blessing has kept my soul alive?

**Practice:** Thank God specifically for that blessing.

### Day 013 Reflection

**Focus:** Being led after deliverance.

**Write:** Where do I need to walk more like someone God is still leading?

**Practice:** Ask for the next step, then take only the next step.

### Day 014 Reflection

**Focus:** Correction without rejection.

**Write:** Where might God be refining me in love?

**Practice:** Receive one correction without defending your old pattern.

### Week 2 Review

1. What courage did love ask of me this week?
2. What promise held me?
3. Where did obedience feel like love instead of pressure?
4. What did I learn about Sabbath, grace, and faithful living?
5. What needs to be surrendered before the next week begins?
