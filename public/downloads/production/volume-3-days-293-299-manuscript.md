## Day 293 - October 20

### Remember Rain for Dry Places

**Scripture Reference:** Judges 15:7

**Production lens correction:** The architecture assigned `chayil`; the production lens uses Samson's vow of revenge without quoting it. Rain for dry places is framed as Spirit-given capacity to let prayer reach the place where injury wants to become retaliation.

Judges 15:7 brings Samson's wounded resolve into the open. The injury has become a plan.

Rain for dry places is needed when pain has hardened into a sentence you are ready to carry out. The Spirit and prayer bring strength beyond natural ability, not so revenge can feel spiritual, but so the heart can yield before it burns everything around it.

Remember rain for dry places by asking where anger has become your weather. The Spirit can make this moment spiritual oxygen: clear, reverent, practical, and strong enough to start the day.

Walk out of this page with courage, tenderness, and clarity. Courage is not the same as payback.

The Spirit teaches prayer by sending strength into the dry place where revenge has been rehearsed.

**Today step:** Before repeating the story of who hurt you, ask God what surrender would look like now.

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, send rain into the dry place in me. Give me strength to surrender revenge and courage to stay clean before Jesus. Amen.

**Journal prompt:** Where has anger become the weather I expect to live under?

**Morning impact:** Walk out of this page with courage, tenderness, and clarity.

## Day 295 - October 22

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, let peace lead in me. Make God's nearness discernible when fear gathers around my decisions. Amen.

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, let grace form wind in my waiting. Cleanse resentment and teach me faithfulness inside limitation. Amen.

**Journal prompt:** Where am I moving forward but still feeling constrained?
