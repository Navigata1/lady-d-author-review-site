# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## August Close / September Week 1: From The Surrendered Heart to Serving with His Compassion

Use these pages beside Days 242-248.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, faithful Friend and saving Lord, lead me out of evasive answers and into honest following. Teach me to serve from belonging, honor repentance wherever grace is moving, receive the beloved Son before representing Him, and rest in Sabbath stewardship over what belongs to You. Amen.

### Day 242 Reflection

- What honest answer have I been delaying because obedience would follow?
- Practice: Answer one avoided question honestly before Jesus and take the next obedient step.

### Day 243 Reflection

- Where am I calling avoidance wisdom?
- Practice: Replace one evasive phrase with an honest prayer.

### Day 244 Reflection

- What vineyard work is the Father placing before me today?
- Practice: Do one small act of service as a response to the Father's voice, not as a performance.

### Day 245 Reflection

- Who have I been defining by an old label instead of present grace?
- Practice: Pray for someone you have been tempted to define by an old label, then treat them with kingdom hope.

### Day 246 Reflection

- Whose transformation should be softening my own heart?
- Practice: When you notice grace in someone else, turn it into praise and your own repentance.

### Day 247 Reflection

- Where has serving become easier than beholding Jesus?
- Practice: Pause before one act of service and behold Jesus as the beloved Son.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 248

- What part of God's vineyard have I been carrying as if it belonged to me?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, release one ministry, family, or work burden back to God as His vineyard.

### Week Review

- Where did I answer Jesus more honestly?
- What evasion became a prayer?
- What act of service came from belonging?
- How did I honor repentance in someone else?
- Where did beholding Jesus reshape my service?
- How did Sabbath rest return ownership to God?
