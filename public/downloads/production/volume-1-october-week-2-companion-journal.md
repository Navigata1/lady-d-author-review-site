# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## October Week 2: Called by Love

Use these pages with Days 280-286.

### Week Prayer

Father, call me by love into the places where I wait, cross, release, wrestle, receive weakness, hold on for blessing, and answer truthfully before You. Let beloved identity, courage, grace, Sabbath mercy, patience, hope, and restoring compassion form me this week. Amen.

### Day 280 Reflection

Where am I waiting for a response that is trying to rename me?

Practice: After taking one faithful step, refuse to rehearse your worth while you wait for the response.

### Day 281 Reflection

What necessary crossing needs courage without hardness and rest without avoidance?

Practice: Name one necessary step you have been avoiding, then ask God for courage and tenderness before you take it.

### Day 282 Reflection

What am I clutching that needs to be entrusted to God with open hands?

Practice: Release one person, plan, or outcome into God's care with a concrete prayer instead of another controlling action.

### Day 283 Sabbath Reflection

What struggle needs to be brought into Sabbath prayer without being polished first?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one honest struggle into prayer without polishing it first.

### Day 284 Reflection

Where has endurance become self-reliance instead of dependence on God?

Practice: Name one place where endurance has become self-reliance, then ask God for honest dependence and wise limits.

### Day 285 Reflection

What blessing do I need to ask God for directly instead of settling for survival?

Practice: Ask God directly for the blessing you need: transformation, courage, mercy, wisdom, or a heart that will not return unchanged.

### Day 286 Reflection

What name, label, or story needs to be brought honestly before God?

Practice: Write one name, label, or story you have been carrying, then ask God what truth He wants to speak over it.

### Week Review

- Where did beloved identity steady me while I waited?
- What crossing required courage and tenderness together?
- What did I entrust to God with open hands?
- How did Sabbath prayer make room for an honest struggle?
- Where did weakness interrupt self-reliance?
- What blessing did I ask God for directly?
- What name or story did I bring honestly into God's presence?
