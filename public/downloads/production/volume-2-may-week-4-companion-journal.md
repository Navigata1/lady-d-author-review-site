# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## May Week 4: Learning His Voice

Use these pages beside Days 137-143.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, wake my heart through Your teaching, free me from anxious ownership, soften the hard places, correct what I have excused, give me discernment without pride, make serious truth lead to surrender, and let Sabbath rest help me carry Your word as bread for the road. Amen.

### Day 137 Reflection

- What truth from Jesus needs to become breath in me today?
- Practice: Choose one teaching of Jesus and sit with it until it becomes prayer.

### Day 138 Reflection

- Where am I confusing faithful participation with anxious ownership?
- Practice: Release one outcome and ask Jesus how to participate faithfully.

### Day 139 Reflection

- What has hardened my listening, and how is Jesus inviting me back?
- Practice: Name one thing that has hardened your listening and ask Jesus to soften it.

### Day 140 Reflection

- What have I been excusing that Jesus is asking me to surrender?
- Practice: Take one honest step away from one compromise.

### Day 141 Reflection

- Where do I need clarity without harshness?
- Practice: Ask Jesus for discernment in one relationship, decision, or commitment.

### Day 142 Reflection

- What choice becomes clearer when I remember that Jesus is Lord of the end and the beginning?
- Practice: Let one serious truth from Jesus clarify one choice today.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 143

- What word from Jesus do I need to carry into Sabbath rest and beyond?
- Practice: Carry one teaching from the week into rest, prayer, and one act of obedience.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus wake my heart beneath the surface?
- What hardened place began to soften?
- Where did discernment become clearer without pride?
- What teaching needs to keep feeding me after this Sabbath?
