## KDP Formula Summary

- Cover width = 0.125 + back cover width + spine width + front cover width + 0.125.
- Cover height = 0.125 + trim height + 0.125.
- 6 x 9 full-wrap cover height with bleed: 9.25 in.
- 6 x 9 full-wrap cover width before spine: 12.25 in.
- Black-and-white white-paper spine width: page count x 0.002252 in.
- Black-and-white cream-paper spine width: page count x 0.0025 in.
- 6 x 9 interior page size without bleed: 6 x 9 in.
- 6 x 9 interior page size with bleed: 6.125 x 9.25 in.

## Recommended Interior Path

1. Build final 6 x 9 interior templates for each manuscript and companion journal.
2. Use no-bleed interiors unless a later design pass intentionally adds edge-to-edge art.
3. Apply mirrored margins and the KDP minimum gutter by page-count band.
4. Add front matter, copyright, dedication, series page, author note, permissions note, and closing pages before locking page count.
5. Re-export final PDF interiors and rerun page-count extraction.
6. Recalculate full-wrap cover dimensions from the final page count and selected paper type.

## Recommended Cover Path

1. Pick a visual route from the nine-cover board: Path, Modern Sacred Minimal, Sanctuary, or a hybrid route.
2. Decide paper type for each book: white paper for brighter covers and modern contrast, or cream paper for warmer devotional feel and slightly thicker spine.
3. Use the chosen final page count and paper type in the official KDP cover calculator before export.
4. Build a full-wrap PDF cover with back cover, spine, and front cover as one image/PDF.
5. Keep front/back text at least 0.125 in inside trim lines and keep spine text at least 0.0625 in from spine edges.
6. Avoid hard vertical color breaks at the spine because KDP allows binding/trim variance.
7. Export print cover art at 300 DPI, flattened, with fonts embedded or outlined in the production PDF.
