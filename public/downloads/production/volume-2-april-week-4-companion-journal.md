# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## April Week 4: Humble Peace and Courageous Allegiance

Use these pages beside Days 109-115.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, reconnect me to faithful spiritual family, refine my influence, guide my discernment, and teach me truthful peace. Let Sabbath rest steady my allegiance to You with courage and tenderness. Amen.

### Day 109 Reflection

- Where has loneliness been telling me I am forgotten?
- Practice: Send one honest encouragement to someone in your faith family.

### Day 110 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to control instead of becoming a faithful example?
- Practice: Lead by example before instruction in one place today.

### Day 111 Reflection

- Where is Jesus asking me to stay faithful instead of scattering my attention?
- Practice: Name one place to stay and one distraction to release.

### Day 112 Reflection

- How do I usually enter tense spaces?
- Practice: Choose a peaceful first sentence before one conversation.

### Day 113 Reflection

- What fear rises when I imagine Jesus calling my ordinary life?
- Practice: Write your name and ask Jesus how He is calling you today.

### Day 114 Reflection

- Who do I need to receive with more mercy and less comparison?
- Practice: Thank God for one different gift or story.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 115

- Where do I need Christ's peace to help me love faithfully?
- Practice: Pray for one strained relationship and release the need to manage every reaction.

### Week Review

- Where did grace reconnect me?
- How did Jesus refine my influence?
- What peaceful first sentence changed a conversation?
- What Sabbath courage should I carry into the next week?
