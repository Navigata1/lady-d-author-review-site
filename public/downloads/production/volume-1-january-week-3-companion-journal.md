# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## January Week 3: The Depth of God's Love

Use these pages beside Days 015-021.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, reveal Your goodness, steady my long view, rename what fear has distorted, walk with me on the uncertain road, and teach me forgiveness, trust, and courage. Amen.

### Day 015 Reflection

**Focus:** God's goodness, grace, and compassion.

**Write:** Where do I need God's character more than an explanation?

**Practice:** Ask God to reveal His goodness in one specific pressure point.

### Day 016 Reflection

**Focus:** Grace that tells the truth.

**Write:** What am I still trying to hide behind performance?

**Practice:** Confess one thing plainly to God without polishing it.

### Day 017 Reflection

**Focus:** Peace over the whole journey.

**Write:** What long-term concern needs to be surrendered to the Father?

**Practice:** Pray over the whole span of that concern, not just today's outcome.

### Day 018 Reflection

**Focus:** Identity spoken by God.

**Write:** What old name tries to return when promise feels slow?

**Practice:** Write one true sentence about who God is forming you to become.

### Day 019 Reflection

**Focus:** God's presence on the road.

**Write:** Where am I between leaving and returning, fear and peace, old and new?

**Practice:** Pause three times today to pray, "Father, be with me on the way."

### Day 020 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy after family hurt.

**Write:** Where do I need God's wisdom for truth, boundaries, and forgiveness?

**Practice:** Place one wound before God without minimizing it.

### Day 021 Reflection

**Focus:** Trust inside honest lament.

**Write:** What honest prayer have I been afraid to bring to God?

**Practice:** End the prayer by naming one way God's love is still trustworthy.

### Week 3 Review

1. What did God reveal about His goodness this week?
2. What did I stop performing?
3. What old name lost power?
4. What road did I walk with more awareness of God's presence?
5. How did I practice mercy without denial?
