# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## May Close and June Week 1: Restored by the Father's Heart

Use these pages beside Days 151-157.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, hold my days in covenant mercy, shine Your light on real ground, free me from comparison, place my life where it can serve, separate mercy from mixture, teach me Your patient rhythm, and restore living movement through Saturday Sabbath rest. Amen.

---

### Day 151 Reflection

**Focus:** Covenant mercy over time and seasons.

**Write:** What timeline do I need to surrender to the Father while still obeying today's light?

**Practice:** Place one timeline you cannot control before God, and choose one faithful action that belongs to this day.

### Day 152 Reflection

**Focus:** Beloved identity receiving light.

**Write:** Where do I need to receive the Father's light instead of trying to manufacture my own?

**Practice:** Ask God to shine light on one undefined place, then refuse to force an answer beyond what He gives today.

### Day 153 Reflection

**Focus:** Freedom from false measuring.

**Write:** Where has comparison made me despise the light God actually assigned to me?

**Practice:** Bless one person's assignment without comparison, then name one faithful way God has asked you to shine where you are placed.

### Day 154 Reflection

**Focus:** Grace that receives placement.

**Write:** Where am I trying to be seen instead of trusting the Father to place me?

**Practice:** Ask God to refine one place where you have been chasing visibility instead of receiving placement.

### Day 155 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy that separates mixture.

**Write:** What mixture do I need the Father to lovingly separate so mercy can rule clearly?

**Practice:** Name one mixture in your life and ask God to separate what is light from what keeps pulling you toward darkness.

### Day 156 Reflection

**Focus:** The Father's patient rhythm.

**Write:** What do I need to close or entrust so this day can keep a faithful rhythm?

**Practice:** Choose one boundary that lets this day close faithfully: stop a task, silence a device, pray over what remains, or prepare one thing for tomorrow.

### Day 157 Reflection

**Focus:** Saturday Sabbath movement from love, not fear.

**Write:** Where has fear been making me freeze or rush instead of moving from the Father's love?

**Practice:** Keep one Sabbath pause before any major action today, and ask whether love or fear is moving you.

---

### Week Review

1. What timeline did I surrender while still obeying today's light?
2. Where did I receive light instead of manufacturing it?
3. How did I bless another person's assignment without comparison?
4. Where did grace refine my desire to be seen?
5. What mixture did the Father help me separate?
6. What boundary helped this day keep a faithful rhythm?
7. How did Saturday Sabbath rest restore movement from love instead of fear?
