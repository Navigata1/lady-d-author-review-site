# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## June Week 4: Gifts for the Body

Use these pages with Days 173-179.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, free my gifts from isolation, strengthen me to receive correction, renew desire near the Comforter, let truthful fire lead to peace, turn honest limitation into prayer, set scarcity logic under God's sufficiency, and strengthen trust in God's power. Amen.

### Day 173 Reflection

Where do I need to stop carrying alone?

Practice: Identify one burden that needs shared leadership, counsel, prayer, or practical help.

### Day 174 Reflection

Where do I want satisfaction without correction?

Practice: Ask God to show one appetite or demand that needs correction before it becomes control.

### Day 175 Reflection

Where would more still fail to heal what my heart is seeking?

Practice: Pause one demand for more and ask what your heart is really seeking beneath it.

### Day 176 Reflection

Where have I softened the name of rejection in my heart?

Practice: Name one complaint honestly, then ask whether it is hiding rejection of God's provision or path.

### Day 177 Reflection

What practical impossibility needs to be named before God?

Practice: Name one practical limitation before God without exaggeration or denial.

### Day 178 Reflection

Where have I reduced God's answer to the resources I can count?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one resource question to God and ask Him to lead beyond panic or denial.

### Day 179 Reflection

Where have I quietly shortened God's ability in my imagination?

Practice: Replace one fear-based conclusion with a prayer that honors God's ability.

### Weekly Review

- What burden needs shared leadership?
- What correction did I receive as mercy?
- Where did desire come back under God?
- What complaint needed a truer name?
- What limitation became prayer?
- How did Sabbath rest loosen scarcity logic?
- What fear-based conclusion needs to be replaced with trust?
