# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## June Week 1: Gifts for the Body

Use these pages with Days 152-158.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, breathe holy purpose into available gifts, set resources apart for renewal, refine humble craft, water wisdom from the inside, turn influence into witness, restore my belonging to the body, and keep my calling near mercy. Amen.

### Day 152 Reflection

What gift have I dismissed as too small for God to use?

Practice: Name one available gift you can offer for someone else's strengthening this week.

### Day 153 Reflection

Where has fear made me treat a gift as too precious to release?

Practice: Ask God how one resource, skill, or opportunity can become renewal for someone else.

### Day 154 Reflection

What faithful craft have I undervalued because it is quiet or unseen?

Practice: Offer one careful act of skill today without needing it to be noticed.

### Day 155 Reflection

Where is my gift functioning while my heart needs renewal?

Practice: Pause before one task and ask the Spirit to renew the heart that carries it.

### Day 156 Reflection

Where does my influence need to become an offering instead of a shield?

Practice: Use one piece of influence to strengthen someone else without centering yourself.

### Day 157 Reflection

Where have I been acting detached from the body God has called me to love?

Practice: On this Sabbath, reach toward the body in one concrete way: encourage, attend, pray, give, serve, reconcile, or ask for help.

### Day 158 Reflection

Where do I need God to name me before I use the gift?

Practice: Bring one gift before God silently and let Him name it before you use it.

### Weekly Review

- What available gift did I stop dismissing?
- Where did fear loosen around a resource or opportunity?
- What quiet craft became worship?
- How did my heart receive renewal before serving?
- Where did influence become humble witness?
- How did Sabbath belonging pull me closer to the body?
- What gift needs to stay near mercy?
