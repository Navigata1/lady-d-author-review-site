# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## July Closeout: Rain Down on Me

Use these pages with Days 210-212.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, close this month by consecrating unpracticed faith, settling mercy in named pressure, and breathing life into loyal obedience. Let rain fall where comfort must become formation and insight must become response. Amen.

### Day 210 Reflection

Where does my faith need consecrated practice?

Practice: Choose one small practice of obedience in an area where your faith needs training.

### Day 211 Reflection

What pressure needs to be named before God today?

Practice: Name one pressure honestly before God and ask for settled obedience there.

### Day 212 Reflection

Where is obedience revealing the condition of my heart?

Practice: Choose one command or conviction you already know, and practice it as a response to grace.

### July Review

- Where did rain come as discernment rather than control?
- What craving or pressure needed honest naming?
- What overlooked need came into God's light?
- What transition needed tenderness?
- Where did Sabbath rest slow me down for counsel?
- What untested place began becoming practiced faith?
- How did obedience become a response to grace this month?
