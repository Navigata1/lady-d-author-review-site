# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## August Week 3: The Surrendered Heart

Use these pages beside Days 221-227.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, expose comparison and form gratitude in me. Keep me listening on the road of costly obedience. Make family desire whole, reorder my longing for honor, sober my asking, heal control into servant care, and let this Sabbath teach me restful attentiveness to Your mercy. Amen.

### Day 221 Reflection

- Where is comparison making God's generosity feel threatening?
- Practice: Thank God for one grace He has given you, then bless one grace He has given someone else.

### Day 222 Reflection

- What costly road is Jesus asking me to keep walking with Him?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to name one faithful road He is asking you to keep walking.

### Day 223 Reflection

- Where might love and ambition be mixed together in my prayers?
- Practice: Bring one family or ministry desire to Jesus and ask Him to purify the motive beneath it.

### Day 224 Reflection

- What desire needs to become honest before Jesus today?
- Practice: Name one desire for recognition or security, then practice one hidden act of faithfulness.

### Day 225 Reflection

- What request needs to be tested by surrender rather than visibility?
- Practice: Review one request and ask Jesus what kind of obedience it would require.

### Day 226 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to use influence for control instead of care?
- Practice: Use one area of influence today to serve, protect, or lift someone else.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 227

- Where is Jesus asking me to follow with less hurry and more mercy?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, slow your pace and ask Jesus who He wants you to notice with mercy.

### Week Review

- Where did comparison lose power?
- What road did Jesus ask me to keep walking?
- How did Jesus purify desire, ambition, or influence?
- Where did servant care replace control?
- How did Sabbath rest make me more attentive to mercy?
