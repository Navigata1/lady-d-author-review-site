# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## January Week 2: The Promise of the Spirit

Use these pages with Days 008-014.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, consecrate my responsibilities, meet my waiting with grace, guard hidden obedience, change my responses, order my worship, draw me near with reverence, and stir my heart toward willing offerings that honor God. Amen.

### Day 008 Reflection

Where do I need to receive responsibility as consecration instead of personal importance?

Practice: Name one responsibility God has placed in your hands, and dedicate it back to Him before you act on it.

### Day 009 Reflection

What old pattern do I need to stop treating as my permanent identity?

Practice: Ask the Spirit for one fresh response that would not come naturally from your old pattern.

### Day 010 Sabbath Reflection

What needs to mature with God before I try to explain it to everyone else?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one hidden calling, burden, or decision to God before explaining it widely.

### Day 011 Reflection

Where do I need more than inspiration and actually need God to change my response?

Practice: Practice one Spirit-shaped response in a place where your old pattern would normally lead.

### Day 012 Reflection

Where does my devotion need more faithful order, care, or follow-through?

Practice: Choose one act of devotion or service and bring it into better order as an offering to God.

### Day 013 Reflection

How can I prepare my heart for God's nearness without becoming casual or afraid?

Practice: Prepare one quiet place or moment for prayer, and enter it with honesty, confession, and reverence.

### Day 014 Reflection

What offering has the Spirit been stirring in me that I have delayed bringing?

Practice: Identify one Spirit-stirred offering you can bring today, then act on it before hesitation talks you out of obedience.

### Week Review

- What responsibility became consecration this week?
- Where did grace interrupt an old pattern?
- What did Sabbath rest help me carry quietly with God?
- Where did the Spirit change an actual response?
- What act of worship or service received better order?
- How did reverent nearness shape my prayer?
- What willing offering did I bring?
