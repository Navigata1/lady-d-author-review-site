# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## September Week 4: Serving with His Compassion

Use these pages beside Days 263-269.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, living Word and Prince of Peace, teach me to serve from rescued courage, whole-hearted love, generous invitation, revealing light, deeper Lordship, victorious authority, and Sabbath attention to Your call. Amen.

### Day 263 Reflection

- What fear needs to hear what God has spoken?
- Practice: Carry one fear into Scripture and let God's voice answer before anxiety does.

### Day 264 Reflection

- What would love require from me in one ordinary place today?
- Practice: Choose one action today because it loves God or serves your neighbor well.

### Day 265 Reflection

- Where can I carry the King's invitation with tenderness?
- Practice: Offer one gentle invitation toward hope, prayer, or encouragement.

### Day 266 Reflection

- What question from Jesus might reveal the path more clearly?
- Practice: Sit with one question Jesus may be asking your heart before you answer the day.

### Day 267 Reflection

- Where have I made Jesus smaller than He is?
- Practice: Name one area where Jesus is inviting deeper Lordship, then yield it in prayer.

### Day 268 Reflection

- What outcome do I need to place under Christ's authority?
- Practice: Release one outcome to the reigning Christ and choose faithful love instead of control.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 269

- What ordinary concern has been making the King's invitation feel light?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, set aside one distraction and give Jesus your full attention before serving.

### Week Review

- Where did God's living Word rescue my courage?
- What loving action brought peace into the day?
- How did I carry invitation without pressure?
- What question from Jesus brought light?
- Where did Sabbath worship restore holy attention?
