# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## February Week 2: Abiding Close to Jesus in Compassion and Courage

Use these pages with Days 039-045.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to abide close to You when grief and need meet. Wake compassion in me, meet my fear of lack, protect hidden prayer, speak courage in the storm, clarify faith from impulse, and call me forward from Sabbath-rooted belonging. Amen.

### Day 039 Reflection

Where do I need to pause with Jesus instead of rushing past grief or weariness?

Practice: Take five quiet minutes with Jesus before serving, answering, fixing, or explaining anything.

### Day 040 Reflection

Where has my heart grown numb, and how is Jesus inviting compassion to wake up again?

Practice: Notice one need you would usually pass by, then respond with one concrete act of mercy.

### Day 041 Reflection

What "not enough" thought needs to be brought near to Jesus today?

Practice: Name one shortage you feel, then ask Jesus for the next faithful step instead of rehearsing the lack.

### Day 042 Reflection

What crowd, demand, or noise do I need to dismiss long enough to pray?

Practice: Set aside one protected prayer moment today, even if it is brief, and keep it without apology.

### Day 043 Reflection

Where do I need the voice of Jesus to become louder than fear?

Practice: When fear rises today, pause and say aloud, "Jesus is near, and fear is not Lord."

### Day 044 Reflection

Where do I need Jesus to distinguish faith from impulse before I move?

Practice: Before taking a risky or emotional step, ask Jesus to clarify whether it is obedience, impulse, fear, or pressure.

### Day 045 Sabbath Reflection

Where is Jesus calling me closer, and what step can I take from rest rather than pressure?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one place Jesus is saying "Come," then answer with one small step from rest, not pressure.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus give grief a peaceful place to go?
- How did compassion wake up again?
- What shortage did I bring near to Jesus?
- What protected prayer moment helped love stay whole?
- Where did Jesus speak courage over fear?
- What impulse did His light clarify?
- How did Sabbath-rooted belonging shape my next step?
