# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## February Close: Abiding Close to Jesus in Remembered Provision and Teachable Faith

Use these pages with Days 053-059 plus the February 29 bonus.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to remember Your provision when lack feels loud, bring my questions near You, trust Your restoring work, walk peacefully when misunderstood, carry loved ones to Your mercy, become teachable after weakness, and receive extra grace for hard truth without turning away. Amen.

### Day 053 Reflection

What small practical concern is trying to become larger than Jesus in my attention?

Practice: Name one practical concern, then ask Jesus to help you respond without blame, panic, or shame.

### Day 054 Reflection

What worry keeps repeating itself, and what mercy from Jesus needs to answer it?

Practice: Write one sentence of worry, then answer it with one remembered mercy from Jesus.

### Day 055 Reflection

What question have I been afraid to bring honestly to Jesus?

Practice: Bring one sincere question to Jesus in writing, then sit quietly before trying to answer it yourself.

### Day 056 Reflection

Where do I need to believe that Jesus can restore what confusion or pain has damaged?

Practice: Name one damaged place before Jesus and ask what restored faithfulness could look like today.

### Day 057 Reflection

Where am I asking to be understood before I am willing to obey peacefully?

Practice: Identify one place you feel misunderstood, then choose one Christlike response that does not depend on being fully explained.

### Day 058 Reflection

What family burden or loved one do I need to carry to Jesus instead of carrying alone?

Practice: Pray for one loved one by name, asking Jesus for mercy while releasing the pressure to control the outcome.

### Day 059 Sabbath Reflection

What weakness or failure is Jesus inviting me to bring into private, teachable nearness?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one weakness to Jesus privately and ask, "What do You want to teach me here?"

### February 29 Bonus Reflection

What hard truth is Jesus asking me to hold with Him instead of avoiding?

Practice: Let this bonus day hold one hard truth with Jesus, asking Him for courage to stay near instead of turning away.

### Week Review

- What practical lack did Jesus steady before worry grew?
- What remembered mercy answered anxiety?
- What sincere question came near Jesus?
- Where did restoration hope rise?
- How did I obey peacefully while misunderstood?
- What loved one did I carry to Jesus with released control?
- How did Sabbath rest turn weakness into teachable nearness?
- What hard truth did extra grace help me hold with Jesus?
