# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## September Week 2: Serving with His Compassion

Use these pages beside Days 249-255.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, Prince of Peace and trusted Lord, teach me to serve with the strength of Your gentleness. Let me lay down what blocks Your way, receive Your light, carry Your invitation, wear Your grace honestly, discern without cruelty, and rest in the Sabbath joy of Your feast. Amen.

### Day 249 Reflection

- Where is Jesus asking me to serve with meek strength?
- Practice: Choose one act of service that carries gentleness instead of pressure.

### Day 250 Reflection

- What do I need to place before Jesus so His way is honored?
- Practice: Lay down one preference that has been blocking compassionate obedience.

### Day 251 Reflection

- What story or word from Jesus is exposing a guarded place in me?
- Practice: Read one hard word from Jesus slowly and ask what fear it is exposing gently.

### Day 252 Reflection

- Who might need me to carry the King's welcome today?
- Practice: Offer one word or act of welcome to someone who may feel outside the room.

### Day 253 Reflection

- Where am I near the invitation but not fully receiving the covering of grace?
- Practice: Bring one uncovered place to Jesus and ask Him to clothe it with grace and repentance.

### Day 254 Reflection

- Where do I need peace that can tell the truth without becoming harsh?
- Practice: In one tense exchange, choose clear peace over anxious reaction.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 255

- Where do I need to receive the invitation before I try to carry it?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, receive the King's invitation again, then share one word of mercy with someone else.

### Week Review

- Where did gentleness become strength?
- What did I lay down for the King's path?
- What fear did Jesus expose through His words?
- Who did I welcome with hope?
- Where did grace clothe what shame exposed?
- How did Sabbath worship renew the invitation?
