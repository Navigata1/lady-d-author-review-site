# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## April Week 2: Faithful Mercy and Resurrection Peace

Use these pages beside Days 095-101.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, heal my stewardship, my attention, my mercy, and my witness. Let Your grace and peace steady me, and let Sabbath rest root me in resurrection hope. Amen.

### Day 095 Reflection

- What has Jesus entrusted to me that needs honest review?
- Practice: Choose one faithful next action in that area.

### Day 096 Reflection

- Where do I need to trust Jesus' authority as healing?
- Practice: Pray "Jesus, reign here" over one disordered situation.

### Day 097 Reflection

- Who have I stopped seeing?
- Practice: Offer one practical act of mercy or encouragement.

### Day 098 Reflection

- Where is Jesus moving me from casual agreement into lived mercy?
- Practice: Turn one conviction into one concrete act of love.

### Day 099 Reflection

- What testimony of Jesus is my life carrying?
- Practice: Share one honest sentence about what He is teaching or healing.

### Day 100 Reflection

- Where do I need grace and peace to steady my courage?
- Practice: Speak "grace and peace" over one anxious place before acting.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 101

- How does the risen Lord's love change the way I enter this Sabbath?
- Practice: Write one sentence of worship naming Jesus as faithful, risen, reigning, and loving.

### Week Review

- What did Jesus ask me to steward honestly?
- Where did mercy become visible?
- How did grace and peace steady my courage?
- What Sabbath hope should I carry into the next week?
