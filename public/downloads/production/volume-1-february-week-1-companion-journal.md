# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## February Week 1: Beloved Identity

Use these pages beside Days 032-038.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, teach me to live as Your beloved child: returning quickly, receiving Your compassion, walking from belonging, welcoming correction, and listening so I may live. Amen.

### Day 032 Reflection

**Focus:** Returning with heart and soul.

**Write:** What part of my heart needs to return instead of hiding?

**Practice:** Pray over one divided place and surrender it before noon.

### Day 033 Reflection

**Focus:** The Father gathering what has been scattered.

**Write:** Where do I need the Father to gather what has been scattered?

**Practice:** Take one gathering step: simplify, confess, organize, rest, call, or pray.

### Day 034 Reflection

**Focus:** Moving from belonging instead of pressure.

**Write:** What step am I trying to take from pressure that God wants me to take from beloved identity?

**Practice:** Pause before one task and receive it as a sent step from belonging.

### Day 035 Reflection

**Focus:** Receiving covenant mercy without suspicion.

**Write:** Where have I been bracing for rejection when the Father is inviting me to receive mercy?

**Practice:** Thank God for one place where His goodness has been more faithful than your fear.

### Day 036 Reflection

**Focus:** Rival loves and holy correction.

**Write:** What rival love has been asking for too much of my heart?

**Practice:** Name it honestly and ask the Father for refining mercy.

### Day 037 Reflection

**Focus:** Remaining close when life feels full.

**Write:** What good gift could become a distraction if I stop returning it to God?

**Practice:** Thank God for the gift and return it to His lordship.

### Day 038 Reflection

**Focus:** Listening so beloved people may live.

**Write:** Where is God asking me to listen so I may live more fully as His beloved child?

**Practice:** Begin Sabbath worship or another spiritual practice with a prayer for teachable listening.

### Week 1 Review

1. Where did I return more quickly?
2. What scattered place did the Father begin gathering?
3. How did belonging change the way I moved through work or family life?
4. What correction felt like mercy?
5. How did Sabbath or another practice become more life-giving?
