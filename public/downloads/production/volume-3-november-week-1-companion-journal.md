# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## November Week 1: Freedom, Fire, and Holiness

Use these pages with Days 307-313.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form freedom, fire, and holiness in the way I receive, stand, wait, obey, rest, grow, and keep breathing under questions. Set my provision apart for witness, seal my belonging in charged places, cleanse my readiness, teach me holy timing, breathe witness from a changed heart, free me from old labels, and strengthen me when questions remain. Amen.

### Day 307 Reflection

How do I respond when God provides through ordinary hands?

Practice: Receive one help or kindness with gratitude and no self-importance.

### Day 308 Reflection

What atmosphere makes me forget who I am in God?

Practice: Before entering a pressured environment, pause and remember who names you.

### Day 309 Reflection

Where does opportunity make me move faster than surrender?

Practice: Before acting on an opportunity, ask God to cleanse your motive and pace.

### Day 310 Reflection

Where do I want direction without delay?

Practice: Practice one act of obedient waiting instead of forcing a premature answer.

### Day 311 Reflection

Where do I want outward confirmation more than inward surrender?

Practice: On this Sabbath, ask God for inward surrender before outward proof.

### Day 312 Reflection

Where do I need others to understand my growth immediately?

Practice: Let one person be confused by your growth without rushing to perform an explanation.

### Day 313 Reflection

What question from others still unsettles me?

Practice: Release one unanswered question about your life back to God before you react to it.

### Weekly Review

- How did provision become witness instead of pride?
- Where did belonging steady you in a charged place?
- What opportunity slowed down into cleaner obedience?
- How did waiting become holy timing?
- How did Sabbath rest make room for inward surrender?
- What old label lost some power?
- Which unresolved question was released back to God?
