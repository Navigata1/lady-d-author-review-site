# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## February Week 2: Filled for Daily Life

Use these pages with Days 039-045.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, meet trembling faith, govern pressured speech, align my witness with Jesus, seal my service in belonging, bring the kingdom into bound places, keep my heart tender, and tend the root beneath my visible fruit. Amen.

### Day 039 Reflection

Where am I asking for assurance because I need wisdom, and where am I delaying obedience?

Practice: Name one fear you have kept circling, then ask the Spirit for enough courage to take the next obedient step.

### Day 040 Reflection

Where do my words need the Spirit's fruit under pressure?

Practice: Before one important conversation, pause and ask the Spirit to govern both your words and your tone.

### Day 041 Reflection

What old script is my life tempted to fulfill instead of God's word?

Practice: Choose one small act that aligns your day with God's word rather than fear's old script.

### Day 042 Reflection

Where have I been serving from neediness instead of received love?

Practice: Before serving today, pause and receive your belonging to God through Jesus.

### Day 043 Reflection

Where have I made peace with bondage that Jesus came to confront?

Practice: Name one place where bondage has felt normal, and pray for the Spirit's kingdom power there before you make peace with it.

### Day 044 Reflection

Where is the Spirit asking me to stay tender instead of defensive?

Practice: Respond to one conviction quickly today: confess, repair, surrender, forgive, or seek counsel.

### Day 045 Sabbath Reflection

What fruit has been showing in my life, and what root may be feeding it?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one visible fruit and ask God what root He wants to tend beneath it.

### Week Review

- Where did trembling faith become movement?
- How did the Spirit govern my words?
- What old script lost power?
- Where did belonging reshape service?
- What bondage did I stop normalizing?
- Where did I stay tender to conviction?
- What root did Sabbath rest reveal beneath visible fruit?
