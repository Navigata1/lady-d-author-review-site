# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## April Close / May Week 1: Ordered Love, Brave Mercy, and Learning His Voice

Use these pages beside Days 116-122.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, order my loves, free me from fear's version of life, make me faithful in small mercy, and teach me Your voice. Let Sabbath rest help me hear life where finality has spoken too loudly. Amen.

### Day 116 Reflection

- Where do I need Jesus to become Lord over the way I love?
- Practice: Name one relationship where approval has become too powerful.

### Day 117 Reflection

- What am I clutching that may keep me from receiving life in Christ?
- Practice: Release one self-protective habit for one day.

### Day 118 Reflection

- What small mercy is Jesus placing within my reach?
- Practice: Offer one simple act of refreshment.

### Day 119 Reflection

- What grace have I received that Jesus may be asking me to pass along?
- Practice: Name one received mercy and give one mercy freely.

### Day 120 Reflection

- Where has fear of criticism made my mercy hesitate?
- Practice: Choose one merciful action that fear has been delaying.

### Day 121 Reflection

- Where do I need to hear Jesus' mercy before I speak judgment?
- Practice: Ask, "What need am I missing?" before judging one situation.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 122

- What place in me needs to hear Jesus speak life again this Sabbath?
- Practice: Name one place that has felt final and surrender it to Jesus.

### Week Review

- How did Jesus reorder love this week?
- Where did small mercy become holy?
- What did I learn about Christ's voice?
- What Sabbath hope should follow me into the next month?
