# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## May Close / June Week 1: Learning His Voice into Peace in the Storm

Use these pages beside Days 151-157.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, receive what feels small, correct fear's story, wake me to Your nearness, help me recognize You in the storm, hold hard stories at the table of grace, turn panic into prayer, and let Sabbath rest teach me to receive Your saving hand. Amen.

### Day 151 Reflection

- What am I withholding because it feels too small to matter?
- Practice: Name one small thing you can bring to Jesus and offer it without apology.

### Day 152 Reflection

- Where has fear been trying to interpret what only Jesus can name?
- Practice: Write down one fear-driven interpretation and answer it with one truth about Jesus.

### Day 153 Reflection

- Where do I need to wake up to Christ's nearness inside the storm?
- Practice: Ask, "Jesus, how are You coming near here?"

### Day 154 Reflection

- Where might fear be causing me to misread help as threat?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to correct your sight in one fear-distorted place.

### Day 155 Reflection

- What hard story needs to sit at the table of grace with Jesus?
- Practice: Bring one unresolved injustice to Jesus and ask what faithful courage looks like today.

### Day 156 Reflection

- What fear needs to become a direct prayer today?
- Practice: Turn one anxious sentence into a direct prayer to Jesus.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 157

- Where do I need to receive Jesus' hand before analyzing the storm?
- Practice: Place one sinking place before Jesus and receive His help.

### Week Review

- What small thing did I place in Jesus' hands?
- Which fearful interpretation did Christ correct?
- Where did panic become prayer?
- How did Sabbath rest help me receive His saving hand?
