# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## February Closeout: Filled for Daily Life

Use these pages with Days 053-059 and the February 29 bonus.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, turn burned places into wisdom, teach my desires to receive with gratitude, enlarge my prayer, help me recognize what God is preparing, meet me in limitation, restore my sight of Jesus, breathe grace and peace into my rest, and bring me back to Christ at the center. Amen.

### Day 053 Reflection

What burned place in my story needs to become wisdom instead of shame?

Practice: Name one hard lesson from a heated season, and ask the Spirit how it can become wisdom instead of shame.

### Day 054 Reflection

What desire is asking to lead me, and what would surrendered receiving look like?

Practice: Name one desire and ask whether it is a need to bring to God or a craving that is trying to lead you.

### Day 055 Reflection

Who needs to be entrusted to God's shepherding care beyond my control?

Practice: Pray for one person or group you cannot control, asking God to shepherd them wisely.

### Day 056 Reflection

Where is God asking me to recognize His preparation instead of clinging to control?

Practice: Identify one place of transition and ask God to help you recognize what He is preparing there.

### Day 057 Reflection

Where have I assumed my current limitations make me unavailable to hear from God?

Practice: Create one quiet listening moment in the actual place where you are, and ask the Spirit to make you receptive.

### Day 058 Reflection

What false or incomplete picture of Jesus needs correction in me?

Practice: Name one incomplete picture of Jesus and replace it with one truth revealed in Scripture.

### Day 059 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need to receive grace and peace before trying to understand or control everything?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause before God's throne in prayer and receive grace and peace before planning your next step.

### Bonus Day Reflection

Where do I need to receive the love of Jesus as freedom rather than mere comfort?

Practice: Name one place where you need Christ's love to free you, and receive His authority there before you act.

### February Review

- Where did daily filling change my words, burdens, or reactions?
- What craving or complaint lost authority?
- Where did I receive shared strength?
- What limitation became a listening place?
- How did Sabbath rest shape February?
- What did the Spirit show me about Jesus?
- What freedom do I want to carry into March?
