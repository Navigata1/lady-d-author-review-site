# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## October Week 3: The Cross and Daily Grace

Use these pages beside Days 284-290.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, faithful Teacher and living Way, heal my first reactions, prepare my following before crisis, give courage for entrusted capacity, unbury what fear has hidden, receive fruit with humility, make small faithfulness sacred, and let Sabbath worship return my work to You. Amen.

### Day 284 Reflection

- Where has troubled news been discipling my reactions more than Jesus has?
- Practice: When unsettling news reaches you, pause for prayer before you react or repeat it.

### Day 285 Reflection

- What readiness have I been trying to borrow instead of cultivate with Jesus?
- Practice: Choose one simple act of personal readiness: prayer, confession, obedience, reconciliation, or Scripture before delay becomes neglect.

### Day 286 Reflection

- What entrusted capacity have I minimized because it seemed small beside someone else's?
- Practice: Name one gift, responsibility, or capacity God has entrusted to you and use it faithfully in one small way.

### Day 287 Reflection

- What have I buried because fear sounded like wisdom?
- Practice: Unbury one neglected responsibility by taking the smallest faithful next action.

### Day 288 Reflection

- Where has Jesus brought growth that I can return to Him with thanks?
- Practice: Thank Jesus for one area of growth without taking ownership of the glory.

### Day 289 Reflection

- What small place of faithfulness needs to become sacred again?
- Practice: Do one small responsibility with whole-hearted faithfulness because Jesus sees it.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 290

- What work, responsibility, or result do I need to return to Jesus in Sabbath rest?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one week of work back to Jesus in worship and release the need to be defined by it.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus heal a first reaction?
- What act of readiness became personal?
- What entrusted capacity did I use faithfully?
- What buried place moved back into the light?
- How did Sabbath rest return work to worship?
