# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## March Week 1: The Comforter Near You

Use these pages with Days 060-066.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, draw near in dry places, thirst, slow fruit, hidden witness, patient endurance, listening prayer, and Sabbath waiting. Keep every experience of Your nearness submitted to Jesus, grounded in Scripture, and fruitful in love. Amen.

### Day 060 Reflection

What truth has God already opened to me that I need to stop treating as distant?

Practice: Open one passage, one prayer, or one honest confession you have been avoiding, and ask the Spirit to bring life there.

### Day 061 Reflection

Where am I thirsty, and what would it mean to come honestly instead of hiding the need?

Practice: Name one place of thirst in prayer, and come to Jesus there without performing strength.

### Day 062 Reflection

What fruit is God growing slowly in me, and where can it bring healing today?

Practice: Choose one fruit of the Spirit to practice in one specific relationship before the day ends.

### Day 063 Reflection

Where do I need the Spirit to bring my public witness and private life back together?

Practice: Name one place where your private obedience needs to match your public witness, and take one humble step there today.

### Day 064 Reflection

Where is Jesus asking me to practice patient endurance instead of anxious escape?

Practice: Choose one word of Jesus you need to keep patiently today, and write the practical obedience it requires.

### Day 065 Reflection

What might the Spirit be saying that my hurry has been drowning out?

Practice: Take five quiet minutes with God, ask what the Spirit is saying, then write one obedient response.

### Day 066 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need Jesus to be my anchor before I understand the next correction or direction?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name what you are waiting for and place it under the faithful truth of Jesus in prayer.

### Week Review

- Where did revealed truth feel nearer than before?
- What thirst did mercy help me name honestly?
- What small fruit began to carry healing?
- Where did my hidden life and public witness move closer together?
- What patient obedience became clearer?
- What did I hear when I made room to listen?
- How did Sabbath rest anchor waiting in Jesus?
