# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## October Close / November Opening

Use these pages with Days 300-306.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach me to carry what is needed without making it my identity, to speak after success with gratitude, to release old instruments, to admit thirst, to let Sabbath rest cleanse my legacy, to receive reordered attention, and to welcome provision with open hands. Form freedom, fire, and holiness under the care of Jesus. Amen.

### Day 300 Reflection

What tool, gift, or survival response am I tempted to make my name?

Practice: Name one tool or ability you use under pressure, and surrender it back to God.

### Day 301 Reflection

Where do my words after success become louder than gratitude?

Practice: After one completed task, practice gratitude before explanation.

### Day 302 Reflection

What am I still holding because it helped me survive?

Practice: Release one old survival posture that no longer belongs in this season.

### Day 303 Reflection

Where am I tired but still performing strength?

Practice: Tell God plainly where you are depleted before you try to keep producing.

### Day 304 Reflection

What pattern is my life becoming through repeated choices?

Practice: On this Sabbath, review one repeated pattern and ask God to cleanse what it is forming in you.

### Day 305 Reflection

What lost thing or unresolved concern has been occupying too much of my attention?

Practice: Name one concern that has taken too much space, and ask God to reorder it.

### Day 306 Reflection

Where am I bracing for lack instead of watching for provision?

Practice: Receive one ordinary provision with gratitude instead of rushing past it.

### Weekly Review

- What tool was surrendered back to God?
- How did gratitude reshape speech after success?
- What old instrument or posture was released?
- Where did honest thirst become prayer?
- How did Sabbath rest cleanse a repeated pattern?
- What concern was reordered under God's voice?
- What provision was received with open hands?
