# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## February Week 3: Abiding Close to Jesus in Worship and Trust

Use these pages with Days 046-052.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, keep me close enough for rescue to become worship, honest need to become prayer, waiting to become trust, hidden wounds to come into Your light, confession to become formation, hard roads to be held by cross-shaped peace, and Sabbath rest to prepare me for Your return. Amen.

### Day 046 Reflection

Where has Jesus rescued, steadied, or corrected me, and how can that mercy become worship today?

Practice: Write down one recent mercy, then turn it into a sentence of worship before making your first major decision.

### Day 047 Reflection

What burden have I been carrying privately that Jesus is inviting me to bring near Him?

Practice: Name one person, wound, or concern before Jesus in plain language, then ask for mercy without editing your heart.

### Day 048 Reflection

Where am I tempted to interpret delay as rejection, and what has Jesus already revealed about His heart?

Practice: When you feel unanswered today, pray one steady sentence: "Lord Jesus, keep me close while I wait."

### Day 049 Reflection

What need have I been hiding that Jesus is inviting me to bring into His healing light?

Practice: Choose one hidden need and place it before Jesus in prayer without minimizing or dramatizing it.

### Day 050 Reflection

What ordinary decision today needs to be shaped by who Jesus truly is?

Practice: Before one decision today, pause and ask how confessing Jesus as Christ should shape your response.

### Day 051 Reflection

Where do I need to trust Jesus' redeeming work on a road I would not have chosen?

Practice: Name one hard road before Jesus, then ask for the next surrendered step instead of demanding the whole map.

### Day 052 Sabbath Reflection

Where is grace calling me into truthful obedience as I rest under the returning Christ?

Practice: On this Sabbath, ask Jesus to show one area where grace is calling you into truthful, peaceful obedience.

### Week Review

- Where did rescue become worship?
- What honest burden came near Jesus?
- How did I wait without calling delay rejection?
- What hidden need did I bring into His healing light?
- How did confessing Jesus shape an ordinary decision?
- Where did cross-shaped peace meet a hard road?
- How did Sabbath rest prepare me to live ready, not afraid?
