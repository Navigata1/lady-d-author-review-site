# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## June Week 3: Peace in the Storm

Use these pages beside Days 165-171.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, meet me beyond familiar ground, teach me honest prayer, let hope rise where need has felt final, shape my help through abiding presence, receive my lack without panic, begin with what is truly in my hands, and make this Sabbath a school of thanksgiving and trust. Amen.

### Day 165 Reflection

- Where do I need to receive Christ's nearness beyond familiar ground?
- Practice: Name one unfamiliar place in your life and ask Jesus what He wants to teach you there.

### Day 166 Reflection

- What need have I been trying to manage instead of bringing directly to Jesus?
- Practice: Turn one real need into a direct prayer for Jesus' help.

### Day 167 Reflection

- Where do I need persistent faith without trying to control the outcome?
- Practice: Stay in prayer over one need you are tempted to abandon.

### Day 168 Reflection

- Where am I trying to help from hurry instead of abiding presence?
- Practice: Before responding to another person's need, ask Jesus to make your presence settled rather than frantic.

### Day 169 Reflection

- Where can I see the need more clearly than I can see the supply?
- Practice: Write one honest provision question, then place it before Jesus without panic.

### Day 170 Reflection

- What actual loaves do I need to place before Jesus without apology?
- Practice: Make an honest inventory of what is needed, what is available, and what must be surrendered.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 171

- Where is Jesus inviting me to give thanks before I can see enough?
- Practice: Give thanks for one surrendered provision before you can see how Jesus will use it.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus meet me beyond familiar ground?
- What need became honest prayer?
- Where did lack lose its authority over my peace?
- How did Sabbath thanksgiving reshape my sense of enough?
