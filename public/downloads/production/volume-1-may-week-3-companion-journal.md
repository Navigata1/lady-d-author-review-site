# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## May Week 3: Love That Moves Into Action

Use these pages beside Days 130-136.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, find me before false words name me, meet me with grace before performance measures me, refine shame through mercy, receive the cost of hidden faithfulness, teach my words to comfort with strength, give me courage for humble field steps, and let Saturday Sabbath rest make me attentive to Your unforsaken kindness. Amen.

---

### Day 130 Reflection

**Focus:** False voices tested before God.

**Write:** What voice has been trying to name me before the Father does?

**Practice:** Write down one voice or sentence you need to test before God instead of carrying it as truth.

### Day 131 Reflection

**Focus:** Grace before impossible outcomes.

**Write:** Where am I trying to perform a future only God can hold?

**Practice:** Name one outcome you cannot manufacture, then ask God for the next faithful action inside that limit.

### Day 132 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy noticing the outsider.

**Write:** Where do I still act as if I am unseen even when mercy is making room?

**Practice:** Receive one kindness without arguing against your need for it, then notice one person who may feel unseen.

### Day 133 Reflection

**Focus:** Costly faithfulness seen by God.

**Write:** What costly obedience do I need to let the Father see without minimizing it?

**Practice:** Name one costly act of faithfulness God has seen, and let gratitude replace the need to prove it.

### Day 134 Reflection

**Focus:** Words that help courage breathe.

**Write:** Where can my words help courage breathe instead of making fear louder?

**Practice:** Before one conversation, ask God to make your words comforting, truthful, and strong enough to reach the heart.

### Day 135 Reflection

**Focus:** Humble field steps.

**Write:** What small field step is God inviting me to take without controlling the outcome?

**Practice:** Take one humble practical step toward provision, reconciliation, service, or obedience without demanding the full outcome first.

### Day 136 Reflection

**Focus:** Saturday Sabbath and unforsaken kindness.

**Write:** What evidence of unforsaken kindness do I need to notice before I move into the next week?

**Practice:** In Sabbath rest, name one evidence that God's kindness has not stopped, then let that mercy shape how you enter the coming week.

---

### Week Review

1. Which false voice did I test before God this week?
2. Where did grace meet me before performance could measure me?
3. How did mercy help me receive being noticed?
4. What costly faithfulness did I let the Father see?
5. Where did my words comfort or strengthen someone else?
6. What humble field step did I take without controlling the outcome?
7. How did Saturday Sabbath rest help me recognize God's unforsaken kindness?
