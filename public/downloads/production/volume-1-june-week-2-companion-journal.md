# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## June Week 2: Restored by the Father's Heart

Use these pages beside Days 158-164.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, speak mercy into the depths, bless restored life, teach me Your patient rhythm, send me from love, form me through covenant mercy, anchor me in Your welcome, and let Saturday Sabbath restore beloved identity in Your presence. Amen.

---

### Day 158 Reflection

**Focus:** Restoring compassion in deep places.

**Write:** Where do I need the Father to speak restoring compassion over a deep or unnamed place in me?

**Practice:** Name one deep place you have been avoiding, and ask the Father to speak mercy there without shame.

### Day 159 Reflection

**Focus:** Blessing that holds restored life.

**Write:** What promise from the Father do I need to breathe under today instead of living under fear's curse?

**Practice:** Speak one blessing aloud over the restored life God is growing in you, then take one small obedient step that agrees with it.

### Day 160 Reflection

**Focus:** The Father's patient completion.

**Write:** What part of my restoration do I need to entrust to the Father's timing instead of forcing it to finish today?

**Practice:** At the close of the day, write one thing God helped you complete and one thing you are entrusting back to Him.

### Day 161 Reflection

**Focus:** Love that sends into ordinary obedience.

**Write:** Where is the Father calling life out of the real ground of my life today?

**Practice:** Identify one ordinary place where God is calling forth life, and offer one concrete act of obedience there.

### Day 162 Reflection

**Focus:** Covenant mercy that forms without shame.

**Write:** Where am I resisting the Father's formation because I am afraid of being corrected or known?

**Practice:** Ask God to show you one place where you are resisting His formation, then yield through one honest act of trust.

### Day 163 Reflection

**Focus:** Identity before responsibility.

**Write:** Where have I been trying to earn welcome through responsibility instead of receiving identity from the Father?

**Practice:** Before handling one responsibility today, pause and say: I receive identity before assignment.

### Day 164 Reflection

**Focus:** Saturday Sabbath and beloved identity.

**Write:** What false name do I need to lay down so I can receive beloved identity from the Creator?

**Practice:** Keep a Sabbath pause from self-measuring, and honor one other person as an image-bearer through a specific act of tenderness or respect.

---

### Week Review

1. Where did mercy speak into a place I had avoided?
2. What blessing did I breathe under instead of fear's curse?
3. What did I complete, and what did I entrust back to the Father?
4. Where did love send me into ordinary obedience?
5. How did covenant mercy form me without shame?
6. Where did I receive identity before responsibility?
7. How did Saturday Sabbath help me lay down a false name and receive beloved identity?
