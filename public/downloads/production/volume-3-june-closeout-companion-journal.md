# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## June Closeout: Gifts for the Body

Use these pages with Days 180-181.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, close this month by turning renewed obedience into shared witness and confirming belonging beyond human control. Free my gifts from isolation, rivalry, suspicion, and narrow expectation. Keep every gift submitted to Jesus and useful to the body. Amen.

### Day 180 Reflection

Where does obedience need to replace repeated explanation?

Practice: Take one practical step that moves a burden from private strain into obedient shared service.

### Day 181 Reflection

Where have I assumed God can only work through the expected person, place, or method?

Practice: Bless one faithful gift in someone else without needing to control where or how God is using it.

### June Review

- What burden moved from private strain into shared service?
- What correction did I receive as mercy?
- Where did desire come back under God?
- What practical limitation became prayer?
- What scarcity thought lost authority?
- Whose gift can I bless without rivalry?
- How did the Spirit make gifts for the body feel more humble, useful, and submitted to Jesus?
