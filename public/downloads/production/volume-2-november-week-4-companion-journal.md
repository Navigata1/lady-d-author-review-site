# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## November Week 4: Resurrection Hope

Use these pages beside Days 326-332.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, living Shepherd, keep me from anxious rehearsal, make me available to the Father's Spirit, meet close relational pain with compassion, form endurance without bitterness, free me from fear of hidden things, bring private formation into humble witness, and let Sabbath rest reorder fear under holy reverence. Amen.

### Day 326 Reflection

- What conversation or pressure am I rehearsing in fear instead of entrusting to Jesus?
- Practice: Name one conversation you are over-rehearsing in fear and ask Jesus for peace before words.

### Day 327 Reflection

- Where do I need to move from self-reliance into Spirit-help?
- Practice: Before one demanding task, pause and ask the Father for Spirit-help instead of relying only on your own force.

### Day 328 Reflection

- What close wound needs the honest compassion of Jesus today?
- Practice: Tell Jesus the truth about one relational wound without minimizing it or using it as permission to become cruel.

### Day 329 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to make opposition louder than belonging to Jesus?
- Practice: Release the need to control one person's reaction and choose one faithful response that honors Jesus.

### Day 330 Reflection

- What hidden concern do I need to entrust to the God who sees clearly?
- Practice: Stop feeding one fear about what you cannot see, and choose the next truthful act you can see.

### Day 331 Reflection

- What truth has Jesus been forming privately that needs humble public obedience?
- Practice: Share one simple truth or encouragement that Jesus has been forming in you, without making it a performance.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 332

- What fear of people needs to be reordered before God in Sabbath worship?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one fear of people into worship and ask God to restore holy reverence in its place.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus quiet anxious rehearsal?
- How did Spirit-help meet a demanding moment?
- What relational wound did I bring into Christ's compassion?
- Where did endurance remain peaceful instead of bitter?
- How did Sabbath rest reorder fear under holy reverence?
