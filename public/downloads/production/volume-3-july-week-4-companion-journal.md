# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## July Week 4: Rain Down on Me

Use these pages with Days 203-209.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, rain on dry places with shepherding care, accountable calling, consecrated transfer, Sabbath counsel, clear obedience, freedom to bless succession, and formation for untested faith. Keep every transition submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 203 Reflection

Who needs shepherding prayer in this transition?

Practice: Pray specifically for the people who need guidance in a transition you are facing.

### Day 204 Reflection

Where does calling need healthy confirmation rather than private pressure?

Practice: Invite prayerful counsel or confirmation around one responsibility you are carrying.

### Day 205 Reflection

Where do I need to share honor so others can step forward well?

Practice: Bless or support one person who needs courage for a responsibility they are stepping into.

### Day 206 Reflection

Where have I been moving faster than counsel or prayer?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause one decision long enough to pray, listen, and seek wise counsel before moving.

### Day 207 Reflection

What clear obedience have I delayed?

Practice: Do one clear thing God has already shown you to do.

### Day 208 Reflection

Where do I need freedom to bless someone else's next step?

Practice: Encourage or pray for someone stepping into responsibility, without comparison.

### Day 209 Reflection

Where does my faith need practice instead of theory?

Practice: Identify one area where faith needs practice, then choose a small act of obedience there.

### Weekly Review

- Who did I pray for as a shepherding act?
- Where did calling need healthy confirmation?
- What honor needed to be shared?
- How did Sabbath counsel change my pace?
- What delayed obedience became clear?
- Where did I bless succession without fear?
- What untested place needs practiced faith?
