# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## May Week 5: Love That Moves Into Action

Use these pages beside Days 144-150.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, help me trust mercy in the morning, receive space inside pressure, let love become formed action, gather what is scattered, protect the seeds of grace, bear fruit without comparison, and enter Saturday Sabbath rhythm as a child sent from rest. Amen.

---

### Day 144 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy that separates light from darkness.

**Write:** What has God been separating in me so His light can be honored clearly?

**Practice:** Name one thing God is showing you as light, and name one dark pattern that no longer gets to stand beside it as if it belongs.

### Day 145 Reflection

**Focus:** God-created space inside pressure.

**Write:** Where do I need God to create space before I try to force an answer?

**Practice:** Create one small expanse in your day: ten minutes of quiet, one honest pause before replying, or one boundary that gives your heart room to listen.

### Day 146 Reflection

**Focus:** Love becoming formed obedience.

**Write:** What loving action have I left suspended because fear kept asking for more delay?

**Practice:** Turn one faithful intention into a formed action before the day ends.

### Day 147 Reflection

**Focus:** Restoring compassion that gathers the scattered soul.

**Write:** What scattered place in me needs the Father's restoring compassion before I act?

**Practice:** Gather what is scattered by writing one clear next step, then do that step before adding more to the list.

### Day 148 Reflection

**Focus:** Seed-bearing promise.

**Write:** What small seed of grace have I been tempted to overlook because it is not yet harvest?

**Practice:** Identify one small seed of grace God has already planted, and give it one practical act of care today.

### Day 149 Reflection

**Focus:** Fruitfulness without comparison.

**Write:** Where have I been comparing my fruit instead of answering God's word in my own field?

**Practice:** Do one faithful thing that belongs to your actual calling today, without measuring it against someone else's assignment.

### Day 150 Reflection

**Focus:** Saturday Sabbath rhythm before sending.

**Write:** Where do I need Sabbath rhythm to purify my action from anxious urgency or self-importance?

**Practice:** Mark this Saturday Sabbath with one clear act of rest and one clear act of worship, trusting God with what remains unfinished.

---

### Week Review

1. What light did God help me honor more clearly?
2. Where did God create space inside pressure?
3. What intention became formed obedience?
4. What scattered place did compassion gather?
5. What seed of grace did I protect or water?
6. How did I resist comparison and answer God's word in my own field?
7. How did Saturday Sabbath rhythm shape the way I moved into action?
