# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## January Week 4: The Invitation to Follow into Revealed Peace

Use these pages with Days 022-028.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, reveal Yourself where fear has made me restless. Anchor my hope in Your promise, let grace be light for my Sabbath rest, restore what suffering has shaken, make hidden service whole under Your gaze, call me near before You send me, and teach me to carry peace without control. Amen.

### Day 022 Reflection

Where is Jesus inviting me to obey from revealed light instead of waiting for full control?

Practice: Name one uncertain place, then choose one faithful action that fits the light Jesus has already given.

### Day 023 Reflection

How would my choices today change if I began from the promise that Jesus is coming?

Practice: Before reacting to pressure, pause and pray, "Jesus, keep my hope anchored in Your promise."

### Day 024 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need the grace of Jesus to become light instead of only a word I know?

Practice: On this Sabbath, receive grace before planning more work. Write one sentence that begins, "The grace of Jesus is enough for..."

### Day 025 Reflection

Where is God restoring, confirming, strengthening, or establishing me right now?

Practice: Name one area where you need restoration, then take one gentle step that agrees with God's strengthening work.

### Day 026 Reflection

Where do I need to trust that Jesus sees service others may overlook?

Practice: Do one hidden act of faithful service for Jesus without asking it to prove your value.

### Day 027 Reflection

Where have I been trying to serve without first coming near to Jesus?

Practice: Spend a quiet moment with Jesus before beginning one responsibility, asking Him to make your obedience flow from nearness.

### Day 028 Reflection

What room, relationship, or conversation needs me to carry peace without forcing an outcome?

Practice: Before entering one conversation or room, pray for the peace of Christ to rest on your words and posture.

### Week Review

- Where did revealed light become obedience?
- How did the promise of Jesus' coming steady me?
- What did Sabbath grace expose, quiet, or restore?
- Where did God strengthen what suffering had shaken?
- What hidden service did I place under Jesus' gaze?
- How did nearness change my assignment?
- Where did I carry peace without trying to control the response?
