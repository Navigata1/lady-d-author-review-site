# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## January Closeout: The Promise of the Spirit

Use these pages with Days 029-031.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, refresh what is depleted, give courage for truthful repair, and govern every fire in me with the love and belonging of God. Close this first month with honesty, mercy, restraint, and surrendered trust. Amen.

### Day 029 Reflection

Where do I need to receive refreshment instead of pretending I am not tired?

Practice: Name one area of depletion and ask God for refreshment before you try to keep producing.

### Day 030 Reflection

Where am I offering a substitute when truth, apology, or wise repair is needed?

Practice: Identify one place where substitution has replaced truth, and take one clean step toward honesty, repair, or wise counsel.

### Day 031 Sabbath Reflection

What fire in me needs to be governed by belonging before it spreads?

Practice: On this Sabbath, surrender one retaliatory thought to God and choose a non-burning response: prayer, silence, counsel, repair, or a wise boundary.

### January Review

- Where did the Spirit meet me in ordinary work?
- What motive, impulse, or fire was refined?
- How did Sabbath rest shape my January?
- Where did the Spirit form fruit instead of pressure?
- What hidden or unformed place did I entrust to God?
- Where did I receive refreshment instead of pretending strength?
- What revenge story, fear story, or pressure story lost power?
