# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## January Week 1: The Promise of the Spirit

Use these pages with Days 001-007.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, awaken me to Your nearness in ordinary work, hidden motives, dry places, surrendered service, inner rooms, tense moments, and corrective mercy. Fill me with wisdom, humility, peace, prayer, and belonging that leads me closer to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 001 Reflection

Where have I treated ordinary work as if it were outside the Spirit's care?

Practice: Name one ordinary task that needs Spirit-given wisdom, and begin it with prayer instead of pressure.

### Day 002 Reflection

What motive beneath my work needs the Spirit's refining fire today?

Practice: Before beginning one important task, ask the Spirit to refine your motive and show you the next faithful action.

### Day 003 Sabbath Reflection

What has been binding my obedience, and how can I receive God's freedom without turning it into pride?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one binding pressure and ask the Spirit to bring deliverance with humility, not self-display.

### Day 004 Reflection

Where do I need to come near to Jesus before trying to serve in His name?

Practice: Before serving or speaking in one important setting, pause and ask Jesus to shape your authority with humility and mercy.

### Day 005 Reflection

Where have I tried to manage myself outwardly while keeping an inner room unfilled?

Practice: Choose one inner room of your life and invite the Spirit to fill it with truth, peace, and surrendered obedience.

### Day 006 Reflection

What complaint can become intercession in me today?

Practice: Turn one complaint into intercession before you repeat it to anyone else.

### Day 007 Reflection

Where is Jesus correcting me as an act of mercy rather than rejection?

Practice: Ask the Spirit to show you one place where Christ's correction is actually mercy, then respond with one honest act of repentance or obedience.

### Week Review

- Where did the Spirit meet my ordinary work with wisdom?
- What motive was refined without shame?
- What did Sabbath rest help me bring honestly to God?
- Where did nearness to Jesus reshape service?
- Which inner room needs continued filling?
- What complaint became prayer?
- What correction did I begin to receive as mercy?
