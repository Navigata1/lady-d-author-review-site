# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## March Week 4: Humble Readiness and Steady Hope

Use these pages beside Days 081-087.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, form me under Your Lordship. Keep my heart humble, truthful, hopeful, and awake. Teach me to walk through ordinary days with steady peace, holy discernment, and Sabbath rest. Amen.

### Day 081 Reflection

- Where has a lesser voice been shaping my worth, ambition, or response?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to form one conversation before you enter it.

### Day 082 Reflection

- What truth have I been tempted to resist because it exposes something tender?
- Practice: Receive one correction without defending yourself immediately.

### Day 083 Reflection

- What person, place, or situation am I grieving with Jesus?
- Practice: Pray over it with open hands and release the outcome to God.

### Day 084 Reflection

- Where do I need Jesus to heal superiority, status anxiety, or the need to be right?
- Practice: Listen to someone as a fellow learner today.

### Day 085 Reflection

- What visible security has been carrying too much of my peace?
- Practice: Thank God for that blessing, then confess Jesus as the deeper foundation.

### Day 086 Reflection

- What fear-driven voice has been shaping how I think about the future?
- Practice: Pause before repeating alarming information and ask whether it leads to faithful readiness.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 087

- How is Sabbath rest waking my heart to Jesus without fear?
- Practice: Set aside one distraction and give Jesus an undivided window of worship, listening, and surrender.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus invite humility?
- Where did His light expose something for healing?
- What became more peaceful when it was placed under His Lordship?
- What one practice should I carry into the next week?
