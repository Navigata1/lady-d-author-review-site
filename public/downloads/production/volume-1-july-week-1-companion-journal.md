# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## July Week 1: Trusting Love in Hard Seasons

Use these pages with Days 182-188.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to trust Your love in hard seasons. Help me receive provision with honesty, correction with humility, Sabbath rest with reverence, and unanswered longing with courage. Amen.

### Day 182 Reflection

Where have I received help, favor, or relief that God is asking me to examine honestly?

Practice: Ask whether love requires gratitude, repentance, repair, or all three.

### Day 183 Reflection

Where might God's disruption actually be mercy calling me back to truth?

Practice: Notice one interruption today and ask what truth God may be bringing into the light.

### Day 184 Reflection

What correction might be God's mercy sending me out of a compromised place?

Practice: Take one humble step without defending the old fear.

### Day 185 Sabbath Reflection

Where has God mercifully led me out, and what faithful response belongs to that mercy?

Practice: Pause on this seventh-day Sabbath and let rest become truthful, grateful obedience.

### Day 186 Reflection

What has God revealed about Himself that needs to become the first truth of my day?

Practice: Complete this sentence: "God has revealed Himself to me as..."

### Day 187 Reflection

What honest question have I been afraid to bring to God because I thought it made my faith look weak?

Practice: Pray the question plainly before trying to solve it.

### Day 188 Reflection

What specific lack or delay do I need to name before God instead of carrying it silently?

Practice: Write the sentence and add, "Father, meet me here with Your truth."

### Week Review

- Where did God invite me to tell the truth more clearly?
- What did Sabbath rest teach me about mercy and obedience?
- Which disappointment needs language instead of silence?
- Where did I see God protect, correct, or welcome me?
- What is one concrete act of trust I will carry into next week?
