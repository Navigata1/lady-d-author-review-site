# Filled with the Holy Spirit — 365-Day Structure

**Subtitle:** A 365-Day Devotional Journey of Power, Comfort, and Fire
**Focus:** Holy Spirit / filling, comfort, conviction, gifts, fruit, rain, oil, breath

**Format locked from meeting:** month + day labels, no weekdays; February 29 included as a leap-year/bonus day; each daily manuscript page should be 4–5 paragraphs + prayer + journal prompt with mature-eye readable type.
