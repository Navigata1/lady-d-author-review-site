# Filled with the Holy Spirit — Scripture Architecture

**Subtitle:** A 365-Day Devotional Journey of Power, Comfort, and Fire
**Focus:** Holy Spirit, filling, comfort, conviction, fruit, gifts, prayer, breath, rain, oil, fire, and empowered witness

## Editorial Standard
- Scripture references only here; final translation remains Lady D + permissions review.
- No scripture reference may appear more than 3 times in the volume.
- Day titles do not repeat within the same month.
- Original-language/context lenses are matched by testament and theological fit, not used as decoration.
- Each day is designed to become a full, mature-eye-readable print page with depth, prayer, and journal prompt.

## Title Options
- **Filled with the Holy Spirit** — 8.8/10: Clear and biblically direct; familiar but strong.
- **Rain Down on Me** — 8.6/10: Worshipful and memorable; connects to meeting language.
- **Sealed and Filled** — 8.2/10: Compact and theological; good series title.
- **Breath, Fire, and Oil** — 8.0/10: Distinctive and symbolic; more poetic than straightforward.
- **Walking in the Spirit’s Power** — 7.7/10: Practical and clear; less unique.
