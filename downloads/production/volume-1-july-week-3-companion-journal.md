# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## July Week 3: Trusting Love in Hard Seasons

Use these pages with Days 196-202.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to trust Your covenant love in the guarded place, the dark place, the long-timing place, and the place where grace acts before I can perform. Let Sabbath rest hold my beloved identity, and let mercy form my morning. Amen.

### Day 196 Reflection

What has been circling the place of obedience God asked me to prepare?

Practice: Name one distraction, fear, or habit that has been feeding on your obedience, then remove or resist it today.

### Day 197 Reflection

Where have I mistaken fear for evidence that God has left me?

Practice: When fear rises, pause and speak one honest sentence to God before reacting.

### Day 198 Reflection

What truth have I been avoiding because I thought peace required denial?

Practice: Name one difficult reality without exaggeration or denial, then place it under God's faithful knowledge of the whole road.

### Day 199 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need to remember that God sees what has pressed on me and still calls me beloved?

Practice: On this seventh-day Sabbath, release one pressure to prove yourself and rest in the Redeemer who sees truthfully.

### Day 200 Reflection

Where is God asking me to trust His faithful timing instead of demanding a faster timeline?

Practice: Identify one place where you have called God late, then ask what faithful obedience belongs to today.

### Day 201 Reflection

What am I trying to secure by performance that God is asking me to receive by grace?

Practice: Surrender one outcome back to God's covenant faithfulness.

### Day 202 Reflection

How would I move through today if I received God's covenant faithfulness before reacting to pressure?

Practice: Write one sentence that begins, "Because God is faithful, I can..."

### Week Review

- What did I guard because God asked me to prepare it?
- How did I bring fear into God's presence instead of letting fear interpret Him?
- Where did truth become peace instead of denial?
- How did Sabbath rest protect beloved identity?
- What outcome did I surrender from performance back to grace?
