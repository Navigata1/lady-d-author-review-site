# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## April Week 3: Living Water and Humble Witness

Use these pages beside Days 102-108.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, lead me in Your light, invite me to living water, steady me in Your whole truth, and teach me Sabbath humility. Let Your healing presence make my witness gentle, awake, and full of hope. Amen.

### Day 102 Reflection

- What pressure has been acting like it gets the first and last word?
- Practice: Name it before the God who holds beginning and ending.

### Day 103 Reflection

- Where do I need endurance as companionship with Jesus?
- Practice: Encourage one person who is enduring something hard.

### Day 104 Reflection

- Where am I thirsty?
- Practice: Tell Jesus plainly where you need living water.

### Day 105 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to edit or avoid God's truth?
- Practice: Receive one hard word with grace instead of fear.

### Day 106 Reflection

- What wounded place needs the light of Christ's promised healing?
- Practice: Choose one action that previews restoration.

### Day 107 Reflection

- Where do I need Christ's light to wake faithfulness?
- Practice: Take one awake and faithful step.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 108

- What outcome do I need to release into Christ's care this Sabbath?
- Practice: Rest from proving, forcing, and managing what belongs to Jesus.

### Week Review

- Where did God's eternal faithfulness steady me?
- How did living water meet honest thirst?
- What truth did I receive instead of trimming it away?
- What Sabbath humility should I carry into the next week?
