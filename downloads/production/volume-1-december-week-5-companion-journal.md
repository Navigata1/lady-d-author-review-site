# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## December Week 5: Daily Surrender at the Mountain of Belonging

Use these pages with Days 357-363.

### Week Prayer

Father, bring my heart to the mountain of belonging with trust. Teach me to receive quiet provision, hear Your redeeming call, wait before the mountain, rest in Sabbath covenant mercy, obey from treasured welcome, carry beloved identity, and answer Your love with a renewed yes. Amen.

### Day 357 Reflection

What quiet provision have I rushed past without letting it strengthen my heart?

Practice: Name one quiet provision God has given, then let gratitude shape one conversation or decision before noon.

### Day 358 Reflection

Where have I mistaken an in-between place for abandonment?

Practice: Before answering one demand, pause and ask, "Father, what are You calling me to receive before I respond?"

### Day 359 Reflection

What holy pause might God be using to prepare my heart for encounter?

Practice: Identify one place where you feel paused, then ask God what He may be preparing in that pause.

### Day 360 Sabbath Reflection

Where have I turned obedience into a test of whether I am worth carrying?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one place where you have been trying to prove your worth, and rest in the truth that God carries before you perform.

### Day 361 Reflection

Where do I need to hear God's call as welcome instead of rejection waiting to happen?

Practice: Choose one concrete act of listening obedience that flows from being treasured, not from trying to earn love.

### Day 362 Reflection

Where has service become heavy because I forgot beloved identity?

Practice: Ask how beloved identity should shape one ordinary interaction today, then act from belonging instead of performance.

### Day 363 Reflection

Where is God inviting a renewed yes from me, and what fear has been guarding that place?

Practice: Name one guarded place where God is inviting a renewed yes, then answer from grace instead of fear.

### Week Review

- What quiet provision strengthened my heart?
- Where did I hear the Father's call in an in-between place?
- What pause began to look like preparation?
- What did Sabbath covenant mercy help me stop proving?
- Which act of obedience flowed from treasured welcome?
- Where did beloved identity make service lighter?
- What renewed yes rose from grace instead of fear?
