# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## December Week 1: Walking as His Witness

Use these pages beside Days 335-341.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, Light of my path and faithful Lord, steady me in the Father's care, make my allegiance visible with humility, heal the places where fear makes me hide, give me peace with integrity, bring complicated relationships under Your Lordship, teach me holy hospitality, and free me to honor Your work in others. Amen.

### Day 335 Reflection

- What fear-shaped decision needs to be answered by the Father's care?
- Practice: Before a fear-shaped decision, pause and ask, What step would I take if I believed the Father values me?

### Day 336 Reflection

- Where is Jesus inviting my private faith to become humble visible obedience?
- Practice: Choose one ordinary setting where you will acknowledge Jesus through words, tone, or obedience.

### Day 337 Reflection

- Where has fear tempted me to hide the One who rescued me?
- Practice: Pray before noon about one place where fear tempts you to hide your allegiance to Jesus.

### Day 338 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to trade integrity for false peace?
- Practice: In one tense relationship, choose gentleness with integrity instead of either silence from fear or speech from anger.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 339

- What complicated relationship needs to be placed under the Lordship of Jesus in Sabbath rest?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, place one complicated relationship under the Lordship of Jesus before you try to manage it.

### Day 340 Reflection

- Who have I been treating as an interruption who may need Christlike reception?
- Practice: Practice one act of attentive hospitality: receive, listen, or honor someone without treating them as an interruption.

### Day 341 Reflection

- Whose faithfulness do I need to honor without comparison?
- Practice: Encourage or honor one faithful person without comparison, envy, or calculation.

### Week Review

- Where did the Father's care steady my witness?
- Where did private faith become visible obedience?
- How did Jesus give me peace with integrity?
- How did Sabbath rest place a complicated relationship under Christ's Lordship?
- Where did hospitality or honor become part of my witness?
