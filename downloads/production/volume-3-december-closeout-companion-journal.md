# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## December Closeout: Living Sealed and Filled

Use these pages with Days 363-365.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, close this devotional year by forming costly obedience, surrendered life, and fruitful welcome in me. Refresh the dry place where following Jesus feels costly, anoint today with trust that releases self-protection, and fill my welcome with love that points beyond me to Jesus and the Father who sent Him. Let this year end with a heart more yielded, more honest, and more available to You. Amen.

### Day 363 Reflection

Where has comfort been negotiating with obedience?

Practice: Name one costly obedience Jesus is asking for and take one faithful step toward it.

### Day 364 Reflection

Where has self-protection been shrinking my obedience?

Practice: Release one self-protective habit and choose one surrendered act of trust.

### Day 365 Reflection

Does my welcome point people toward Jesus or back toward myself?

Practice: Offer one act of welcome that points beyond you toward the love of Jesus.

### Volume 3 Closeout Review

- Where did the Spirit refresh costly obedience?
- What self-protection was released into surrendered life?
- What welcome became more transparent to Jesus?
- What theme from <em>Filled with the Holy Spirit</em> needs to continue into the next season?
- What prayer from this volume do I want to carry forward?
