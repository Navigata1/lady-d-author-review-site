# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## February Week 1: Filled for Daily Life

Use these pages with Days 032-038.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, fill my daily life with restraint, consecrated steps, wise freedom, awakened obedience, honest offering, holy confirmation, and courage that begins by receiving from God. Amen.

### Day 032 Reflection

Where is my response in danger of becoming bigger than the wound?

Practice: Before responding to one heated situation, wait long enough to pray, seek wisdom, and choose a non-destructive next step.

### Day 033 Reflection

What pain pattern am I tempted to pass forward?

Practice: Name one blame pattern you will not pass forward, and choose a truthful, merciful next step instead.

### Day 034 Reflection

Where do I need Spirit-given wisdom more than self-rescue?

Practice: Identify one pressured place and ask the Spirit for wisdom before making your next decision there.

### Day 035 Reflection

What has God already shown me that I have been slow to obey?

Practice: Name one instruction from God you have delayed, and take one concrete step of obedience today.

### Day 036 Reflection

What small offering can I bring while my faith is still learning courage?

Practice: Bring one small obedient offering to God from the place where you still feel uncertain.

### Day 037 Reflection

What have I placed before God but kept trying to take back?

Practice: Place one fear, plan, or offering before God without taking it back through overcontrol.

### Day 038 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need to be clothed by the Spirit before I try to act brave?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one dry place where courage is thin, and ask the Spirit to clothe you before you act.

### Week Review

- Where did I stop retaliation from becoming my response?
- What pain pattern did I refuse to pass forward?
- Where did Spirit-given wisdom help me stop self-rescuing?
- What delayed obedience became clearer?
- What offering did I bring from hesitant faith?
- Where did God confirm surrender?
- How did Sabbath rest help me receive courage before acting?
