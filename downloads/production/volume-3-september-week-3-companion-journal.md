# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## September Week 3: Power to Witness

Use these pages with Days 258-264.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form power to witness through peace after fear, worship before proving, consecrated service close to home, true worship built on surrendered ground, Sabbath belonging, renewed faithfulness after obedience becomes visible, and hope while questions rise. Keep every witness submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 258 Reflection

What fear needs to be met by God's peace before I move?

Practice: Name one fear before God, then receive His peace before making your next decision.

### Day 259 Reflection

Where does worry need to be renamed by worship?

Practice: Create one small act of worship before you answer the loudest pressure of the day.

### Day 260 Reflection

What misplaced trust needs to be surrendered or reordered?

Practice: Identify one misplaced trust and take one practical step to put God first there.

### Day 261 Reflection

What holy obedience needs to be built where fear once stood?

Practice: Replace one surrendered habit with one clear act of worship or obedience.

### Day 262 Reflection

Where has fear made me think trembling disqualifies obedience?

Practice: On this Sabbath, rest in God's claim over you, then obey one clear step without pretending you feel fearless.

### Day 263 Reflection

Where am I trying to control reactions before I obey?

Practice: Release one reaction you cannot control and entrust your obedience to God.

### Day 264 Reflection

Where do I need hope while questions are still unanswered?

Practice: When a question or accusation rises, pause before reacting and ask the Spirit for settled courage.

### Weekly Review

- Which fear did God's peace meet?
- How did worship rename worry?
- What misplaced trust did God ask me to surrender?
- What true worship was built on surrendered ground?
- How did Sabbath belonging quiet performance?
- What reaction did I entrust to God?
- Where did hope rise while questions remained unresolved?
