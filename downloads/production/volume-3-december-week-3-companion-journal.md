# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## December Week 3: Living Sealed and Filled

Use these pages with Days 349-355.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach me to live sealed and filled as Jesus forms humility, identity, courage, mercy, reverence, quiet witness, and rest in belonging. Keep me near the Teacher, fill me with Jesus' identity when I am misnamed, breathe courage into hidden fear, anoint my words with mercy, make Sabbath reverence stronger than lesser fears, root my witness in the Father's care, and let being fully known become peace. Amen.

### Day 349 Reflection

Where have I been trying to rise above the path Jesus is using to form me?

Practice: Choose one ordinary act of teachability before chasing visible importance.

### Day 350 Reflection

Where has a wrong label started shaping how I see myself?

Practice: Name one false label before God and refuse to let it become your identity.

### Day 351 Reflection

What hidden fear has been drying my courage?

Practice: Tell God the hidden fear by name, then take one small obedient step anyway.

### Day 352 Reflection

What truth has God been forming privately that now needs a faithful voice?

Practice: Speak one formed truth with humility instead of keeping it buried in fear.

### Day 353 Reflection

What lesser fear has been louder than holy reverence?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one lesser fear and place it under reverence for God.

### Day 354 Reflection

Where have I felt too small to matter?

Practice: Offer one small faithful act to God without needing it to be noticed by people.

### Day 355 Reflection

Where do I feel unseen even while God knows me completely?

Practice: Pause once today and receive God's detailed care over the part of you that feels unseen.

### Weekly Review

- What ordinary teachability brought me nearer to Jesus?
- What false label lost authority?
- What hidden fear was named before God?
- What private truth became merciful witness?
- How did Sabbath reverence reorder lesser fears?
- What small faithful act was offered quietly?
- Where did detailed care become belonging?
