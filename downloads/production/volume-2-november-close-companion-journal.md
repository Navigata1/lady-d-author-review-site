# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## November Close: Resurrection Hope

Use these pages beside Days 333-334.

### Closeout Prayer

Jesus, compassionate Lord and faithful Teacher, teach me the Father's careful attention. Let resurrection hope lift courage in overlooked places and quiet the fear of being forgotten. Amen.

### Day 333 Reflection

- What small concern have I dismissed that the Father still sees with care?
- Practice: Bring one small, dismissed concern to the Father and receive His attention as care, not criticism.

### Day 334 Reflection

- Where do I need God's intimate knowledge to quiet my need to prove I am not forgotten?
- Practice: Before explaining yourself today, pause and let God's intimate knowledge quiet the fear of being forgotten.

### November Resurrection Hope Review

- Where did Jesus turn fear into steadier courage?
- What place of pressure became a place of witness?
- Where did God's compassion meet something I thought was too small to matter?
- What truth from November do I need to carry into December's witness arc?
