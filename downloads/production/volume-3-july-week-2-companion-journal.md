# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## July Week 2: Rain Down on Me

Use these pages with Days 189-195.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, rain on dry places with reverent courage, fruitful honesty, settled requests, Sabbath humility, teachable listening, hope in the waiting, and mercy that becomes guidance. Keep my petitions submitted to Jesus and my courage tender. Amen.

### Day 189 Reflection

What need requires reverent courage in the right place?

Practice: Identify one need that should be brought into the right place with humility, clarity, and prayer.

### Day 190 Reflection

Where does my story need clarity without bitterness?

Practice: Write one sentence of truth about your situation without exaggeration, denial, or accusation.

### Day 191 Reflection

What clear request needs to replace a vague ache?

Practice: Turn one vague ache into a clear, prayerful request.

### Day 192 Reflection

What matter needs to be brought before the Lord before I push for an answer?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one unresolved matter before God before pressing for the next human answer.

### Day 193 Reflection

Am I prepared to receive God's answer, or only to present my request?

Practice: After presenting a request, sit quietly and ask God to make your heart teachable.

### Day 194 Reflection

Where do I need to trust God to affirm what is right in His time?

Practice: Thank God for one place where truth, justice, or belonging has been affirmed, even if the process took time.

### Day 195 Reflection

What mercy in my life could become guidance for someone else's journey?

Practice: Identify one mercy, lesson, or answer that could help someone else walk with more clarity.

### Weekly Review

- What need came into the light with reverence?
- What truth became clearer without bitterness?
- What request became settled and specific?
- How did Sabbath humility change the pace of waiting?
- Where did listening become part of prayer?
- What right thing needs patience until God affirms it?
- What mercy can become guidance for someone else?
