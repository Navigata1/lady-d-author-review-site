# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## December Week 2: Walking as His Witness

Use these pages beside Days 342-348.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, saving Lord and Prince of Peace, focus my witness where You send me, let Your nearness shape my words, free me from anxious accumulation, move me toward practical mercy, teach me Sabbath compassion, restore hidden shame, and keep me whole when goodness is resisted. Amen.

### Day 342 Reflection

- Where is Jesus focusing my witness instead of asking me to carry everything?
- Practice: Name one specific person, place, or responsibility Jesus has actually placed before you today, and serve there faithfully.

### Day 343 Reflection

- Where does my day need the nearness of God's kingdom more than the pressure of my worry?
- Practice: Speak or act from kingdom nearness in one ordinary moment: bring calm, mercy, truth, or hope where pressure expects fear.

### Day 344 Reflection

- What am I trying to gather before I obey what Jesus has already shown me?
- Practice: Identify one extra layer of control you are using to delay obedience, and take the faithful step Jesus has already made clear.

### Day 345 Reflection

- Where have I been defending a position while missing a person who needs mercy?
- Practice: Notice one person or situation that needs practical mercy, and take one concrete step toward help.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 346

- What act of restful goodness would honor God and care for a real need this Sabbath?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, practice one act of restful goodness that honors God and cares for a real need.

### Day 347 Reflection

- What withered place have I been hiding from Jesus' restoring word?
- Practice: Bring one hidden limitation or shame to Jesus in prayer, and ask for one obedient step toward wholeness.

### Day 348 Reflection

- Where do I need to stop regretting a faithful act of mercy just because it was misunderstood?
- Practice: Remember one faithful good you should not regret simply because it was misunderstood.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus focus my witness?
- How did kingdom nearness change my tone?
- What extra control did I release?
- How did Sabbath rest and mercy belong together?
- Where did Jesus keep me whole when goodness was resisted?
