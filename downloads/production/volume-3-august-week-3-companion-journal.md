# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## August Week 3: Oil for the Waiting Season

Use these pages with Days 227-233.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, give oil for the waiting season: Sabbath belonging under God's word, prayer that becomes obedience, sober courage in hard stories, wise closure, settled patience under misunderstanding, breath for opened truth, and mercy to move when You make a way. Keep every response submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 227 Reflection

Where do I need to remember that I belong to God before I face pressure?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause before pressure and remember that you belong to God first.

### Day 228 Reflection

Which prayer is God asking me to live out in action?

Practice: Choose one prayer that now requires a faithful action and take the next humble step.

### Day 229 Reflection

Where does my heart need to be purified from reaction or spectacle?

Practice: Refuse to feed one reactive appetite, and choose a sober act of prayer instead.

### Day 230 Reflection

What completed responsibility do I need to release back to God?

Practice: Close one completed responsibility with prayer instead of replaying it all day.

### Day 231 Reflection

Where do I feel pressured to explain what is not ready to be explained?

Practice: Name one place where you feel misunderstood, then choose a patient and truthful response.

### Day 232 Reflection

What truth have I been afraid to face?

Practice: Face one delayed truth with prayer, counsel, or one honest next step.

### Day 233 Reflection

Where is God opening a narrow but faithful way forward?

Practice: Identify one open window for faithful movement and take it with prayerful humility.

### Weekly Review

- Where did Sabbath belonging steady me?
- Which prayer needed obedient movement?
- What reactive appetite needed refining?
- What responsibility needed closure?
- Where did misunderstanding require patience?
- What truth opened and required breath?
- What narrow way forward did mercy make visible?
