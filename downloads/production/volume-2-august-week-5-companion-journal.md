# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## August Week 5: The Surrendered Heart

Use these pages beside Days 235-241.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, trusted Lord and true Light, teach me to recognize Your mercy, receive Your correction, follow You into fruitfulness, honor humble obedience, trust You with hard things, and rest in Your authority. Let this Sabbath become a place where truth heals instead of frightens me. Amen.

### Day 235 Reflection

- Where have I been slow to celebrate the wonderful things Jesus is doing?
- Practice: Name one humble or unexpected place where Jesus may be working, and thank Him before critiquing it.

### Day 236 Reflection

- What reaction in me needs the light of Jesus before it becomes a decision?
- Practice: Pause before one irritation and ask what Scripture has already revealed about Jesus' welcome.

### Day 237 Reflection

- Where do I need Jesus to grow real fruit beneath visible activity?
- Practice: Choose one visible habit and ask Jesus to deepen its hidden root.

### Day 238 Reflection

- What small obedience may be preparing room for Jesus to enter?
- Practice: Complete one simple act of obedience you have been postponing.

### Day 239 Reflection

- What faithful step would agree with the prayer I keep praying?
- Practice: Pray specifically about one hard thing, then take one faithful step that agrees with that prayer.

### Day 240 Reflection

- Where am I waiting for approval when Jesus has already made obedience clear?
- Practice: Take one obedient step you have delayed because you wanted approval first.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 241

- What question is Jesus asking beneath the question I keep bringing Him?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, sit quietly with Jesus and ask what motive He wants to bring into the light.

### Week Review

- Where did I recognize mercy instead of resisting it?
- What fear did Jesus expose gently?
- What fruit did He invite beneath the leaves?
- What humble obedience prepared room for Him?
- What faithful step moved with my prayer?
- How did Sabbath light make honesty feel safe?
