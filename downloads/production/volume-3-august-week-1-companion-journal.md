# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## August Week 1: Oil for the Waiting Season

Use these pages with Days 213-219.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, give oil for the waiting season: renewed fruit in mixed surroundings, purified allegiance, consecrated memory, honest prayer, mercy for the honest cry, rest received well, and freedom guarded after relief. Keep my waiting submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 213 Reflection

What influence has been shaping my heart quietly?

Practice: On this Sabbath, notice one influence shaping your heart, and bring it under God's care before the day continues.

### Day 214 Reflection

Where has closeness become compromise?

Practice: Identify one relationship, habit, or influence that needs a clearer boundary for faithful witness.

### Day 215 Reflection

What mercy do I need to remember before my loyalty drifts?

Practice: Recall one specific mercy of God and let it correct one misplaced loyalty.

### Day 216 Reflection

Where do I need honest prayer instead of excuses?

Practice: Confess one area honestly to God without minimizing or collapsing into despair.

### Day 217 Reflection

Where do I need to stop coping and start calling on God?

Practice: Turn one hidden struggle into a direct prayer for God's help.

### Day 218 Reflection

How do I handle relief after God has helped me?

Practice: Thank God for one relief or mercy, then choose one way to steward it faithfully.

### Day 219 Reflection

What old cycle is trying to re-form after relief?

Practice: Name one early sign of an old cycle and choose a concrete act of surrender today.

### Weekly Review

- What influence did Sabbath rest reveal?
- Where did compromise need a boundary?
- What mercy helped me remember God?
- What excuse became honest prayer?
- What hidden struggle became a cry for help?
- How did I steward relief?
- What cycle needs daily surrender?
