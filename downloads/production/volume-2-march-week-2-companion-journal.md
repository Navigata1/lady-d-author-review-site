# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## March Week 2: The Way in Ordinary Days Through Mercy and Availability

Use these pages with Days 067-073.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, lead me through ordinary movement with mercy, loosen attachments that compete with Your call, teach me servant greatness, restore my sight with compassion, free me from comparison, make my faith fruitful, and turn Sabbath rest into open-handed availability. Amen.

### Day 067 Reflection

Where might Jesus be asking me to notice need while I am simply on the way?

Practice: During one ordinary errand or transition, ask Jesus to help you notice one person with mercy.

### Day 068 Reflection

What attachment makes following Jesus feel costly or difficult right now?

Practice: Practice one small act of generosity or simplicity that loosens your grip on control.

### Day 069 Reflection

Where is Jesus inviting me to serve from love rather than from the need to be seen?

Practice: Choose one act of service today as a response to Jesus' love, not as a way to be noticed.

### Day 070 Reflection

Where do I need Jesus to restore vision that pain or weariness has narrowed?

Practice: Ask Jesus to restore your sight in one area where pain, fear, or weariness has narrowed your vision.

### Day 071 Reflection

Where does comparison make another person's grace feel threatening to me?

Practice: Bless one person who receives attention, help, or opportunity without letting comparison own your response.

### Day 072 Reflection

Where might I have leaves without fruit, and what honest step is Jesus asking of me?

Practice: Ask Jesus to show one place where appearance needs to become real fruit, then take one honest step.

### Day 073 Sabbath Reflection

What am I holding tightly that Jesus may be inviting me to make available?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one resource, gift, or piece of time you can make available to Jesus without anxious grasping.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus help ordinary movement become merciful?
- What attachment loosened through generosity or simplicity?
- How did service become a response to love?
- Where did Jesus restore narrowed vision?
- How did comparison loosen its grip?
- What appearance began becoming real fruit?
- How did Sabbath rest make my hands more available to Jesus?
