Day 001 Reflection

Daily Reflection

Where have I treated ordinary work as if it were outside the Spirit's care?

Practice: Name one ordinary task that needs Spirit-given wisdom, and begin it with prayer instead of pressure.

Day 002 Reflection

Daily Reflection

What motive beneath my work needs the Spirit's refining fire today?

Practice: Before beginning one important task, ask the Spirit to refine your motive and show you the next faithful action.

Day 059 Sabbath Reflection

Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need to receive grace and peace before trying to understand or control everything?

Practice: On this Sabbath, pause before God's throne in prayer and receive grace and peace before planning your next step.

Bonus Day Reflection

Daily Reflection

Where do I need to receive the love of Jesus as freedom rather than mere comfort?

Practice: Name one place where you need Christ's love to free you, and receive His authority there before you act.

Day 060 Reflection

Daily Reflection

What truth has God already opened to me that I need to stop treating as distant?

Practice: Open one passage, one prayer, or one honest confession you have been avoiding, and ask the Spirit to bring life there.

Day 364 Reflection

Daily Reflection

Where has self-protection been shrinking my obedience?

Practice: Release one self-protective habit and choose one surrendered act of trust.

Day 365 Reflection

Daily Reflection

Does my welcome point people toward Jesus or back toward myself?

Practice: Offer one act of welcome that points beyond you toward the love of Jesus.
