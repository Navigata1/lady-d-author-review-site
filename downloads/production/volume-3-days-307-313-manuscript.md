**Production lens correction:** The architecture assigned `mashach`; the production lens uses Saul receiving provision on the road after anointing. Power to witness is framed as set-apart receiving that does not confuse provision with ownership.

Power to witness is not only strength to speak; it is also holiness in how we receive. Freedom, fire, and holiness teach the heart to accept provision without grabbing, boasting, or acting as if every gift exists to enlarge the self.

Trust power to witness by asking how you respond when God provides through ordinary hands. Let the lesson move from the biblical context into that hidden pressure point and back into prayerful surrender.
