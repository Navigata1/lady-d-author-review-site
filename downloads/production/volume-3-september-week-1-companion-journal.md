# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## September Week 1: Power to Witness

Use these pages with Days 244-250.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, form power to witness through honest need, consecrated cries, truthful mercy, remembered deliverance, Sabbath faithfulness in hidden places, courage spoken by God's presence, and honest questions held inside belonging. Keep every witness submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 244 Reflection

Where do I need to cry out honestly instead of pretending?

Practice: Name one place of need plainly before God, without dressing it up.

### Day 245 Reflection

What pressure needs to be named before God today?

Practice: Pray specifically about one pressure, then ask what surrendered response belongs to today.

### Day 246 Reflection

What truth may be God's mercy before visible rescue?

Practice: Receive one truth God is using to correct, steady, or remind you.

### Day 247 Reflection

What deliverance do I need to remember today?

Practice: Write down one past deliverance and carry it into one fearful responsibility today.

### Day 248 Reflection

Where is God growing fruit in a hidden place?

Practice: On this Sabbath, honor one hidden act of obedience without needing it to be seen by everyone.

### Day 249 Reflection

What fear-name needs to be replaced by God's truth?

Practice: Replace one fear-name you have accepted with one truth-name God speaks over His children.

### Day 250 Reflection

What hard question do I need to bring to God instead of hiding?

Practice: Write one hard question as a prayer, then end it with a sentence of trust.

### Weekly Review

- Where did need become honest prayer?
- What cry needed surrender?
- What truth arrived before relief?
- Which past deliverance gave courage?
- What hidden faithfulness mattered on Sabbath?
- What fear-name was challenged?
- What honest question stayed inside belonging?
