# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## November Week 2: Resurrection Hope

Use these pages beside Days 312-318.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, faithful Keeper and risen Overcomer, teach me to remain with You under pressure, hold fast to what grace has given, receive my identity under Your name, open the hidden room of my heart, bring weakness near Your victory, return to received truth, and rest at the table of grace without bitterness. Amen.

### Day 312 Reflection

- Where do I need to believe that Jesus is keeping me while I endure?
- Practice: Name one pressure you are under and ask Jesus to keep your obedience tender instead of tense.

### Day 313 Reflection

- What has Jesus given me that I need to hold fast today?
- Practice: Identify one good thing Jesus has given you to guard, and refuse one distraction that weakens it.

### Day 314 Reflection

- Where do I need God's name over me to become stronger than insecurity?
- Practice: Before one decision, pause and say, I belong to God; then choose from that place.

### Day 315 Reflection

- Where is Jesus asking for welcome instead of polite distance?
- Practice: Spend two quiet minutes naming the room in your heart where Jesus is knocking, then welcome Him there.

### Day 316 Reflection

- What weakness needs to be brought near to Christ's victory?
- Practice: Bring one weakness to Jesus and ask Him for courage that comes from His victory, not your image.

### Day 317 Reflection

- What truth have I received that needs to be remembered and practiced again?
- Practice: Remember one truth God has already given you, and practice it in one concrete choice today.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 318

- What misjudgment do I need to place in Christ's hands instead of carrying as a weapon?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, release one need to defend your worth and ask Jesus to keep your heart tender while He holds the truth.

### Week Review

- Where did Christ keep my heart alive under pressure?
- What did I guard because grace had given it to me?
- How did God's name over me change a decision?
- Where did opening the door to Jesus become nourishment?
- How did Sabbath rest release bitterness and return me to grace?
