# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## February Week 1: Abiding Close to Jesus in Focused Trust

Use these pages with Days 032-038.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me to abide close to You. Focus my assignment, steady me under scrutiny, keep my heart tender to the Spirit, join mercy and obedience in me, root Your word deeply, keep me teachable, and receive my grief in Sabbath nearness. Amen.

### Day 032 Reflection

What burden have I been carrying that Jesus has not assigned to me today?

Practice: Name one thing Jesus has actually assigned for today, and release one burden He has not asked you to carry right now.

### Day 033 Reflection

Where has fear of being misunderstood made me anxious in my walk with Jesus?

Practice: Before defending yourself inwardly, ask Jesus to settle your heart under His merciful Lordship.

### Day 034 Reflection

Where is the Spirit inviting me to stay soft, honest, and responsive?

Practice: Pray slowly, "Holy Spirit, keep my heart tender to truth," then obey one conviction you already know is from God.

### Day 035 Reflection

Where does Jesus want to join mercy and obedience more deeply in me?

Practice: Ask Jesus to show one way your practice of rest or obedience can become more merciful and more surrendered to Him.

### Day 036 Reflection

What tends to snatch God's word from my attention before it can take root?

Practice: Choose one sentence from today's reading and repeat it in prayer three times before moving into the next task.

### Day 037 Reflection

What lesson has Jesus been repeating because He loves me enough to form me?

Practice: Write one lesson Jesus has been repeating in your life, then choose one simple way to practice it today.

### Day 038 Sabbath Reflection

What grief, loss, or unfinished sorrow do I need to tell Jesus about honestly?

Practice: On this Sabbath, tell Jesus one grief plainly, without editing it into something more spiritual-sounding.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus focus my assignment?
- How did His Lordship steady me under scrutiny?
- What conviction did I respond to with tenderness?
- Where did mercy and obedience come closer together?
- What word from Jesus did I guard from hurry?
- How did I practice teachability?
- What grief did Sabbath nearness help me bring to Christ?
