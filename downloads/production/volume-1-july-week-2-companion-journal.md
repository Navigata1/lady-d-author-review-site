# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## July Week 2: Trusting Love in Hard Seasons

Use these pages with Days 189-195.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to trust Your promise in the places that still feel unfinished. Let hope rise before performance, let faith receive Your righteousness, and let Sabbath rest remind me of Your faithful leading. Amen.

### Day 189 Reflection

Where has fear been building a substitute plan because I am tired of waiting?

Practice: Name the substitute, then ask what promise or truth should interrupt it.

### Day 190 Reflection

Where has my view of the future become smaller than God's promise?

Practice: Pause near a window or outside and ask God to widen your vision.

### Day 191 Reflection

Where is God inviting me to trust His promise before I can see the outcome?

Practice: Write one revealed truth from God and answer it with one sentence of trust.

### Day 192 Sabbath Reflection

What past faithfulness do I need to remember so fear does not define my future?

Practice: On this seventh-day Sabbath, list three ways God has led you before and rest your next concern under that remembrance.

### Day 193 Reflection

What assurance am I asking for, and what deeper need is underneath that question?

Practice: Bring the question to God without turning it into a demand for control.

### Day 194 Reflection

What one concrete preparation may God be asking me to make today?

Practice: Complete one faithful preparation without demanding the whole map first.

### Day 195 Reflection

Where is God asking me to prepare faithfully while I wait for Him to act?

Practice: Turn one spiritual insight into a prepared place: schedule it, set it out, write it down, or take the first practical action.

### Week Review

- What substitute plan did God interrupt with hope?
- Where did God widen my vision?
- How did Sabbath rest help me remember His leading?
- What question did I bring to God more honestly?
- What careful preparation became obedience this week?
