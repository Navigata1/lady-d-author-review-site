# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## Leap Day and March Week 1: Beloved Identity into Love in the Timing of Struggle

Use these pages beside the February 29 bonus entry and Days 060-066.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, let the extra day become grace, teach me Your way in struggle, mark me by Your presence instead of performance, meet me by name, make room for seeking, redeem what fear clutches, call me back where I have drifted, and anchor me in Your revealed character. Amen.

### Leap Day Reflection

**Focus:** Grace for the extra day.

**Write:** Where do I need to receive the Father's knowing before I carry the next responsibility?

**Practice:** Name one responsibility you have been carrying as if you were unknown, then ask the Father to meet you there by name.

### Day 060 Reflection

**Focus:** Knowing God's way in the timing of struggle.

**Write:** What struggle needs the Father's way, not only my preferred timing?

**Practice:** Ask God to show you His way before asking Him to change the timing.

### Day 061 Reflection

**Focus:** Presence before performance.

**Write:** Where am I trying to appear strong instead of asking for God's presence?

**Practice:** Pause before one pressure point and ask, "Father, go with me here."

### Day 062 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy that meets me by name.

**Write:** What would change if I began this struggle from being known by the Father?

**Practice:** Before checking demands, take one minute to breathe and receive God's nearness.

### Day 063 Reflection

**Focus:** A place to seek God.

**Write:** What ordinary place can become a place of seeking God in this season?

**Practice:** Choose one chair, walk, journal page, or Sabbath pause as today's meeting place.

### Day 064 Reflection

**Focus:** Redeemed belonging instead of fear-management.

**Write:** What part of my life am I treating as self-owned when the Father is calling it redeemed?

**Practice:** Offer one part of the day back to God as a redeemed gift.

### Day 065 Reflection

**Focus:** Returning before distance becomes normal.

**Write:** Where is God calling me to return before distance becomes normal?

**Practice:** Take one humble step back toward prayer, truth, worship, counsel, or repair.

### Day 066 Reflection

**Focus:** God's revealed character as the anchor.

**Write:** Which part of God's revealed character needs to answer my fear today?

**Practice:** Write one word from God's character and carry it through the day.

### Week 1 Review

1. Where did being known by God change how I carried responsibility?
2. What did I learn about God's way in struggle?
3. Where did presence become more important than appearance?
4. What did I return to before distance hardened?
5. Which word from God's character became an anchor?
