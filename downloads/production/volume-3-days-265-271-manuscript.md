## Day 265 - September 22

### Carry Oil for the Journey

**Scripture Reference:** Judges 6:30

**Production lens correction:** The architecture assigned `shemen`; the production lens uses the public anger after Gideon's obedience. Oil for the journey is framed as consecrated courage when surrender is misunderstood and opposed.

Judges 6:30 shows the cost of visible obedience. The men of the city demand an answer for what has been torn down.

Oil for the journey is needed after obedience becomes costly. Power to witness is not only the courage to act in secret; it is the consecrated steadiness to keep belonging to God when people react. The Spirit anoints the next step when opposition tries to make fear the whole story.

Carry oil for the journey by asking where criticism has made you question obedience that God already clarified. The Spirit can keep your heart soft without letting it become ruled by intimidation.

Let prayer turn insight into obedience before noon. Consecrated courage does not need to become harsh in order to stand.

The Spirit gives power to witness by anointing courage when obedience is opposed.

**Today step:** Name one place where obedience may be misunderstood, then ask God for courage without bitterness.

**Prayer:** Holy Spirit, anoint my courage for the journey. Keep me steady, gentle, and faithful when obedience is opposed. Amen.

**Journal prompt:** Where has criticism made me question obedience God already clarified?

**Morning impact:** Let the Spirit carry Carry Oil for the Journey into one faithful step today.
