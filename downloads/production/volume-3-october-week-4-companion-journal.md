# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## October Week 4: The Spirit and Prayer

Use these pages with Days 293-299.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach prayer in the place where injury, reaction, fear, explanation, belonging, restraint, and limitation all need to be brought under Jesus. Send rain into dry places, cleanse what has been exposed, let peace lead, breathe truthful witness, seal belonging, strengthen restraint, and form grace in the waiting. Amen.

### Day 293 Reflection

Where has anger become the weather I expect to live under?

Practice: Before repeating the story of who hurt you, ask God what surrender would look like now.

### Day 294 Reflection

What reaction still needs cleansing instead of hiding?

Practice: Name one reaction honestly before God and receive both mercy and correction.

### Day 295 Reflection

What voice is directing my next response: fear, pressure, or God?

Practice: Before responding to pressure, pause and name the voice you are obeying.

### Day 296 Reflection

Where am I explaining myself from defensiveness instead of prayer?

Practice: Give one explanation today with humility, clarity, and no self-protective exaggeration.

### Day 297 Reflection

Where has fear trained me to call captivity wisdom?

Practice: On this Sabbath, sit quietly before God and name one fear you have accepted as normal.

### Day 298 Reflection

Where does restraint feel like rejection to me?

Practice: When you feel restrained, ask God for one clean next step instead of forcing the whole outcome.

### Day 299 Reflection

Where am I moving forward but still feeling constrained?

Practice: Name one limitation without resentment, then ask God how to be faithful inside it today.

### Weekly Review

- Where did prayer interrupt revenge?
- What reaction received both mercy and correction?
- How did peace lead under pressure?
- What explanation became more truthful and humble?
- How did Sabbath rest seal belonging before fear defined normal?
- Where did restraint become a place of strength?
- What limitation became a place for grace to breathe?
