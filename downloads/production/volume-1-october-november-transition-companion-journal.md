# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## October/November Transition: Called by Love to Eternal Hope

Use these pages with Days 301-307.

### Week Prayer

Father, carry me from called by love into eternal hope. Comfort what feels exposed, move mercy into practical care, loosen what fear clutches, teach Sabbath rest to receive provision, cover shame with grace, protect the road from old accusation, and revive desire where grief has been long. Amen.

### Day 301 Reflection

What part of my story needs God's comfort more than my hiding?

Practice: Name one part of your story that still feels too complicated to bring into God's light, then ask Him for comfort without shame.

### Day 302 Reflection

What practical step of care is God asking me to receive or take?

Practice: Identify one practical step of care God is placing in front of you, then take it without reducing mercy to emotion.

### Day 303 Reflection

What am I holding for security that God is asking me to hold with open hands?

Practice: Name one thing you are clutching for security, then ask God whether He is inviting you to hold it with open hands.

### Day 304 Sabbath Reflection

Where do I need Sabbath rest to help me receive care instead of performing self-sufficiency?

Practice: On this Sabbath, receive one form of practical care without apologizing for needing help.

### Day 305 Reflection

Where is comparison making it hard for me to receive the grace God is giving?

Practice: Notice one place where comparison is stealing your ability to receive grace, then thank God for the portion He is giving you.

### Day 306 Reflection

What old quarrel or accusation do I need to refuse on the road ahead?

Practice: Refuse one old quarrel, accusation, or rehearsed complaint that would poison a road God is trying to redeem.

### Day 307 Reflection

What desire is God gently reviving after grief or delay made it feel unsafe?

Practice: Name one desire grief or delay has made you afraid to feel, then place it before God without forcing the outcome.

### Week Review

- What hidden or complicated part of my story did I bring into God's light?
- Where did mercy become practical care?
- What security did I begin holding with open hands?
- How did Sabbath rest help me receive provision or care?
- Where did grace free me from comparison?
- What old accusation did I refuse on the road?
- What desire began to live again under eternal hope?
