# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## October Week 3: Called by Love

Use these pages with Days 287-293.

### Week Prayer

Father, call me by love through mystery, a changed walk, remembered mercy, Sabbath truth, honest grief, nearness with truth, and peace that preserves life. Let Your promise, shalom, holy memory, covenant mercy, welcome, beloved identity, and restoring love form me this week. Amen.

### Day 287 Reflection

Where am I demanding explanation before I receive the blessing God is already giving?

Practice: Name one unanswered question, then ask God for the blessing, steadiness, and obedience you need while you wait for fuller understanding.

### Day 288 Reflection

Where do I need to stop despising a transformed pace?

Practice: Notice one place where God has changed your pace, then choose one faithful step that honors the change instead of resenting it.

### Day 289 Reflection

What remembered mercy needs to shape my next decision?

Practice: Write one remembered mercy from your story, then name how it should shape one decision today.

### Day 290 Sabbath Reflection

What long-held tension needs private Sabbath prayer before public action?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one long-held tension before God in private prayer before you decide what needs to be said publicly.

### Day 291 Reflection

What emotion have I been editing that needs the Father's welcome?

Practice: Give God ten honest minutes with an emotion you have been editing, explaining, or hiding.

### Day 292 Reflection

What truth needs honesty and compassion instead of avoidance or punishment?

Practice: Name one truth you have been avoiding, then ask God how to hold it with both honesty and compassion.

### Day 293 Reflection

Where is God asking me to move from regret into repentance, repair, release, or trust?

Practice: Bring one regret to God and ask whether He is calling you toward repentance, repair, release, or a renewed trust in His preserving work.

### Week Review

- Where did God bless me before every question was answered?
- What transformed pace did I begin to honor?
- What remembered mercy shaped a decision?
- How did Sabbath prayer protect a tender truth?
- What emotion did I stop editing in the Father's presence?
- Where did truth and compassion stand together?
- What regret began moving toward repentance, repair, release, or trust?
