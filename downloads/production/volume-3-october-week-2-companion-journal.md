# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## October Week 2: The Spirit and Prayer

Use these pages with Days 279-285.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach prayer through cleansed conversations, discernment over curiosity, honesty about hidden sweetness, set-apart purpose in public moments, Sabbath fruit under group pressure, cleansed speech, and belonging that is stronger than proving. Keep every word and motive submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 279 Reflection

What are my conversations confirming in me?

Practice: Before one important conversation, ask the Spirit to cleanse your motives and steady your words.

### Day 280 Reflection

Where has curiosity been louder than wisdom?

Practice: Pause before revisiting one old place, memory, or temptation, and ask God for discernment.

### Day 281 Reflection

What have I been enjoying without wanting to explain?

Practice: Tell God the whole truth about one pleasure, habit, or source of comfort.

### Day 282 Reflection

Where am I being swept along by what everyone expects?

Practice: Before one gathering or public responsibility, ask God to set your motives apart.

### Day 283 Reflection

What kind of person do I become when others are watching?

Practice: On this Sabbath, notice one group influence around you and ask the Spirit for courage, tenderness, and clarity.

### Day 284 Reflection

Where has cleverness become a shield for me?

Practice: Before making a point, ask whether your words are serving truth or trying to win.

### Day 285 Reflection

What am I trying to prove, and what would belonging say instead?

Practice: Name one thing you are trying to prove, then rest your identity in God's claim over you.

### Weekly Review

- Which conversation needed cleaner motives?
- Where did discernment become louder than curiosity?
- What hidden sweetness did I bring into honest prayer?
- How did God set apart a public moment?
- How did Sabbath rest form fruit under group pressure?
- Where did clean speech matter more than winning?
- How did belonging answer the need to prove?
