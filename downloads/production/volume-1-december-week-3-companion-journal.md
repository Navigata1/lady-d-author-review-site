# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## December Week 3: Daily Surrender Under the Reign of Faithful Love

Use these pages with Days 343-349.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to surrender daily under Your unmatched holiness, restoring compassion, faithful promise, Sabbath covenant love, chosen love that sends, redeeming mercy that plants, and eternal reign. Let what You have revealed become stronger than fear, pressure, and urgency. Amen.

### Day 343 Reflection

What fear needs to be brought under the truth of who God has revealed Himself to be?

Practice: Name one fear, then speak one truth about God's character before you decide your next step.

### Day 344 Reflection

Where do I need God's faithfulness to restore safety, order, or breathing room today?

Practice: Before entering one demanding space, pause and ask the Father to restore steadiness before you speak or act.

### Day 345 Reflection

What outside reaction most often pulls my heart away from steady surrender?

Practice: Identify one outside reaction that has been steering your heart, then choose one action shaped by God's promise instead.

### Day 346 Sabbath Reflection

What fear around me have I been carrying as if it belonged inside my heart?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one fear you have borrowed from the atmosphere around you and lay it before the Father.

### Day 347 Reflection

What obedient step have I delayed because I wanted fear to disappear first?

Practice: Before noon, take one small act of obedience you have delayed while waiting to feel completely unafraid.

### Day 348 Reflection

What desire may need refining before it can be planted in God's love?

Practice: Ask the Father what desire He wants to refine so that something more rooted, holy, and whole can grow.

### Day 349 Reflection

What temporary pressure have I been treating as if it had final authority?

Practice: Before responding to one urgent pressure, pause and ask what changes when you remember that the Lord's reign outlasts it.

### Week Review

- What fear became smaller as God's character became clearer?
- Where did restoring compassion give me steadiness?
- Which outside reaction lost authority over my heart?
- What borrowed fear did Sabbath rest help me lay down?
- What obedience moved before fear fully disappeared?
- What desire did redeeming mercy begin to refine?
- What urgency changed under the truth of God's faithful reign?
