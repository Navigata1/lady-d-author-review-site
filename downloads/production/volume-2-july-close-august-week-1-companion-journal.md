# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## July Close / August Opening

Use these pages beside Days 207-213.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, lead me through transition, form faithfulness where commitment feels costly, make my life welcoming to the vulnerable, purify my motives, give mercy a concrete road, meet the attachments that make surrender sorrowful, and let this Sabbath open my heart to saving grace. Keep my next step simple, honest, and close to You. Amen.

### Day 207 Reflection

- What transition is asking me to follow Jesus with fresh trust?
- Practice: Name one transition and ask Jesus what faithful following looks like there.

### Day 208 Reflection

- Where is Jesus asking me to receive holy wisdom instead of reacting defensively?
- Practice: Ask Jesus where a faithful calling is forming you through discomfort.

### Day 209 Reflection

- Who might Jesus be asking me to welcome instead of treat as an interruption?
- Practice: Make room for one person who might normally feel inconvenient.

### Day 210 Reflection

- Where might obedience be mixed with image, fear, or control?
- Practice: Before one act of obedience, ask Jesus to purify the motive behind it.

### Day 211 Reflection

- Where does love need a clearer practice today?
- Practice: Choose one relationship and practice one clear act of honor, truth, or protection.

### Day 212 Reflection

- What attachment makes surrender feel costly right now?
- Practice: Name one attachment that makes surrender sorrowful and ask Jesus to meet you there.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 213

- Where am I still trying to manage what only God can save, heal, or make whole?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, release one illusion of self-sufficiency and receive God's saving mercy again.

### Week Review

- Where did transition become an invitation to follow?
- How did Jesus form faithfulness, welcome, or motive?
- What attachment did Jesus gently expose?
- How did Sabbath rest open the surrendered heart to grace?
