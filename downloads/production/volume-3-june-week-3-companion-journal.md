# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## June Week 3: Gifts for the Body

Use these pages with Days 166-172.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, make ordinary provision useful in my hands, let hope rise through daily mercy, turn burdens into intercession, strengthen honest belonging, renew caregiving places, teach love to listen when I cannot supply everything, and help me receive shared mercy. Amen.

### Day 166 Reflection

Where have I rejected the ordinary shape of today's gift?

Practice: Prepare one ordinary provision with gratitude instead of resentment.

### Day 167 Reflection

Where has cynicism made morning mercy look small?

Practice: Begin one ordinary moment by naming a mercy that arrived before you earned it.

### Day 168 Reflection

What grief or burden have I been carrying without surrender?

Practice: Take one burden you have heard or carried and pray it honestly before reacting.

### Day 169 Reflection

Where has frustration tempted me to detach from the body?

Practice: Tell God one hard truth about belonging, then ask Him for strength to love wisely.

### Day 170 Reflection

Where has care become too heavy for my private strength?

Practice: Name one caregiving burden before God and ask for help, wisdom, or shared support.

### Day 171 Reflection

Where am I trying to supply what only God can give?

Practice: On this Sabbath, release one need you cannot meet into God's care before taking your next faithful step.

### Day 172 Reflection

Where do I need to admit that I cannot carry this alone?

Practice: Ask for one specific kind of help, prayer, counsel, or shared responsibility.

### Weekly Review

- What ordinary provision became useful through gratitude?
- Where did hope rise from daily mercy?
- What burden became intercession?
- How did honest prayer protect belonging?
- Where did caregiving receive renewal?
- What did Sabbath rest release from savior-pressure?
- What burden needs to be shared?
