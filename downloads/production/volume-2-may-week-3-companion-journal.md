# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## May Week 3: Learning His Voice

Use these pages beside Days 130-136.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, teach me in ordinary places, keep my questions close, make my heart responsive to the light You have given, soften what has grown guarded, pull the thorns from my trust, form watchful courage, and let Sabbath rest protect small kingdom beginnings. Amen.

### Day 130 Reflection

- Where do I need to believe Jesus can teach me right where I am?
- Practice: Sit still for three minutes and ask Jesus to teach you where you are.

### Day 131 Reflection

- What question do I need to bring near Jesus instead of carrying alone?
- Practice: Bring one unanswered question to Jesus without pulling away.

### Day 132 Reflection

- What light have I received that now needs a living response?
- Practice: Take one response that honors what Jesus has already shown you.

### Day 133 Reflection

- Where might Jesus be inviting my guarded heart to come home?
- Practice: Remain in prayer long enough to listen over one guarded place.

### Day 134 Reflection

- What care or false security is trying to choke the word in my life?
- Practice: Name one thorn and surrender it before it crowds out obedience.

### Day 135 Reflection

- Where do I need watchfulness without fear and patience without denial?
- Practice: Choose one calm faithful action instead of a fear-driven reaction.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 136

- What small beginning needs Sabbath hope instead of impatience?
- Practice: Bless one small beginning in prayer instead of dismissing it.

### Week Review

- Where did Jesus teach me in an ordinary place?
- What question stayed close enough to become formation?
- Which thorn became clearer this week?
- How did Sabbath rest help me honor small kingdom growth?
