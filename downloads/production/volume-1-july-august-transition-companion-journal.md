# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## July/August Transition: Trusting Love in Hard Seasons to Love in Relationships

Use these pages with Days 210-216.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to live from covenant belonging as July closes and August begins. Let Your welcome, mercy, obedience, Sabbath rest, patient reminders, generational faithfulness, and restoring compassion shape my relationships with visible love. Amen.

### Day 210 Reflection

Where do I need to receive God's covenant welcome before trying to manage the future?

Practice: Before noon, choose one action that agrees with the truth that God is God to you.

### Day 211 Reflection

What unsettled place has been trying to define me, and what has God revealed that speaks a truer identity?

Practice: Name one unsettled place, then write one sentence of beloved identity over it.

### Day 212 Reflection

What act of obedience would feel like covenant response instead of performance today?

Practice: Choose one relational, concrete act of covenant keeping.

### Day 213 Sabbath Reflection

What quiet sign of God's mercy should become more visible in my relationships?

Practice: On this seventh-day Sabbath, choose one relationship practice that makes mercy visible without needing attention.

### Day 214 Reflection

What covenant reminder helps me remember whose I am when pressure rises?

Practice: Restore one small covenant reminder before worry shapes your first response.

### Day 215 Reflection

What pattern am I helping carry forward, and what small act of faithfulness can strengthen it today?

Practice: Strengthen one generational or household witness through a small faithful action.

### Day 216 Reflection

Who near me needs to be seen with restoring compassion instead of treated as background?

Practice: Notice one overlooked person close to daily life, then practice restoring compassion in a concrete way.

### Week Review

- Where did I live from covenant welcome instead of anxious control?
- What unsettled place received a truer identity?
- How did Sabbath rest make mercy more visible in my relationships?
- What reminder helped me remember whose I am?
- Who became less overlooked because compassion awakened?
