# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## February Week 2: Beloved Identity

Use these pages beside Days 039-045.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, teach me to seek You honestly, receive mercy before accusation, breathe under Your rule, guard my soul, remember Your gifts, do what is right and good, and live as one You are bringing into belonging. Amen.

### Day 039 Reflection

**Focus:** Seeking God without performance.

**Write:** What part of my seeking has been performance instead of honest return?

**Practice:** Give God five honest minutes without editing your heart.

### Day 040 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy speaking before accusation.

**Write:** Where have I confused the Father's correction with abandonment?

**Practice:** Answer accusation with the truth that the Father is merciful and you can return.

### Day 041 Reflection

**Focus:** Knowing God in the heart.

**Write:** What false power has felt too large in my heart lately?

**Practice:** Pause three times and confess that the Lord is God and you are His beloved child.

### Day 042 Reflection

**Focus:** Guarding the soul and passing on testimony.

**Write:** What testimony do I need to remember so fear does not become what I pass forward?

**Practice:** Share or write one specific way God has helped you.

### Day 043 Reflection

**Focus:** Gifts that become gratitude.

**Write:** What gift in my life needs to become gratitude instead of entitlement?

**Practice:** Name three gifts you did not create alone and thank God for each one.

### Day 044 Reflection

**Focus:** Doing what is right and good from love.

**Write:** What right and good step have I delayed because it felt costly or unseen?

**Practice:** Do one quiet right thing without announcing it.

### Day 045 Reflection

**Focus:** Out of bondage and into belonging.

**Write:** Where do I still live like I was only brought out, not also being brought in?

**Practice:** Complete this sentence: "God did not only bring me out; He is bringing me into..."

### Week 2 Review

1. Where did I seek God more honestly?
2. How did mercy answer accusation?
3. What false power became smaller as I remembered God?
4. What testimony or gift did I remember?
5. What right and good step helped beloved identity become visible?
