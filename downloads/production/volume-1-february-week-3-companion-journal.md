# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## February Week 3: Beloved Identity

Use these pages beside Days 046-052.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, teach me to listen before I move, carry Your words in my heart, bring fear honestly to You, set clean boundaries around allegiance, stop measuring myself by impressiveness, worship from rescue, and meet bitter places with mercy. Amen.

### Day 046 Reflection

**Focus:** Listening before movement.

**Write:** Where am I moving too quickly without first listening to the Father?

**Practice:** Pause before one responsibility and ask how God wants you to listen before you move.

### Day 047 Reflection

**Focus:** God's words upon the heart.

**Write:** What word has shaped my heart that the Father wants to replace with His truth?

**Practice:** Carry one short truth about God's love through the day.

### Day 048 Reflection

**Focus:** Fear brought into the Father's welcome.

**Write:** What feels too large for me right now, and how can I bring that fear honestly to the Father?

**Practice:** Name one large obstacle in prayer before you decide what to do.

### Day 049 Reflection

**Focus:** Clear allegiance without contempt toward people.

**Write:** What pattern or allegiance needs a clear boundary because I belong to the Father?

**Practice:** Ask God for one clean next step of surrender.

### Day 050 Reflection

**Focus:** Loved before impressiveness.

**Write:** Where have I been trying to become impressive enough to feel loved?

**Practice:** Answer comparison with the truth that the Father's love is not waiting for applause.

### Day 051 Reflection

**Focus:** Worship from rescue.

**Write:** Where am I trying to perform strength instead of receiving the Lord as my strength?

**Practice:** Turn one pressure point into praise.

### Day 052 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy in bitter places.

**Write:** What bitter place do I need to bring honestly to the Father instead of carrying alone?

**Practice:** Name the bitterness in prayer and ask what mercy looks like there.

### Week 3 Review

1. Where did listening change my movement?
2. What word from God steadied my heart?
3. What fear became easier to bring into the Father's care?
4. What boundary protected beloved identity?
5. Where did mercy meet bitterness?
