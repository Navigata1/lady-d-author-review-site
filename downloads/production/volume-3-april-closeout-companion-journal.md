# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## April Closeout: Conviction Without Condemnation

Use these pages with Days 119-120.

### Closeout Prayer

Holy Spirit, close this month by anchoring calling in God's name and turning practical skill into obedient service. Let conviction remain mercy, not condemnation, and prepare my heart for fruit that grows in surrender. Amen.

### Day 119 Reflection

What gift or responsibility needs to be named and surrendered back to God?

Practice: Name one gift or responsibility God has entrusted to you, and surrender it back to Him for Spirit-filled use.

### Day 120 Reflection

What practical skill or task needs to be offered to God today?

Practice: Before one practical task, ask the Spirit to purify your motive and make the work useful to God.

### April Review

- Where did conviction feel like mercy instead of condemnation?
- What shame pattern lost power when brought to the Comforter?
- What boundary protected life this month?
- What gift did I receive with open hands?
- What hidden place did God water quietly?
- What practical skill can become worshipful service in May?
- What fruit do I want to grow through surrender next month?
