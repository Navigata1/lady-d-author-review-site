# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## July Week 4: The Way, the Truth, and the Life

Use these pages beside Days 200-206.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, trusted Lord and true Light, let received mercy become practiced mercy in me. Expose hidden hardness, make grace accountable, heal resentment, protect the vulnerable, give me courage to surrender what harms, and let this Sabbath become a place where light makes room for resurrection hope. Amen.

### Day 200 Reflection

- Where have I received mercy but resisted becoming merciful?
- Practice: Before responding to someone who owes you, remember one mercy Jesus has shown you.

### Day 201 Reflection

- What hidden resentment may be affecting people around me?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to show whether one private resentment is leaking into your words, home, or work.

### Day 202 Reflection

- Where is Jesus making my received mercy accountable in real life?
- Practice: Name one relationship where Jesus is asking mercy to become more concrete.

### Day 203 Reflection

- Where might unforgiveness be costing me freedom?
- Practice: Ask Jesus for the first step toward release from resentment.

### Day 204 Reflection

- Where might my words, habits, or influence be making another person's path harder?
- Practice: Identify one word, habit, or pattern that could become a stumbling block, and surrender it to Jesus.

### Day 205 Reflection

- What damaging access point or pattern is Jesus asking me to surrender decisively?
- Practice: Remove one access point, excuse, or pattern that Jesus has shown is damaging your walk.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 206

- What is Jesus removing so life, peace, and resurrection hope can grow in me?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, sit quietly with Jesus and ask what He is removing so life can grow.

### Week Review

- Where did received mercy become practiced mercy?
- What hidden hardness did Jesus bring into the light?
- How did Jesus protect peace by naming what harms?
- What did Sabbath rest teach me about conviction becoming hope?
