# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## December Closeout: Daily Surrender as the Father Draws Near

Use these pages with Days 364-365.

### Closeout Prayer

Father, steady my heart with Your faithfulness and consecrate me with Your mercy. Help me close this year without shame, striving, or fear. Teach me to remember what You have revealed, receive what You are forming, and enter the next step as one who belongs to You. Amen.

### Day 364 Reflection

Where do I need to let God's faithfulness speak louder than my unfinished list?

Practice: Write down one way God made His faithfulness visible this year, then take one obedient step from gratitude instead of pressure.

### Day 365 Reflection

What part of my heart needs to be set apart for God's presence as this year closes?

Practice: Before the year ends, pray through one guarded area of your heart and ask the Father to set it apart for His presence.

### Volume 1 Year-End Review

- Where did I learn that God's love begins before my performance?
- Which entry or scripture reference most helped me receive beloved identity?
- Where did the Father meet me in struggle, timing, forgiveness, action, restoration, relationships, promise, calling, hope, or daily surrender?
- What Sabbath truth helped me remember grace before obedience?
- Where did I see the Father's patience with me?
- What one practice from this volume do I want to carry into the next season?

### Closing Surrender

Father, I place this year in Your hands. I release the need to prove, defend, control, or carry what belongs to You. Let Your love remain the foundation beneath my obedience. Let Your mercy form the morning ahead. Amen.
