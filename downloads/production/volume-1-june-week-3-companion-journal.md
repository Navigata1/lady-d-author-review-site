# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## June Week 3: Restored by the Father's Heart

Use these pages beside Days 165-171.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, restore my responsibility under blessing, teach me to receive provision, let mercy meet anxious need, give me patient eyes, help me lean into promise, restore wise compassion, and let Saturday Sabbath teach me to obey from love. Amen.

---

### Day 165 Reflection

**Focus:** Blessing before responsibility.

**Write:** Where do I need to receive the Father's blessing before I carry responsibility today?

**Practice:** Before beginning one responsibility, pause and receive the Father's blessing over your identity and assignment.

### Day 166 Reflection

**Focus:** Grace before performance.

**Write:** Where do I resist receiving because I feel I am unworthy of needing provision?

**Practice:** Receive one form of provision without rushing to earn it back: food, rest, help, quiet, or encouragement.

### Day 167 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy over anxious need.

**Write:** What anxious need do I need to place under the Father's wide and attentive care?

**Practice:** Name one anxious need, then pray over it as something seen by the Father rather than hidden from Him.

### Day 168 Reflection

**Focus:** Patient honesty instead of condemnation.

**Write:** Where have I been judging the whole story from one unfinished page?

**Practice:** Write one honest sentence about what still needs healing and one grateful sentence about what God has already made good.

### Day 169 Reflection

**Focus:** Promise stronger than fear.

**Write:** What promise am I trying to secure by control instead of trusting the Father to hold it?

**Practice:** Name one promise you need to trust again, and release one control habit that has been pretending to protect it.

### Day 170 Reflection

**Focus:** Restoring compassion that blesses wisely.

**Write:** Where has pain narrowed my sight, and how is the Father inviting me to bless wisely again?

**Practice:** Choose one person or family to bless through prayer, encouragement, repair, or a wise act of service.

### Day 171 Reflection

**Focus:** Saturday Sabbath and promise-led obedience.

**Write:** What promise is the Father asking me to rest in, and what next step is love teaching me to take?

**Practice:** Keep a Sabbath pause before making plans, then write the one obedient step you believe the Father is teaching you to take next.

---

### Week Review

1. How did receiving blessing change the way I carried responsibility?
2. What provision did I receive without trying to earn it back?
3. What anxious need did I place under the Father's care?
4. Where did patient honesty replace condemnation?
5. What promise did I trust instead of controlling?
6. How did restoring compassion help me bless wisely?
7. How did Saturday Sabbath teach me to rest in promise before taking the next step?
