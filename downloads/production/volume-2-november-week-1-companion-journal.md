# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## November Week 1: Resurrection Hope

Use these pages beside Days 305-311.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, living Way and Morning Star, lead me toward the river of life, teach me faithful choices under grace, answer shame with welcome, shine over dark places, show me the end of night, steady me with trustworthy words, and let Sabbath rest make hope worshipful and ready. Amen.

### Day 305 Reflection

- Where does my soul need to remember that God's future is life?
- Practice: Bring one dry place to Jesus and ask Him for one life-giving step.

### Day 306 Reflection

- Where do I need both accountability and grace to shape my next decision?
- Practice: Choose one faithful action today because it matters to Jesus, not because you are trying to earn His love.

### Day 307 Reflection

- What shame-name needs to be answered by Christ's welcome?
- Practice: Release one shame-name in prayer and receive Christ's invitation to remain near.

### Day 308 Reflection

- What darkness needs the witness of Jesus' identity today?
- Practice: Begin one dark-feeling task by naming who Jesus is before naming what you fear.

### Day 309 Reflection

- Where has darkness been acting like it owns the future?
- Practice: Refuse one despair-shaped decision and choose one action that agrees with God's future light.

### Day 310 Reflection

- What promise needs to carry weight in my life today?
- Practice: Choose one promise of God to carry into a specific fear or decision.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 311

- How can Sabbath worship make me ready in love rather than tense in fear?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, turn one hope about Christ's return into worship and one act of readiness.

### Week Review

- Where did the river of life refresh a dry place?
- What faithful choice was shaped by grace?
- What shame-name lost authority?
- How did the Morning Star give courage?
- How did Sabbath rest make hope worshipful and ready?
