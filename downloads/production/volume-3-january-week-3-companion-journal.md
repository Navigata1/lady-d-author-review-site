# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## January Week 3: The Promise of the Spirit

Use these pages with Days 015-021.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, bring rain to dry places, govern my impulses, make my gifts fruitful, give oil for merciful witness, form belonging into fruit, sustain hidden growth, and hover over what is still unformed in me. Amen.

### Day 015 Reflection

What useful offering have I been delaying that could bring life to a dry place?

Practice: Bring one practical offering to a dry place today: a prayer, apology, act of service, gift, or faithful follow-through.

### Day 016 Reflection

What fire am I tempted to kindle that God is asking me to leave unlit?

Practice: Identify one reaction or rush you will not kindle today, and choose a surrendered pause instead.

### Day 017 Sabbath Reflection

Where does my giftedness need more love, humility, or patience?

Practice: On this Sabbath, thank God for one ability He has entrusted to you, then ask how it can serve others without pride.

### Day 018 Reflection

Where can I bring light instead of heat today?

Practice: Carry light into one place today through a merciful word, a truthful encouragement, or a hidden act of service.

### Day 019 Reflection

What kind of fruit is my life quietly multiplying right now?

Practice: Name one fruit of the Spirit you want to see formed more deeply, and practice it in one specific relationship.

### Day 020 Reflection

Where am I tempted to quit because the fruit is not visible yet?

Practice: Continue one faithful practice that feels slow, and ask the Spirit for patience while fruit forms.

### Day 021 Reflection

What unformed place needs to be surrendered instead of forced today?

Practice: Name one unformed place in your life and surrender it to the Spirit before trying to force clarity.

### Week Review

- What practical offering brought rain to a dry place?
- Where did restraint become worship?
- What did Sabbath rest teach me about giftedness and love?
- Where did I bring light instead of heat?
- What fruit is the Spirit forming over time?
- What hidden growth do I need to keep watering?
- What unformed place did I entrust to the Spirit?
