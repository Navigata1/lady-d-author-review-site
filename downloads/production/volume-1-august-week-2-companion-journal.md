# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## August Week 2: Love in Relationships

Use these pages with Days 217-223.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to receive Your covenant love with reverence, identity, blessing, peace, correction, mercy, and patience. Let my relationships be shaped by Your promise instead of my fear. Amen.

### Day 217 Reflection

Where have I treated belonging as comfort but resisted the faithful response it asks of me?

Practice: Choose one relationship where words, boundaries, or follow-through need to agree more clearly with covenant faithfulness.

### Day 218 Reflection

What old name needs to lose authority because the Father is calling me by a truer one?

Practice: Write one old name you have lived under, then answer it with a truer identity rooted in God's call.

### Day 219 Reflection

Where is God asking me to receive blessing first so I can love without proving myself?

Practice: Bless one person with encouragement, prayer, provision, or patient presence before worry shapes the day.

### Day 220 Sabbath Reflection

Who am I afraid God will overlook, and what would it look like to entrust that person to Him with peace?

Practice: Pray honestly for one beloved person, then choose one peaceful action that releases control without withdrawing love.

### Day 221 Reflection

Where do I need to let God's clear answer lead me instead of asking Him to bless my familiar plan?

Practice: Name one place where God may be redirecting you, then respond with one act of trust instead of resistance.

### Day 222 Reflection

Who do I need to see through God's mercy instead of through my disappointed expectation?

Practice: Speak or write one blessing over a person whose path has been hard for you to understand.

### Day 223 Reflection

Where do I need to trust God's appointed timing instead of forcing a relational outcome?

Practice: Identify one anxious timing issue, then choose a patient act of obedience that does not force the outcome.

### Week Review

- Where did covenant love become more than comfort language?
- What truer name began to replace an old identity?
- How did blessing move through me to another person?
- What beloved person did I entrust to God with more peace?
- How did God's mercy help me bless someone whose path is different from my expectations?
- Where did I practice patience with God's appointed timing?
