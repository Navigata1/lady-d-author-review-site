# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## June Close / July Week 1: Peace in the Storm into The Way, the Truth, and the Life

Use these pages beside Days 179-185.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, prepare me with grace, redeem my hindsight, lead me from worship into mercy, receive the places where my effort was not enough, correct and heal what needs Your saving presence, speak wholeness over what has torn at life, and make this Sabbath a place of small, concrete faith under Your Lordship. Amen.

### Day 179 Reflection

- Where do I need to recognize preparation as mercy?
- Practice: Name one place where Jesus may be preparing you before you understand what comes next.

### Day 180 Reflection

- What do I understand now that I could not understand then?
- Practice: Look back at one confusing season and ask Jesus what He has taught you through it.

### Day 181 Reflection

- What need do I need to bring to Jesus without pretense?
- Practice: Bring one real need to Jesus plainly, without dressing it up to sound stronger than you feel.

### Day 182 Reflection

- Where do I need to bring inability back to Jesus?
- Practice: Name one place where your effort was not enough and bring it back to Jesus without shame.

### Day 183 Reflection

- Where do I need both correction and mercy from Jesus?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to correct one unbelieving pattern while still bringing Him the need you are carrying.

### Day 184 Reflection

- What truth about Jesus must get the first word over my worry?
- Practice: Before worrying through a hard need, name one truth about Jesus' authority over it.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 185

- What mountain needs one small act of obedience under Christ's Lordship?
- Practice: Name one mountain and one concrete act of Sabbath-rooted obedience.

### Week Review

- How did Jesus prepare me before I understood the road?
- What inability did I bring back to Him without shame?
- Where did His authority get the first word?
- How did Sabbath rest anchor small, concrete faith?
