# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## June Week 4: Peace in the Storm

Use these pages beside Days 172-178.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, lead me through transition, keep me abiding in revealed truth, purify my compassion, teach me surrender beyond self-preservation, steady me with kingdom hope, guard me from hidden influence, and make this Sabbath a place of listening beyond scarcity anxiety. Amen.

### Day 172 Reflection

- Where am I tempted to treat a transition as abandonment?
- Practice: Name one ending or transition and ask Jesus to lead your next faithful step.

### Day 173 Reflection

- What revealed truth about Jesus do I need to abide in today?
- Practice: Write one truth about Jesus that God has made clear to you, and choose one way to live from it.

### Day 174 Reflection

- Where might I be resisting costly obedience while calling it care?
- Practice: Ask Jesus to show you where fear may be disguising itself as protection.

### Day 175 Reflection

- Where am I trying to save a false life Jesus is asking me to surrender?
- Practice: Identify one place where self-preservation is leading you, and ask Jesus what surrendered faithfulness looks like there.

### Day 176 Reflection

- Where do I need a kingdom horizon for present faithfulness?
- Practice: Name one costly obedience and pair it with one truth about Christ's coming kingdom.

### Day 177 Reflection

- What hidden influence has been spreading through my thoughts or reactions?
- Practice: Identify one quiet influence and bring it under the warning and mercy of Jesus.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 178

- Where has scarcity anxiety caused me to misread what Jesus is saying?
- Practice: Name one scarcity anxiety and pause long enough to listen again for what Jesus is actually saying.

### Week Review

- What transition did Jesus lead me through?
- What truth became bread for the road?
- Where did compassion become more cross-shaped?
- How did Sabbath listening loosen scarcity anxiety?
