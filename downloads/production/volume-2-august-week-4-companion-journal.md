# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## August Week 4: The Surrendered Heart

Use these pages beside Days 228-234.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, ask me the question that draws out honest need. Open my eyes, teach me to trust Your work and reward, stir my heart to recognize You, move me from description to discipleship, cleanse what crowds prayer, and let this Sabbath become a healing place in Your presence. Amen.

### Day 228 Reflection

- What need is Jesus asking me to name plainly?
- Practice: Tell Jesus one need you have been minimizing or hiding.

### Day 229 Reflection

- What do I need to ask Jesus for without hiding behind complicated words?
- Practice: Pray one plain sentence to Jesus about the sight or healing you need.

### Day 230 Reflection

- Where is Jesus asking me to trust Him with both work and reward?
- Practice: Do one assigned task faithfully without measuring whether it is being noticed.

### Day 231 Reflection

- Where have I become familiar with Jesus without freshly recognizing Him?
- Practice: Pause before one decision and ask what would honor Jesus there.

### Day 232 Reflection

- Where do I need to move from knowing about Jesus to following Him?
- Practice: Write one sentence naming who Jesus is to you, then take one action that agrees with it.

### Day 233 Reflection

- What has been crowding the place in me that belongs to prayer?
- Practice: Remove one distraction, habit, or compromise that has been crowding prayer.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 234

- Where does Jesus want to turn a cleansed place into a healing place?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, bring one need to Jesus in worship and make room to pray for someone else's healing.

### Week Review

- What need did I name honestly?
- Where did Jesus open my eyes?
- How did I practice faithful work without bargaining?
- What did Jesus cleanse so prayer could breathe again?
- How did Sabbath worship make room for healing?
