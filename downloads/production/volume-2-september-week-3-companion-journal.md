# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## September Week 3: Serving with His Compassion

Use these pages beside Days 256-262.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, true Light and trusted Lord, teach me to serve with clean wisdom, humble questions, healing nearness, peaceful trust, Scripture-shaped correction, renewed invitation, and Sabbath resurrection hope. Amen.

### Day 256 Reflection

- Where do I need light before I answer?
- Practice: Pause before one pressured response and ask Jesus for the clean way through.

### Day 257 Reflection

- What assumption do I need to bring into the presence of Jesus?
- Practice: Bring one guarded assumption to Jesus and ask Him to teach you instead of defend you.

### Day 258 Reflection

- Where do I need Jesus' nearness before I try to solve anything?
- Practice: Name one family, grief, or responsibility burden to Jesus without dressing it up.

### Day 259 Reflection

- What future question am I trying to control before I serve?
- Practice: Release one future question to Jesus and practice one peaceful act of mercy now.

### Day 260 Reflection

- Where do I need Scripture and God's power to correct my assumptions?
- Practice: Revisit one decision through Scripture and prayer before you act on your first reaction.

### Day 261 Reflection

- Where am I invited but still refusing to come close?
- Practice: Make room for ten unhurried minutes with Jesus before you rush into service.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 262

- What earthly concern needs to be held inside resurrection hope?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, release one earthly anxiety to Jesus and serve from resurrection hope.

### Week Review

- Where did light keep me from reacting?
- What assumption became prayer?
- Where did Jesus meet family, grief, or responsibility with nearness?
- What future question did I release?
- How did Scripture and God's power correct me with grace?
- Where did Sabbath worship lift my eyes toward resurrection hope?
