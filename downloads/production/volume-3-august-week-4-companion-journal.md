# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## August Week 4: Oil for the Waiting Season

Use these pages with Days 234-240.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, give oil for the waiting season: Sabbath readiness purified by rest, wise boundaries, settled courage before oversized fear, peace that outlasts crisis, witness through ordinary tools, correction that calls the heart home, and honest prayer in hidden places. Keep every movement submitted to Jesus. Amen.

### Day 234 Reflection

Where is God calling me from waiting into faithful movement?

Practice: On this Sabbath, sit quietly before God, then name one faithful movement He is calling you toward.

### Day 235 Reflection

What boundary would help protect the deliverance God is forming in me?

Practice: Name one boundary that protects obedience, and take one respectful step to honor it.

### Day 236 Reflection

What has felt too strong for too long?

Practice: Write down one fear that feels oversized, then answer it with one truth about God's faithfulness.

### Day 237 Reflection

Where do I need to learn how to live after pressure eases?

Practice: Practice one act of peace after pressure: slow your pace, soften your tone, or receive rest without guilt.

### Day 238 Reflection

What ordinary tool or gift have I been dismissing?

Practice: Use one ordinary gift or tool to serve, encourage, protect, or speak truth today.

### Day 239 Reflection

Where is God warning me with mercy?

Practice: Name one drift pattern honestly, then take one return step before it deepens.

### Day 240 Reflection

Where has fear made me hide?

Practice: Bring one hidden fear to God plainly, then tell one trusted person if support is needed.

### Weekly Review

- What Sabbath rest purified my readiness?
- What boundary protected obedience?
- What oversized fear needed God's truth?
- Where did peace need to outlast crisis?
- What ordinary tool became witness?
- What warning felt like mercy?
- What hidden fear became honest prayer?
