# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## January Week 4: The Depth of God's Love

Use these pages beside Days 022-028.

### Weekly Opening Prayer

Father, teach me to rest secure, move forward in mercy, welcome others from beloved identity, bless small beginnings, and obey as a returned heart rather than a performing heart. Amen.

### Day 022 Reflection

**Focus:** Secure trust in the body and heart.

**Write:** Where has fear been living in my body, not only in my thoughts?

**Practice:** Breathe slowly and pray for secure trust before beginning a task.

### Day 023 Reflection

**Focus:** Ordinary faithfulness in a larger story.

**Write:** What small obedient action might matter more than I can see?

**Practice:** Do one ordinary act faithfully without needing visible results.

### Day 024 Reflection

**Focus:** Mercy that calls me forward.

**Write:** Where has God shown mercy, but I have still been afraid to move?

**Practice:** Take one next step of surrendered movement.

### Day 025 Reflection

**Focus:** A tender heart.

**Write:** What stubborn or guarded place needs the Father's loving correction?

**Practice:** Pray honestly over that place without defending it.

### Day 026 Reflection

**Focus:** Welcome shaped by beloved identity.

**Write:** Who near me may feel like a stranger or outsider?

**Practice:** Offer one concrete act of welcome with wisdom and kindness.

### Day 027 Reflection

**Focus:** Hope for small beginnings.

**Write:** What small beginning have I been tempted to dismiss?

**Practice:** Bless and nurture it in one practical way today.

### Day 028 Reflection

**Focus:** Obedience as return.

**Write:** What spiritual practice has become performance?

**Practice:** Begin that practice with a simple prayer of return.

### Week 4 Review

1. Where did I experience more secure trust?
2. What ordinary faithfulness did I practice?
3. What stubborn place became softer?
4. Who did I welcome?
5. How did obedience become return instead of performance?
