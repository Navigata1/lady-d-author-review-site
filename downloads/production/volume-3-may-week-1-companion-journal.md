# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## May Week 1: Fruit That Grows in Surrender

Use these pages with Days 121-127.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, grow fruit in surrendered work, shared belonging, prayerful space, hidden preparation, renewed service, honest identity, and responsive listening. Let my next decision show that Your truth has gone deeper than information. Amen.

### Day 121 Reflection

What is my ordinary work saying about the state of my surrender?

Practice: Offer one practical task to God before you begin, and do it with quiet integrity.

### Day 122 Reflection

Where do I need to receive my part without competing for someone else's?

Practice: On this Sabbath, bless one person's gift or contribution without comparing it to your own.

### Day 123 Reflection

What part of my day needs to become a place of meeting with God?

Practice: Set apart one small space or time today for quiet prayer without multitasking.

### Day 124 Reflection

What hidden detail is God asking me to honor today?

Practice: Complete one hidden detail faithfully without needing immediate recognition.

### Day 125 Reflection

Where has service become dry duty instead of surrendered worship?

Practice: Before serving, ask God to renew your motive and show one way to serve without resentment.

### Day 126 Reflection

Where am I trying to be seen instead of surrendered?

Practice: Notice one place you are performing an image, and choose one honest, humble action instead.

### Day 127 Reflection

Where am I moving on yesterday's instruction without listening today?

Practice: Pause before your next decision and ask, "Lord, what are You saying now?"

### Weekly Review

- What ordinary work became witness through surrender?
- Where did I bless someone else's gift without comparison?
- What space became a place of meeting with God?
- What hidden detail did I honor?
- Where did God renew dry service?
- What image did I release?
- What changed because I listened before deciding?
