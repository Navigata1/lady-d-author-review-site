# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## November Week 3: Resurrection Hope

Use these pages beside Days 319-325.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, worthy Lamb and faithful Sender, let redemption sing louder than my wounds, teach me to travel lighter, free me from bitterness when obedience is rejected, help me trust Your justice, make me wise and tender, prepare me before pressure arrives, and let Sabbath rest return my witness to belonging. Amen.

### Day 319 Reflection

- What old sentence needs to be answered by the Lamb's redeeming love?
- Practice: Name one old sentence your pain repeats, then answer it with the truth that Jesus has redeemed you.

### Day 320 Reflection

- What am I carrying because I am afraid Jesus will not provide?
- Practice: Release one extra burden you have been carrying as if Jesus cannot provide.

### Day 321 Reflection

- Where do I need to release rejection without releasing my calling?
- Practice: Surrender one rejected effort to Jesus and ask Him what faithful next step remains yours.

### Day 322 Reflection

- What unresolved wrong do I need to entrust to Jesus instead of rehearsing revenge?
- Practice: Place one unresolved injustice before Jesus and refuse one revenge-shaped thought or action.

### Day 323 Reflection

- Where do I need both wisdom and innocence today?
- Practice: Ask Jesus for one wise boundary and one tender response in a difficult relationship or assignment.

### Day 324 Reflection

- What pressure-filled room do I need to enter with preparation instead of panic?
- Practice: Prepare one faithful word or boundary for a pressure-filled setting, then entrust the outcome to Jesus.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 325

- Where might Jesus be turning pressure into witness, and how can Sabbath rest keep my heart humble?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, ask Jesus to turn one pressure point into quiet witness without letting it become performance.

### Week Review

- Where did redemption sing louder than an old wound?
- What burden did I lay down in trust?
- What rejection did I release without becoming bitter?
- How did Jesus teach me wisdom without coldness?
- How did Sabbath rest return my witness to belonging?
