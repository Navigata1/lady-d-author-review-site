# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## January Close: The Invitation to Follow into Cross-Shaped Faithfulness

Use these pages with Days 029-031.

### Closing Prayer

Jesus, close this first month by teaching me wise courage, truthful peace, and Sabbath-rooted cross-shaped faithfulness. Let me follow You without panic, pride, avoidance, or self-protection. Amen.

### Day 029 Reflection

Where do I need Jesus to guide my courage with wisdom instead of panic or pride?

Practice: Name one pressure point, then ask Jesus whether faithfulness requires staying steady, setting a boundary, or moving wisely.

### Day 030 Reflection

Where has avoidance been pretending to be peace in my life?

Practice: Identify one place where avoidance has been pretending to be peace, then pray for a gentle, truthful next step.

### Day 031 Sabbath Reflection

What self-protective pattern is Jesus inviting me to surrender as I follow Him?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one self-protective pattern Jesus is asking you to surrender, then receive His grace for one faithful step.

### January Review

- Where did Jesus invite me to follow from surrender rather than control?
- How did He teach truthful peace?
- What did the Sabbath entries help me receive?
- Where did He bring mercy, restoration, and nearness?
- What false peace or self-protection did He expose?
- What one sentence describes how January changed my walk with Jesus?
