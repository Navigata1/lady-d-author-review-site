# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## January Week 4: The Promise of the Spirit

Use these pages with Days 022-028.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, root my fruitfulness in God's blessing, teach me faithful stewardship, open my eyes to provision, help me receive life as gift, expose counterfeit fire, govern my strength, and make every rescue lead me into deeper surrender. Amen.

### Day 022 Reflection

Where am I trying to multiply effort without first receiving God's life and blessing?

Practice: Pause before one effort to multiply results, and ask the Spirit to root it in God's blessing rather than anxiety.

### Day 023 Reflection

What entrusted area needs Spirit-formed stewardship instead of neglect or control?

Practice: Choose one entrusted area and practice stewardship there through order, care, mercy, or follow-through.

### Day 024 Sabbath Reflection

What provision have I been treating as ordinary instead of receiving as grace?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name three provisions you did not earn and receive them with gratitude before asking for more.

### Day 025 Reflection

Where have I treated my life as something to prove instead of something received from God?

Practice: Take one practical step that honors your life as a gift: rest, hydrate, walk, ask for help, or stop one punishing thought.

### Day 026 Reflection

Where has pressure been shaping my choices more than the Spirit's truth?

Practice: Identify one place where pressure is driving you, and choose one truthful, prayerful response instead of manipulation.

### Day 027 Reflection

Where does my strength need surrender before it becomes harmful?

Practice: Name one strong impulse and surrender it to the Spirit before acting on it.

### Day 028 Reflection

Where has God helped me, and what hidden area still needs His formation?

Practice: Thank God for one place He has helped you, then ask what hidden area still needs formation.

### Week Review

- Where did I move from pressure into blessing?
- What stewardship became more faithful?
- What did Sabbath gratitude uncover?
- How did I honor life as gift?
- Where did I reject manipulation or fear?
- What strength did I surrender?
- What hidden area still needs the Spirit's formation?
