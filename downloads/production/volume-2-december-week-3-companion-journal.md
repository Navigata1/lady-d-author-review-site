# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## December Week 3: Walking as His Witness

Use these pages beside Days 349-355.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, beloved Servant and trusted Lord, teach me hidden obedience, align my day with Your revealed purpose, let me serve from beloved identity, make my witness strong and peaceable, restore bruised places with Sabbath grace, widen hope in Your name, and cleanse fearful interpretations. Amen.

### Day 349 Reflection

- Where am I confusing faithful witness with the need to be noticed?
- Practice: Offer one hidden act of obedience to Jesus without trying to make it visible.

### Day 350 Reflection

- What ordinary or understated place needs to be interpreted through God's revealed purpose?
- Practice: Read one promise or truth from Scripture before interpreting a difficult moment by fear.

### Day 351 Reflection

- Where do I need to serve from beloved identity instead of proving myself?
- Practice: Before serving today, pause and receive beloved identity before taking action.

### Day 352 Reflection

- Where do I mistake loudness for faithfulness?
- Practice: In one conversation, practice truthful calm instead of quarrel, sarcasm, or volume.

### Sabbath Reflection - Day 353

- What bruised place needs Christlike gentleness this Sabbath?
- Practice: On this Sabbath, treat one bruised place with Christlike gentleness instead of pressure or contempt.

### Day 354 Reflection

- Who have I quietly excluded from the reach of hope in Jesus' name?
- Practice: Pray for one person or group you have not naturally included in your hope.

### Day 355 Reflection

- Where might fear be causing me to misname something God is doing?
- Practice: Revisit one situation you have judged harshly and ask Jesus whether fear has shaped your interpretation.

### Week Review

- What hidden obedience belonged to Jesus this week?
- How did beloved identity change my service?
- Where did truthful calm become witness?
- How did Sabbath rest teach Christlike gentleness?
- Where did Jesus cleanse a suspicious interpretation?
