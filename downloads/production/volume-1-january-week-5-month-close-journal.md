# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## January Week 5 / Month Close: The Depth of God's Love

Use these pages beside Days 029-031 and the January review.

### Month-Close Opening Prayer

Father, thank You for bringing Your word near, teaching me Your ways through love, and warning my heart before distance grows. Let January's lessons become a surrendered life, not only devotional pages. Amen.

### Day 029 Reflection

**Focus:** The word made near to heart and mouth.

**Write:** What has God already made near to my heart that I keep treating as far away?

**Practice:** Speak one sentence of surrender aloud before beginning your work.

### Day 030 Reflection

**Focus:** Obedience seen again through the Father's patience.

**Write:** Where do I need to see obedience through love instead of pressure?

**Practice:** Choose one command, Sabbath rhythm, or spiritual practice and receive it as formation rather than performance.

### Day 031 Reflection

**Focus:** Love that warns before the heart drifts.

**Write:** Where is the Father lovingly warning me before distance grows?

**Practice:** Answer one conviction quickly and honestly.

### January Review

1. What did I learn about the depth of God's love?
2. Where did I experience belonging before performance?
3. What fear did the Father gently expose?
4. What spiritual practice became more grace-shaped?
5. What next step of surrender should carry into February?
