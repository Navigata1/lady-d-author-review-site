# Walking with Jesus - Companion Journal

## January Week 3: The Invitation to Follow into Surrendered Hope

Use these pages with Days 015-021.

### Week Prayer

Jesus, lead me down from holy moments into faithful obedience. Free me from attachment, renew my hope, speak truthful mercy, give courage to keep praying, make small obedience sacred, and anchor rejection under resurrection hope. Amen.

### Day 015 Reflection

Where do I need to follow Jesus in the descent, not only in the moment of glory?

Practice: Identify one sacred insight God has given you, then ask how to carry it humbly instead of rushing to display it.

### Day 016 Reflection

What attachment is Jesus gently exposing so I can walk with Him more freely?

Practice: Choose one small act of release or generosity that says Jesus is your treasure, not the thing you were holding.

### Day 017 Sabbath Reflection

What cost of following Jesus do I need to place under His promise of renewal?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one cost of following Jesus and place it under His promise of renewal.

### Day 018 Reflection

What truth from Jesus have I been avoiding because the road feels costly?

Practice: Name one truth Jesus has been making clear, then ask for courage to receive it without running from the road.

### Day 019 Reflection

What voice has been trying to silence my prayer for mercy?

Practice: Pray one honest mercy prayer out loud, especially in a place where discouragement has made you quiet.

### Day 020 Reflection

What small assignment might be part of Jesus' larger purpose today?

Practice: Identify one small assignment in front of you and complete it as obedience to Jesus, not as a meaningless task.

### Day 021 Reflection

Where do I need resurrection hope to answer the voice of rejection?

Practice: Name one rejected or discouraged place, then pray for Jesus to anchor it under resurrection hope instead of human verdict.

### Week Review

- How did I carry a holy insight with humility?
- What attachment did Jesus expose?
- What did Sabbath renewal help me entrust to Him?
- Where did truthful mercy steady me?
- What prayer became honest again?
- What small assignment became sacred obedience?
- Where did resurrection hope answer rejection?
