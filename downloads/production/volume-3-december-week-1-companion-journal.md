# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## December Week 1: Living Sealed and Filled

Use these pages with Days 335-341.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, teach me to live sealed and filled in provision, dependence, welcome, peace, Sabbath release, rejection, and accountability. Free me from provision anxiety, breathe trust into ordinary needs, give discernment for where witness can dwell, make me a carrier of peace, grow fruit without force, fill me for clean release, and make accountability humble. Amen.

### Day 335 Reflection

Where is fear making me overpack my heart?

Practice: Release one extra layer of control you have been carrying out of fear.

### Day 336 Reflection

Where does worry speak before dependence?

Practice: Before making a provision decision, pause and pray one sentence of dependence.

### Day 337 Reflection

Where am I trying to force welcome that has not been given?

Practice: Stop forcing one door and ask God where faithfulness actually has room to stay.

### Day 338 Reflection

How do I enter the rooms God places before me?

Practice: Before entering one room or conversation, ask God to make you a carrier of peace.

### Day 339 Reflection

Where am I trying to force peace instead of bearing it?

Practice: On this Sabbath, offer one peaceful word or prayer and release the response to God.

### Day 340 Reflection

Where has rejection kept me emotionally stuck?

Practice: Release one rejected effort to God without rehearsing resentment.

### Day 341 Reflection

Where does accountability need to soften and sober me?

Practice: Let one sober truth change a concrete choice before noon.

### Weekly Review

- What fear-driven control was released?
- Where did dependence speak before worry?
- What forced door was surrendered?
- How did you carry peace into an ordinary room?
- How did Sabbath rest help you offer peace without forcing it?
- What rejection was released cleanly?
- What accountability became humble fruit?
