# Filled with the Holy Spirit - Companion Journal

## April Week 4: Conviction Without Condemnation

Use these pages with Days 112-118.

### Week Prayer

Holy Spirit, settle my wordless prayers, breathe trust into hidden waiting, help me receive mercy, free my relationships from control, consecrate my commitments, heal shame, and teach me to listen before I act. Keep conviction close to Jesus and free from condemnation. Amen.

### Day 112 Reflection

Where do faithful work and unmet longing meet in my life?

Practice: Name one area where you have been faithful but still feel an ache, and bring it to God without forcing a solution.

### Day 113 Reflection

What am I trying to manage that belongs to God's hidden work?

Practice: Release one outcome you have been trying to manage, and choose one faithful action that does not force God's timing.

### Day 114 Reflection

What help might God be forming and bringing that I need to receive?

Practice: Identify one help God may be bringing, and receive it with gratitude instead of suspicion.

### Day 115 Reflection

Where does fear turn recognition into control?

Practice: On this Sabbath, receive one person or gift with gratitude, then release the need to control it.

### Day 116 Reflection

What commitment needs the Breath of God today?

Practice: Choose one commitment and ask God for one action that makes it more faithful, humble, and life-giving.

### Day 117 Reflection

What hiding strategy is the Comforter gently asking me to surrender?

Practice: Bring one hidden shame to God in prayer, and consider sharing it with a trusted, spiritually safe person.

### Day 118 Reflection

Where am I acting before I have listened?

Practice: Before one task, pause and ask, "Lord, what are You saying before I act?"

### Weekly Review

- What ache did I bring to God without forcing a solution?
- Where did I release control over God's hidden work?
- What help did I receive with gratitude?
- How did Sabbath rest free recognition from control?
- What commitment received fresh breath?
- Where did I listen before acting?
