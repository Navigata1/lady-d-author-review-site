# Surrendering to God's Love - Companion Journal

## December Week 4: Daily Surrender in the Wilderness of Trust

Use these pages with Days 350-356.

### Week Prayer

Father, teach me to surrender daily as Your beloved child in the wilderness of trust. Let deliverance form my identity, worship answer fear, grace speak before performance, Sabbath mercy quiet alarm, patient faithfulness transform complaint, love heal bitterness, and restoring compassion lead me into obedience that receives Your care. Amen.

### Day 350 Reflection

What old label has tried to follow me after God already made a way?

Practice: Name one old fear or label that has followed you, then answer it with one truth about how God has carried you.

### Day 351 Reflection

Where do I need worship to interrupt the fear that keeps replaying what I survived?

Practice: Choose one simple act of embodied worship before worry gets to set the tone of your day.

### Day 352 Reflection

Where have I been trying to perform before receiving the mercy God has already shown?

Practice: Before doing one demanding task, pause and thank God for one mercy that came before your effort.

### Day 353 Sabbath Reflection

What need have I been carrying as alarm instead of bringing it honestly into God's presence?

Practice: On this Sabbath, name one honest need without letting fear drive it, and let worship remind you that the Redeemer is still near.

### Day 354 Reflection

Where is honest need beginning to sound like complaint in my heart?

Practice: Turn one complaint into one honest prayer before you discuss the problem with anyone else.

### Day 355 Reflection

What bitter place needs to be brought to God before I build a story around it?

Practice: Bring one bitter place to the Father directly, then ask what obedient step He is showing you next.

### Day 356 Reflection

What healing-shaped act of obedience is the Father inviting me to take today?

Practice: Ask the Father for one healing-shaped act of obedience, then take that step before worry argues with it.

### Week Review

- What old label lost authority as beloved identity became clearer?
- How did worship interrupt fear?
- Where did mercy speak before performance?
- What honest need did Sabbath rest help me bring without fear taking the lead?
- Which complaint became prayer?
- What bitterness did love begin to heal?
- What healing-shaped obedience did restoring compassion invite?
