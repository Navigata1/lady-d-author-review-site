# Walking with Jesus — Scripture Architecture

**Subtitle:** A 365-Day Devotional Journey with the Son
**Focus:** Jesus, discipleship, abiding, healing, obedience, compassion, the cross, resurrection hope, and walking near Him

## Editorial Standard
- Scripture references only here; final translation remains Lady D + permissions review.
- No scripture reference may appear more than 3 times in the volume.
- Day titles do not repeat within the same month.
- Original-language/context lenses are matched by testament and theological fit, not used as decoration.
- Each day is designed to become a full, mature-eye-readable print page with depth, prayer, and journal prompt.

## Title Options
- **Walking with Jesus** — 8.9/10: Evergreen and instantly clear; needs distinctive subtitle/design because the phrase is familiar.
- **Following the One Who Calls** — 8.4/10: Active discipleship feel with strong scriptural energy.
- **Nearer to Jesus Every Day** — 8.1/10: Warm and accessible; slightly softer.
- **The Road with Jesus** — 7.8/10: Visual and cover-friendly; less explicit as a 365 devotional.
- **Abiding with the Son** — 7.4/10: Theologically rich but a little formal for Lady D’s everyday warmth.

## Monthly Structure
- **January:** The Invitation to Follow
- **February:** Abiding Close to Jesus
- **March:** The Way in Ordinary Days
- **April:** Healing in His Presence
- **May:** Learning His Voice
- **June:** Peace in the Storm
- **July:** The Way, the Truth, and the Life
- **August:** The Surrendered Heart
- **September:** Serving with His Compassion
- **October:** The Cross and Daily Grace
- **November:** Resurrection Hope
- **December:** Walking as His Witness
