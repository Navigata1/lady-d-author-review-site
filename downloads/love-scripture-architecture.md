# Surrendering to God’s Love — Scripture Architecture

**Subtitle:** A 365-Day Devotional Journey into the Father’s Heart
**Focus:** God the Father, His love, surrender, identity, restoration, forgiveness, calling, and daily trust

## Editorial Standard
- Scripture references only here; final translation remains Lady D + permissions review.
- No scripture reference may appear more than 3 times in the volume.
- Day titles do not repeat within the same month.
- Original-language/context lenses are matched by testament and theological fit, not used as decoration.
- Each day is designed to become a full, mature-eye-readable print page with depth, prayer, and journal prompt.

## Title Options
- **Surrendering to God’s Love** — 9.4/10: Author-born in the meeting; active, reverent, intimate, and broad enough for all twelve months.
- **Held by the Father’s Heart** — 8.7/10: Emotionally warm and pastoral; less action-oriented but very beautiful.
- **The Love That Carries Me** — 8.5/10: Strong testimony resonance and accessible reader promise.
- **Only God’s Love Matters** — 7.9/10: Memorable and punchy; slightly less graceful as a book title.
- **Living Inside God’s Love** — 7.6/10: Clear and devotional; less distinctive than the author-born title.
